## Overview

The **Convergence War** was the pivotal galactic conflict that reshaped Galia Expanse, leading to the current **Golden Era**. It was fought primarily over the control of **Iris** and the exploitation of the **Tufa**.

---

## The Belligerents

### The Aggressors (The Factions)

- **Motivation**: Control of **Iris** (sentient planet in HRZ) and its unlimited energy.
- **Action**: Discovered entry to the Cataclysm; harvested **Genesium** by killing **Tufa** ("Children of the Cataclysm").
- **Participants**: Virtually **all major species/factions** participated, but with distinct motivations (The Three Paths).

### The Three Paths to War (Entry Points)

1. **MUD (The Promised Paradise)**: Saw the Cataclysm as the fulfillment of the **Gotti Revelation** (Holy Land). Driven by internal power struggles (Church vs Pearce vs Synod).
2. **Sogmian (The Sovereign Vow)**: **Empress Paizul** entered to "verify" the anomaly. Discovered psychic influence and Tufa sentience. Switched to defense.
3. **Ustur (The Inhabitation)**: **Timpo** (heretic) found entry. **Elder Ombra** convinced the Council to "inhabit" the Cataclysm for evolution, not conquer it.

### The Defenders (Sogmians)

- **Motivation**: The **Du Prah (Sogmian Vows)** — *"Shelter those who cannot defend themselves."*
- **Discovery**: Sogmians recognized that Tufa were **sentient beings**, not cargo.
- **Action**: Intercepted slave auctions, shielded Tufa worlds, engaged in open war against the galaxy.

---

## Key Phases (Sogmian Timeline)

1. **The Shield**: Sogmians intercept "treaty" ships (slave auctions), protecting Tufa.
2. **Retaliation**: Galactic powers besiege Sogmian systems.
3. **The Shattering**: **Sov Arei Paizul** (Sogmian leader) assassinated.
4. **The Exile**: **Bekalu** (Paizul's husband/successor) leads Sogmians to HRZ exile. **The Last Stand** (Titan ship) is forged.
5. **ONI Formation**: In exile, Sogmians ally with **Punaab** (gold), **Mierese** (prowess), and **Photoli** (wisdom).
6. **The Reckoning**: ONI armada returns. Massive campaigns (e.g., erasure of MUD forces at Daestina).
7. **The Akalma Incident**: Sogmians attack the banished House Akalma, nearly repeating the sins of their enemies (unchecked emotion).
8. **MRZ Collapse (~2522)**: The factions retreat from HRZ and MRZ. Chaos breaks out across the Medium Risk Zone — the power vacuum left by retreating armies plunges the MRZ into lawlessness, refugee crises, and civil unrest.
9. **The Accord (~2523)**: Bekalu chooses restraint. **Treaty of Peace** signed. The Treaty's formal "independence" for MRZ territories only escalated the chaos.

---

## Aftermath & Consequences

### Political

- **Council of Peace (COP)** established to govern Galia.
- **ONI Consortium** formalized as a major faction.
- **ECOS** rises from the power vacuum left by the war's destruction.

### Economic & Infrastructure

- **Star Atlas** (Sentient Blockchain) created/emerges to manage economy.
- **Starpath warp-gates** built to reconnect the galaxy.
- **Golden Era** begins.

### The Cataclysm

- **Iris** (Planet) fractured mentally by the genocide of her children (Tufa) → Paranoid/Hostile.
- **Access Sealed**: Entry to HRZ/Cataclysm made **illegal** by COP. Tech to enter is guarded.
