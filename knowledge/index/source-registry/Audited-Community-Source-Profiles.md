# Audited Community Source Profiles

This file promotes high-confidence source-level findings from audited ingestion campaigns. It does not promote article claims into the historical timeline, product, governance, lore, or economic knowledge bases.

## Aephia Industries publications

**Entity ID:** `MEDIA-AEPHIA`  
**Institutional relationship:** `GUILD-AEPHIA`  
**Source role:** Guild-affiliated community publication and research resource  
**Audited corpus:** 64 written publications  
**Audit result:** Approved for archival use

Aephia publishes numbered weekly newsletters, Town Hall reports, guides, analysis, and institutional material. It is especially useful for reconstructing what was being discussed or expected at a given time.

**Strengths**

- Regular publication cadence and contemporaneous coverage.
- Broad coverage of product development, governance, economy, and community activity.
- Frequent references to official announcements, broadcasts, and community sources.

**Limitations**

- Guild affiliation should remain visible when evaluating conflicts or competitive subjects.
- Roadmaps, operational guides, recipes, schedules, and economic mechanics may become outdated.
- Aephia reporting is evidence of contemporary understanding, not automatic proof of implementation.

**Lineage rule:** Record linked or attributed primary sources separately whenever present.

## Intergalactic Herald

**Entity ID:** `MEDIA-INTERGALACTIC-HERALD`  
**Source role:** Community publication, curator, directory, and event-coverage outlet  
**Audited corpus:** Campaign Bravo with source-lineage enhancement  
**Audit result:** Approved for archival use

Intergalactic Herald preserves a large volume of Star Atlas community material. Its archive may include original reporting, interviews, editorials, directories, curated summaries, and repackaged external work.

**Strengths**

- Broad discovery value across creators, events, interviews, and publications.
- Useful secondary chronology and directory function.
- Preserves references to sources that may later become difficult to locate.

**Limitations**

- Do not assume all material originated with the Herald.
- Original creators and publications must be preserved through source-lineage records.
- Curated summaries should not replace the linked primary source when that source survives.

**Mandatory lineage fields:** publication role, relationship, primary sources, original creators, and lineage confidence.

## Source-lineage standard

All future community-source records should capture:

- publication role;
- relationship to the original source;
- primary sources;
- original creators;
- lineage confidence.

Unknown lineage must be recorded as unknown rather than inferred.
