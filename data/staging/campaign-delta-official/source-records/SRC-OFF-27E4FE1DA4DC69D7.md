## Classified Claims

- If you’ve enlisted your Claim Stakes in Faction Claims, you can periodically claim your earned R4 resources (Fuel, Food, Ammunition, and Toolkits) directly on the platform.
- 💡 Make sure you have a small amount of SOL in your wallet to cover transaction fees before claiming.
- Go to play.staratlas.com and connect your wallet.
- Click on the “Faction Claims” tab in the navigation menu.
- In the “Faction Claims” section, find your enlisted Claim Stakes.
- Click “Claim All” under each resource type to collect available rewards.
- Once the transaction is confirmed, the rewards will be sent directly to your wallet.

If you’ve enlisted your Claim Stakes in Faction Claims, you can periodically claim your earned R4 resources (Fuel, Food, Ammunition, and Toolkits) directly on the platform.

In the “Faction Claims” section, find your enlisted Claim Stakes.

Approve the transaction in your wallet.
