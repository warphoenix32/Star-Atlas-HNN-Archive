## Preserved Official Text

Holosim is the free-to-play browser game set in the Star Atlas universe. You don’t need to install anything or connect a wallet to get started, just open your browser and play.
Steps to start playing:
Go to the official Holosim page: holosim.staratlas.com
Watch or skip the intro, you can enjoy the cinematic introduction or go straight into the game.
Launch the game: click “Launch Game” to begin
Create your character: choose a name (cannot be changed later) and pick your faction. Your faction sets your starting region and allies.
Optional: Battle Pass: you’ll be asked if you want to purchase a Battle Pass for additional rewards, but this is not required to play.
Follow the tutorial quests: a glowing “Quests” icon will guide you through your first steps and explain the basics of fleets, resources, and missions.
That’s it! From there, you can explore the map, manage fleets, and progress through quests at your own pace
