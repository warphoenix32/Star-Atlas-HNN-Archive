## Preserved Official Text

To get started with using crew members for ship operations in SAGE, the first step is to make sure your crew is imported into SAGE. If you haven't done this yet, please follow this guide on importing crew members into SAGE
How Crew Members Work in SAGE for Ship Operations
In the current version of SAGE, all crew members are considered equivalent in terms of their utility. When you create a fleet in SAGE, the system will automatically assign the required number of crew members from your starbase to the ships in your fleet. For example, if you create a fleet with an X4 ship, which requires one crew member, the system will randomly select one crew member from the starbase and assign them to the ship.
For ships like the Lowbie, which requires only one crew member for basic operation but has space for an additional passenger, the system will automatically assign one crew member for operation. However, you have the option to manually add one more crew member to the ship for transport purposes. For more details on manually adding crew to your fleet, please refer to this article
Preparing Your Fleet for Operations
Once your crew members are assigned to the ships, your fleet will need to be refueled before it can start any operations, such as mining or scanning. Make sure to follow the guide on refueling your fleet to ensure your fleet is ready for action.
Additional Tips:
Automatic Assignment: Remember that the SAGE system currently assigns crew members randomly, so there is no need to worry about specific roles or aptitudes.
Manual Addition for Transport: Use the manual addition option to transport extra crew members between starbases, especially if you have extra space available on your ships.
Fleet Management: Regularly check your fleet's fuel levels and crew assignments to maintain optimal operation.
