## Preserved Official Text

Aptitude and personality traits are key features of crew members in Star Atlas, designed to provide unique advantages and customization options. However, you might be wondering whether these attributes have any utility in the current version of SAGE
Short Answer: Not Yet
As of now, aptitude and personality traits do not have any functional impact in SAGE. These attributes are planned for future releases, where they will play a significant role in enhancing gameplay and providing strategic advantages. For now, all crew members are treated equally regardless of their aptitudes or personality traits.
Stay tuned for announcements on when these attributes will become active, and in the meantime, continue to explore other ways to optimize your crew’s performance in SAGE!
