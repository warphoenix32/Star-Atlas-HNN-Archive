## Overview

> *"What truly drives the ruler of the Zenith Door?"*
> *The answer is known: genuine peace. But to the rest of Galia, whispers persist.*

The Zenith Door stands as a **pivotal nexus** within the frontiers of ONI and Ustur territory — the **closest ONI territory to the borders of the HRZ**. Home to the galaxy's foremost academic institution and the COP's elite border fleet, it is ruled by one of the canon's most important characters: **Chior.eldr**, an Ustur Elder who dreams of a galaxy without factions or wars.

Zenith Door is not merely a sector — it is **the origin point of four interlocking storylines** that define Galia's modern era:

1. **Chior's peacekeeping crusade** — a centuries-long diplomatic project
2. **The Iris Academy** — Galia's greatest research institution, architect of Project Starpath
3. **The Herdus Tragedy** — a co-founder's descent into madness, cult prophecy, and criminal empire
4. **The COP Crisis** — the irony of a peace council waging war, and the Elder trying to stop it

### Strategic Importance

- **Nexus** between ONI and Ustur territory
- **Closest ONI sector to the HRZ borders**
- Natural chokepoint for any HRZ expansion or exploration
- Home to both Galia's **premier academic institution** and the COP's **elite border defense fleet**

---

## Founding (~2539)

- **~2539**: **Chior.eldr** and his disciple **Herdus.soul** discover the **wrecked sector** of Zenith Door
- Together they mobilize the local population and, using **all of their personal resources**, manage to **stabilize the sector**
- This act of selfless reconstruction establishes Chior's reputation and lays the foundation for Zenith Door's future as a diplomatic capital
- **~2545**: Chior relocates the **Iris Academy** (conceptually founded ~2525) to **Carvum**, an abandoned mining planet in Zenith Door, establishing it as the institution's permanent headquarters

> [!NOTE]
> The Iris Academy was **conceptually founded ~2525** by Chior.eldr and Professor Exfeheros during the post-war rebuilding period. It was **physically relocated to Carvum in Zenith Door ~2545**, after Chior had stabilized the sector (~2539). Both dates are canon — they describe different milestones.

## Chior.eldr — Character Profile

### The Figure (Major Canon Character)

- **Species**: Ustur — rank `.eldr` (Elder), highest tier of Ustur hierarchy
- **Born**: ~2391
- **Trained by**: **Armi.eldr** — the faction leader who guided the Ustur through the Convergence War
- **Twin pupil alongside**: **Ponel.eldr** — who would later become his philosophical opposite
- As an Elder: **virtually immortal** (fused with core), can be revived via the Pool of Reincarnation
- Full **artifact-level precision** in the Ustur Elder Conscience
- An **exotic Elder** who deviates from other Ustur Elders' agendas
- **One of the principal characters** of the entire Star Atlas lore

### Chior's Vision

- Seeks **balance and peaceful coexistence** in Galia
- Dreams of a **galaxy without factions or wars**
- Uses power, wealth, and influence always toward this objective
- A **genuine neutral agent** — not aligned with any faction's expansionist goals
- Possesses **unparalleled diplomatic finesse** — brokered agreements with almost every significant Galia faction
- Maintains Zenith Door as an **oasis for diplomacy**

### The Chior-Ponel Divergence

Chior and Ponel were twin pupils of Armi.eldr — the leader who guided the Ustur through the Convergence War. After the war, they diverged completely:

- **Chior** → stayed in the Elder Order, became Galia's foremost **pacifist diplomat** (Zenith Door)
- **Ponel** → broke away to found the **Anfoil State** (MRZ-15) — a meritocratic technate that suppresses emotion

Their opposing philosophies — Chior's empathy-driven diplomacy vs Ponel's cold efficiency — represent the **fundamental Ustur schism**. Ponel even attempted to bribe or destroy the Iris Academy, viewing it as a rival to Anfoil's own intellectual project.

## The Herdus Tragedy

> *The ghost of Herdus haunts Zenith Door's success.*

Chior's greatest failure and Zenith Door's darkest chapter: his own disciple's descent into madness.

**Herdus.soul** co-founded Zenith Door alongside Chior in ~2539 and likely helped establish the early Iris Academy. But by ~2559, he had left his master and begun wandering the MRZ alone — his reasons unclear: disillusionment? Restlessness? The first whispers of something darker?

### The Fall

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| ~2539 | Co-founded Zenith Door with Chior. A beacon of learning and peace |
| ~2545 | Likely involved in establishing the Iris Academy at Carvum |
| ~2559 | **Leaves Chior.eldr** — begins wandering the MRZ alone |
| ~2569 | After ascending to the **.soul** stage, Herdus **goes insane** — the vast expansion of consciousness may have exposed him to the Dreamer Below's psychic influence |
| ~2577 | A mysterious Photoli delivers "The Revelation" — Herdus founds the **Church of the Dreamer Below** and joins the BMAH in Yuldun Waste |
| ~2577-2624 | Rises to **Leading Auctioneer** of the BMAH while secretly channeling resources to the Church |
| ~2605 | Takes infant **Saand** (Punaab) as a **vessel for the Dreamer Below** |
| ~2624 | **Killed by Saand** during the Never Alone incident — Church exposed, BMAH scattered |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Herdus.soul** is a pivotal character connecting four major storylines: **Zenith Door** (co-founder with Chior.eldr), **Church of the Dreamer Below** (founder), **BMAH** (former leader), and the **Never Alone** incident (his death). His arc spans from idealist peace-builder to insane cult prophet to underworld kingpin.

## Key Institutions

### Iris Academy (HQ: Planet Carvum)

> *"The search for knowledge is the common unifying endeavor of all sentient species."* — **Professor Exfeheros, Headmaster**

The galaxy's **most prestigious research institute** in the known sectors of Galia. A **neutral institution** devoted to advancing knowledge for the greater good.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Founded** | ~2525 (concept); ~2545 (relocated to Carvum, Zenith Door) |
| **Founders** | **Chior.eldr** and **Professor Exfeheros Platinum Bar** |
| **HQ** | **Planet Carvum** — abandoned mining planet, Zenith Door sector |
| **Leader** | **Professor Exfeheros Platinum Bar** — Headmaster |
| **Governance** | **Council of Professors** — Headmaster elected from among them |
| **COP Status** | Allied but autonomous — **direct COP Senate seat**. Likened to Cambridge/Oxford with parliamentary seats |
| **Branch** | **Anchorage** — outpost in Glowhaven |
| **AI** | An **AI overseer** monitors faculty and student activities |

#### Professor Exfeheros Platinum Bar

- **Species**: **Punaab** (confirmed via naming convention and multiple cross-references)
- One of Galia's **greatest intellectuals** and scientists
- Friend and ally of Chior.eldr
- **~2573**: Developed specialized equipment enabling exploration of the **Weeping Nebula's toxic rain** — the first technological breakthrough of its kind
- **~2576**: Established a small **Iris Academy research settlement** in the Weeping Nebula
- Authorized a **deep-sea exploration expedition** to investigate anomalous readings from the **Bluevael Prime Dead Zone**
- **Ponel.eldr** (Anfoil State) has attempted to bribe or destroy the Iris Academy — viewing it as a rival

#### Carvum — The Academy Planet

- An **abandoned mining planet** — Carvum's ancient mines still generate significant revenue as the Academy's **sole planetary proprietor**
- Features **rural villages** alongside multiple research institutions
- Retired **Commander Goe** (MUD) lives on a farm near the Academy — a Convergence War veteran who led HRZ expeditions
- Selected for its strategic location: **near the HRZ threshold**, within reach of COP governing bodies — a perfect balance of scientific exploration and political influence

#### Major Achievements

- **Project Starpath** — the Academy's defining breakthrough: a revolutionary **network of warp gates** that reconnected the fractured galaxy and reignited interstellar trade. Ushered in a **new Golden Age**
- **Galactic Indices**: GFI-8 (no army, but former students include top military strategists), GWI-5 (largest research institute, patents, endowments), GPI-3 (COP Senate seat, shapes policy from behind the scenes)
- **Galactic partnerships**: Cooperation agreement with the **Scriptorium of Lumikir**; Anchorage branch in **Glowhaven**; research settlement in **Weeping Nebula**; expedition authorized to **Bluevael Dead Zone**

### The Dawn Vanguard

- **COP elite fleet** created specifically to guard the **HRZ/MRZ border**
- **Flagship**: **C11-class capital ship** — commanded by a **Sogmian officer**
- Capable of **obliterating a squadron of Fimbul Mambas** with a single missile barrage
- Equipped with **tractor beams** for rescue and recovery operations
- **Elite troops, extremely powerful** — first line against HRZ threats
- Stationed permanently at Zenith Door due to its geographic position

#### The Paradox of Zenith Door

An elite military force stationed in a pacifist's domain. Chior did not invite the Dawn Vanguard — the COP stationed them here. Their presence is simultaneously:

- A **shield** — protecting Zenith Door from HRZ threats (Tufa incursions, unknown dangers)
- A **sword** — that could be turned on Chior if his opposition to Scarka becomes too threatening
- A **symbol** — of the COP's reach into even the most peaceful corners of Galia

## The COP Crisis: Chior vs. Vaor Scarka

### The COP's War

> [!WARNING]
> The "war" Chior opposes refers to **recent COP invasions of MRZ territories** — including Pergamos and Frenir. The peace-keeping body has become an aggressor.

- The COP, under Vaor Scarka's influence, has **invaded MRZ sectors**:
  - **Pergamos** — COP military intervention (`canon/geography/sectors/pergamos.md`)
  - **Frenir** — COP-imposed order after warlord era (`canon/geography/sectors/frenir.md`)
- Scarka's declared milestones: Frenir slave traders destroyed, Jorvik influence disrupted, Pergamos agreements signed
- Despite a declared cease-fire, the threat of escalation remains

### Chior's Counter-Offensive (Diplomatic)

- **~2623**: Chior.eldr **vocally opposes** the COP's military expansionism
- Calls for an **emergency cease-fire vote**: COP army limits + MRZ population rights
- Proposes an **emergency motion** for Council DAO approval
- Behind the scenes, the **Ustur faction seeks a peaceful resolution**

### The Free Cities of Humanity

- **~2623**: **Redam** (MRZ-1) reaches out to old MUD colonies including **Zenith Door** and **Free Harbors** to pressure COP into accepting a **truce in the MRZ**
- This loose alliance — the Free Cities of Humanity — represents the first organized MRZ-wide resistance to COP military expansion, and Chior is its most prominent voice

### The Rivalry

- **Chior.eldr**: Genuine peace through diplomacy, consensus, and centuries of patient relationship-building
- **Vaor Scarka**: Victory only through vanquishing adversaries and expanding COP dominion
- This is the **galaxy's highest-stakes rivalry** — an immortal Elder vs. the most powerful military commander in Galia

## Narrative Hooks

- **Chior's Crusade**: An immortal Elder using centuries of wisdom to prevent galactic war
- **Exfeheros & Chior**: The academic and the diplomat — allies shaping Galia's future from Zenith Door
- **COP as Aggressor**: The irony of a peace council waging war — and the man trying to stop it from within
- **Dawn Vanguard Tension**: An elite military force stationed in a pacifist's domain — shield, sword, or leash?
- **HRZ Gateway**: What comes from beyond the border that requires this level of defense?
- **The Herdus Ghost**: Chior's co-founder became a cult prophet and criminal kingpin — does Chior know the full truth?
- **The Chior-Ponel Mirror**: Two students, one master, opposite conclusions — who was right?
- **Iris Academy Secrets**: Hidden vaults of classified combat technology. What else does Carvum hide beneath its mining tunnels?
