## Overview

The Barrot Gateway sits within the heart of the ONI medium zone. Deceptively ordinary at first glance — boasting respectable mineral wealth but unremarkable biodiversity. Yet at its heart lies an **enigmatic wormhole**, a cosmic riddle that has transformed this sector into an intergalactic hub for tourism and speculation.

---

## The Legend of Barrot

### The Explorer

**Barrot** was a **Sogmian of House Xictus**. In the era preceding the Convergence War, he dared to approach the wormhole enigma. He died a few years before the current canon timeline — a legendary figure who entered Galia's permanent historical record.

### The Journey

- **~2495**: A strange portal opens in MRZ-14 — Barrot enters
- Barrot entered the wormhole and returned within **moments**
- But he was **obviously changed** by the passage of time
- His vessel was **inexplicably different** from when it departed
- He possessed **otherworldly trinkets** that defied explanation

### The Tale

Barrot described a realm beyond comprehension:

- A place where **the laws of gravity faltered** and **time held no dominion**
- Within this surreal world, he stumbled upon a **grand mansion**
- Inside, an **ethereal being** challenged him to **games of wit and strategy**
- Barrot **triumphed** over the trials
- As reward, the being gifted him the enigmatic trinkets
- He was then whisked back to Galia

### What Really Happened (SECRET — Not Public Knowledge)

> [!CAUTION]
> The following is canon truth but NOT public knowledge in-universe. Only the Archon and Creator know this.

The wormhole is a gateway to a **pocket dimension in the HRZ** controlled by a **Photoli**. This Photoli:

- Lures "guests" into the pocket dimension through the wormhole
- Challenges them to **games** — but these are survival trials, not mere puzzles
- Awards **prizes to the survivors**
- **Broadcasts** these events to other creatures and civilizations in the HRZ — a galactic blood sport disguised as entertainment
- Barrot was one of the rare survivors who made it back to Galia

### The Mass Crossing Attempts

- **~2495**: Following Barrot's fame, many people attempt to cross the now-famous Barrot Gateway
- At this time, the gate was still approachable — people **could enter**
- However, upon finding the **trial on the other side**, most quickly turned back, **terrified for their lives**
- Only a handful returned, most of them **heavily traumatized** and refusing to talk about what they found
- Those who pressed deeper into the trial were never seen again

### Legacy

- The **veracity of Barrot's account** remains the topic of endless public debate
- While many entered the gateway in the early period, **no one successfully replicated Barrot's full journey** — most fled in terror, and those who pressed on never returned
- **~2517**: **Barrot himself** goes back inside the gateway to explore the strange dimension once more — **he never returns**. From this point, it becomes **impossible to enter the portal**
- Over time, the public came to believe the wormhole is unapproachable (spatial distortion prevents approach — this may be connected to Barrot's final entry, or the Photoli's selective filtering)
- The legend birthed an entire economy of gambling and tourism

## The Artifacts

### Barrot's Trinkets

- Currently held by **Barrot's family** (House Xictus descendants)
- Objects of immense speculation and study

### The Temporal Pendant (Confirmed Artifact)

- The only **verified** artifact from Barrot's journey
- A pendant capable of **traversing temporal distortions**
- This is how Barrot navigated back from the pocket dimension
- Its properties defy conventional physics — a genuine anomaly
- Location: with Barrot's family (House Xictus)

---

## The Wormhole

### Public Understanding (What Galia Knows)

- The wormhole remains an **elusive sentinel** at the sector's center
- **Unapproachable**: Ships seemingly drift in perpetuity, never bridging the distance
- A **cosmic illusion** suggesting massive spatial distortions
- Attempts to reach it are exercises in futility — you can see it, but never arrive
- COP and ONI have **attempted to study it but failed** — current stance is "leave it alone"

### True Nature (SECRET)

- The wormhole is a **controlled portal** to a Photoli's pocket dimension in the HRZ
- The "unapproachable" distortion may be selective — the Photoli chooses who enters
- The spatial distortion is real but may serve as a filter, not a barrier
- Connected to HRZ space — making it technically a hidden backdoor into the most dangerous zone

## Economy: The Gaming Sector

### Tourism & Gambling

- The sector evolved into a **thriving tourist destination** built on Barrot's legend
- **Opulent gaming estates** inspired by his narrative have mushroomed across the sector
- Visitors wager **ATLAS** for a chance to earn elusive rewards
- The games echo the legendary challenges from Barrot's account — wit and strategy
- A galactic destination that blends mystery, luxury, and risk

### Governance

- Run by **local consortiums and casino operators** (not faction-controlled)
- Specific factions and named operators TBD (Creator will develop later)
- The casinos are the real power in the sector — wealth from tourism dwarfs mineral extraction
