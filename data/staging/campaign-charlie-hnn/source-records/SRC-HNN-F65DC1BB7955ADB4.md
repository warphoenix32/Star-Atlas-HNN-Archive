SPCECMX is a new collection of funny and entertaining NFTs for the Star Atlas metaverse created by Andrea and Marcello, two Italian enthusiasts of the Star Atlas metaverse. Together, with Mauro an artist with a unique style, the trio have decided to combine their passion for the game with their creativity to launch a collection of funny and entertaining NFTs called “SPCECMX”.
SPCECMX, a limited edition collection of NFTs that will be exclusively sold on Magic Eden, one of the most renowned crypto marketplaces in the world. “SPCECMX” vision is to answer the question: “What happens when digital art meets the funny side of the cosmos?”
Press enter or click to view image in full sizePreview of SPCECMX Fimbul Lowbie NFT
The purpose of “SPCECMX” is to spread awareness of the Star Atlas world and to document in a funny way the highlights of the metaverse’s roadmap, as well as the community surrounding it. While waiting for the official launch of the game, Mauro, Andrea and Marcello are committed to entertaining the community in an ironic and sarcastic way, through the creation of a collection of NFTs containing colorful figurines that represent people, objects, and scenes referring to the world of Star Atlas.
