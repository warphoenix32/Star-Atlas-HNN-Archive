## Overview

**Calico Industries** emerged from a visionary union of **MUD and ONI innovators** who diverged from traditional militaristic paths to champion cultural integration, diplomacy, and cooperative growth.

## Key Facts

- **Philosophy**: True strength arises from **diversity, not force**. Genuine peace comes when every voice is heard, every culture respected, and every idea tested.
- **Visual Identity**: Lighter hues and silhouettes — symbolizing **openness and unity**. Stark contrast to angular, intimidating militaristic designs.
- **Technology Focus**: **Stealth innovations**, exotic sciences, and enhanced agricultural methods serving diverse populations.
- **Rivalry**: Criticized by **Pearce** (MUD military-industrial faction), who view Calico's emphasis on commerce and cultural exchange as threatening to order.

## CARY — Civilian Division

- **Purpose**: Dedicated to **sustaining life and habitats** across the galaxy.
- **Mission**: A tangible vision of prosperity through shared effort and mutual respect — not domination.
- **Impact**: Promotes **interspecies collaboration** and sustainable development.

## Ethos

- Lasting peace cannot be secured solely through military might.
- Fostering **mutual understanding, collective enterprise**, and the enrichment from the convergence of countless perspectives is essential.
- Calico proves that **strength from unity and diversity** is a viable alternative future for Galia.
