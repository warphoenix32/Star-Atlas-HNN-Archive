## Preserved Official Text

Collectibles in Star Atlas are a category of NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) that represent unique digital items each stored on the Solana blockchain. These items are part of the broader Star Atlas metaverse and can include cosmetic, aesthetic, or potentially utility-driven elements across different game modes.
Types of Collectibles
The Star Atlas Collectibles category includes a variety of NFT assets such as:
Crew Gear – Cosmetic items
Charms – Small symbolic items
Emotes – Animation-based expressions used for character interaction
Memories – Never Alone Mission Rewards
Posters – Limited-edition artwork NFTs, some originally tied to early sales campaigns
Pets – Digital companions that may serve aesthetic or light gameplay functions in the future
Skins – Custom visual modifications for ships
Trophies – Commemorative items representing participation or achievements in past events
Each collectible may serve different purposes ranging from cosmetic use to unlocking access, recognition, or exclusives across the Star Atlas ecosystem
💡 Note:Collectibles are distinct from gameplay NFTs like ships, crew and structures. They are primarily cosmetic and optional, though some may have utility in future releases
Marketplace Details
You can explore, buy or sell collectibles on the Star Atlas Galactic Marketplace
