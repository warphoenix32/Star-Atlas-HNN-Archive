## Overview

The **Cataclysm** is a territory within the **High-Risk Zone (HRZ)** where **Iris influences reality itself**. It is the native homeland of the **Tufa** species.

---

## Key Facts

## Iris: The Sentient Core

- **True Nature**: **Iris** is a **sentient planet** in the heart of the HRZ.
- **Relationship**: Sees the Cataclysm as her territory and the **Tufa as her children**.
- **The Fracture**:
  - During the pre-war era, factions discovered how to enter the Cataclysm.
  - They expropriated **Genesium** (the rarest, most valuable material in the galaxy) by **killing Tufa** and harvesting it from their bodies.
  - This trauma caused Iris to develop a "personality disorder," fracturing her mind.
  - **Cosmic Currents**: Manifestations of Iris's fractured, paranoid mind.
- **Current State**: Extremely paranoid and protective. Repels all invaders by default.

## The Convergence War Connection

- **~2510**: Deep space explorer **Gyun Oman** discovers the Cataclysm — he scans the data and **illegally sells it to all 3 rival factions** (MUD, ONI, Ustur), each believing they are the sole recipients
- **Casus Belli**: The factions (except Sogmians) fought to control Iris and its unlimited energy source.
- **~2512**: All 3 factions, thinking they were the **sole owners** of the cataclysmic location, converge to settle the area — **all-out war breaks out** after the inability to settle the disputed territory
- **Sogmian Intervention**: Sogmians recognized Tufa sentience and opposed the harvesting/slavery.
- **~2522**: **Iris conceals itself** and the space known as the Cataclysm. No one is able to get inside or outside anymore — Iris actively sealed the region, cutting off all access.
- **Post-War Status**:
  - **COP Ban**: The **Council of Peace (COP)** banned all unauthorized travel to the HRZ altogether — formalizing a reality Iris had already imposed.
  - **Guarded Tech**: Technology/systems to reach the Cataclysm are guarded "under 7 keys."
