## Preserved Article

Star Atlas is proud to announce the third edition of the Council of Peace Assembly (COPA) , the premier showcase for leadership, creativity, and collective vision in the Galia Expanse.

In the wake of sweeping changes across the galaxy, this COPA arrives at a pivotal moment. Minor factions rise, major players reposition, and the Medium Zone echoes with the ambitions of both rebels and rulers. Amid this turbulence, captains and their communities gather to define the future through cooperation, strategy, and the power of unity.

Will your DAC rise to claim its place among the stars?

COPA is your moment to shine. Win glory. Win recognition. Win the future.


## Key Information for Participants


## Registration Phase

- Opens: June 23rd (Access the registration form via the COPA webpage: https://copa.staratlas.com/ )
- Closes: July 17th (Late entries will not be accepted)
- Approved DACs Announced: July 21st (Official list published on Star Atlas Discord and social media)

## Competition Details

- Event Date: TBA (Schedule will be released on Discord)
- Format: Slideshow presentation, which will be shared live during the scheduled COPA broadcast and competition.
- Judging: A panel of experts will evaluate each DAC’s submission based on a set of criteria.

## Evaluation Criteria

A distinguished panel of judges will review DAC presentations according to the following categories:


## 1. Story & Structure

- DAC lore, theme, and narrative cohesion
- Governance framework
- Membership structure and recruitment process

## 2. Building & Development

- Use of Star Atlas IP in launches, campaigns, or products
- Community-building efforts
- Tools, programs, and achievements contributed to the ecosystem

## 3. Vision & Future Strategy

- Planned use of Star Atlas IP
- Roadmaps for future development (tools, products, in-game strategy)
- Long-term sustainability and goals

## 4. Growth & Community Engagement

- Strategies for expanding membership and visibility
- Marketing and promotional efforts inside and outside the Star Atlas ecosystem
Note: Judges will place significant emphasis on tangible impact . DACs must provide verifiable evidence to support the claims made in their presentations.


## Prizes: Claim Your Space in the Galaxy

Victors of this edition will earn prestigious properties in the heart of their factions’ Central Space Stations, a tangible proof of their influence in the Star Atlas universe.

- 1st Place: Tier 3 Property at the Central Space Station (faction-aligned)
- 2nd Place: Tier 2 Property at the Central Space Station
- 3rd Place: Tier 1 Property at the Central Space Station
In addition, all DACs who present a high-quality presentation, regardless of whether they reach the podium, will be given access to a direct line of communication with the Star Atlas team for support in developing their activities.


## What is COPA?

COPA is a celebration of the Star Atlas community’s ingenuity and drive. Whether you’re a veteran commander or a newcomer building your first DAC, participating in COPA offers:

- Deep insights from Star Atlas leadership and top-tier DACs
- Practical knowledge in building and running a successful organization
- Opportunities to network and collaborate with other players
- The chance to shape galactic history while competing for real rewards
