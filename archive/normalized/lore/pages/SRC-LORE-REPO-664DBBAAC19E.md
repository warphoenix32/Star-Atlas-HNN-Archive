## Overview

Situated deep within the Medium Zone, **Ilidae** is immediately identifiable by its distinct **amber luminescence** emitted from its star.

## Key Lore

### The Monoliths

- **~2466**: Strange monoliths are first found in the Ilidae Sector
- The amber hue is **harnessed by monoliths** strategically positioned on each planet.
- Purpose and origin: unknown to the galaxy at large.
- **~2646**: A **MUD explorer** discovers that the monoliths are actually a **star map** — their coordinates would send captains **deep into the High-Risk Zone**. The implications of what lies at those coordinates remain unknown

### The Termiks

- **~2471**: After several expeditions, various branches of the **semi-sentient species** known as the Termiks are discovered across the sector's planets
- Researchers learn they are the ones building the monoliths, but the reason remains a mystery
- Tirelessly search for **minerals and ores** to construct more monoliths.
- **Uniform behavior** across all branches — yet no indication they're aware of parallel activities on neighboring planets.
- **Archon Confirmed**: The monolith blueprint was **genetically implanted** into the Termiks. They don't learn it — they are born knowing it.
- **Archon Confirmed**: The Termiks are unknowingly building a **feedback loop / energy network** — monoliths harvest the amber star's energy, Termiks build more monoliths, network grows.

### Dangers

- Plentiful natural resources attract outsiders.
- Some exploit Termiks — capturing them as **rare pets or hunting prizes**.
- **~2498**: **Black Market groups** begin hunting the Termiks to sell them as **slaves** — Termiks can be exploited as either pets, prizes, or slaves depending on the buyer
- **Hunters often vanish** in Ilidae's untamed expanses without a trace.
- (Termik-related mystery — to be explored with Termik species deep-dive)

### ECOS Intervention

- **~2501**: The **ECOS Fleet rains down** over the groups attacking the Termiks
- ECOS establishes a **permanent defensive base** in the sector for Termik protection
- This aligns with the **Grove Doctrine** (~2493) — ECOS's mandate to protect galactic wildlife
- **~2569**: In cooperation with the **Iris Academy** (`canon/geography/sectors/zenith_door.md`), ECOS establishes a **sustainable city** dedicated to the **research and protection** of the Termiks
