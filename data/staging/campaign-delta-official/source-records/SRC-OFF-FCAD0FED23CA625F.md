What is Build? ​
Star Atlas Build is the official hub for developers and creators building in the Star Atlas ecosystem. This program allows you to build using our IP with no fees until you reach $1,000,000 in revenue.
Whether you're crafting tools, designing content, or launching your own Star Atlas-inspired project, this site gives you everything you need to get started:
Access to core on-chain programs powering SAGE, the DAO, Marketplace and more
A growing Galaxy API for querying live data like items, markets and inventory
TypeScript packages to simplify integration (like @staratlas/sage or @staratlas/data-source)
A Creative IP program for using official Star Atlas artwork, lore and branding
Who is Build for? ​
Developers — building tools, dashboards, bots, or blockchain integrations
Creators — designing games, art, merch, lore, or media inspired by the Star Atlas universe
Curious explorers — just looking to understand what’s possible and how to get involved
Whether you're building on-chain, off-chain, or something in between, this site is your perfect starting point.
