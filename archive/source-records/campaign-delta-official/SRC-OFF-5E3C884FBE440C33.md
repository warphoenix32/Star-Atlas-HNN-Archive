## Preserved Official Text

No, you do not need a crypto wallet to start playing Holosim. The game is designed to be free-to-play and accessible directly from your browser.
When you create your character, the game automatically generates a background account for you. This account lets you save progress, form fleets, complete quests, and explore without needing to connect a wallet.
