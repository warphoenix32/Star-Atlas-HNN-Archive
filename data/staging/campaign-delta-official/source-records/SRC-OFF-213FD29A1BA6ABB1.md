## Preserved Official Text

In SAGE, transporting crew members between starbases can be essential for strategic gameplay, such as repositioning your team or preparing for new operations. Fortunately, some ships have extra passenger slots that allow you to carry crew members from one starbase to another.
Step-by-Step Guide to Transporting Crew:
Select a Ship with Passenger Slots: Choose a ship that has extra passenger slots beyond the number required for basic operation. For example, ships like the Lowbie, Maxhog, and Niruch have additional space for passengers.
Assign Crew Members to the Passenger Slots: Once you have selected a suitable ship, manually add the crew members you want to transport as passengers. To learn how to manually add crew members
Plan Your Route: Determine the destination starbase and plan the route for your ship. Ensure that your ship has enough fuel and resources for the journey
Execute the Transport: Start the journey to transport the crew members from the current starbase to the desired destination. Monitor your ship's status and fuel levels during the trip to ensure a safe arrival.
