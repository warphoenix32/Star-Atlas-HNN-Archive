iBUYPOWER Launches Star Atlas Branded PC
Posted by Krigs | Jul 22, 2022 | Featured
During the very first Council of Peace Assembly, a Star Atlas community member won a Star Atlas branded PC made in partnership with iBUYPOWER. It was teased that a future product line would be available for the rest of the community to buy. At the first 426 Live, it was officially announced these products will now be available to order from iBUYPOWER. The centerpiece of this Star Atlas package is the limited edition Star Atlas wrapped PC, available as both a case and fully built PC. This is the first time that any type of officially licensed Star Atlas products will be available to the public for purchase. These items will be offered in limited quantity on a first come first serve basis. To be eligible for purchase, buyers must register their intent with iBUYPOWER here.
Star Atlas Branded PC
Star Atlas Branded PC by iBUYPOWER
Each PC will be stamped with a serial number as part of a limited edition series. Players considering purchasing the fully spec’d iBUYPOWER PC should check out the specs below. Current suggested MSRP is currently ~$2,500
PC Specs:
HYTE Y60 Star Atlas Ultimate Edition Branded Case 12th Gen
Intel i9 12900K
KF NVIDIA RTX3080+ or higher
16GB Ram (unless SA recommends 32GB)
Z690 Motherboard 1TB Hard Drive
Liquid Cooled AIO with customized Star Atlas Animation
Custom Professional Grade Headset
HYTE eclipse HG10 Star Atlas Edition Wireless Headset
If players still want to get in on the Star Atlas branded paraphernalia without the four figure price tag, they can opt to purchase a Star Atlas Branded PC Case, Headset, and Desk Mat for ~$280. This option allows players to still get in on these limited edition items at a much lower cost.
