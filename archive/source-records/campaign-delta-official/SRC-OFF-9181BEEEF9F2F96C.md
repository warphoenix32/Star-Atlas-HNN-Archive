- Access the Operations Tab At your Faction’s Starbase, open the Operations tab to: Base Management: Resupply your starbase with essentials like food and toolkits to keep it operational and earn Loyalty Points (LP).
- Craft Workshop: Select recipes, set quantities, and assign crew for crafting.
- Staff Effort: Track crafting and upgrade projects in progress.
- Using the Craft Workshop Choose a recipe: Pick the item you wish to craft from your unlocked recipes.
- Set quantity: Decide how many you want to produce.
- Assign crew: The more crew you assign, the faster crafting completes.
- Start crafting: Approve the on-chain transaction to begin crafting.
- Track and Claim Crafted Items In Staff Effort, you can: View Crafting Projects: Monitor your current crafting jobs and claim finished items.
- Check Upgrade Projects: Track your participation in starbase upgrades to unlock advanced recipes and capabilities.
