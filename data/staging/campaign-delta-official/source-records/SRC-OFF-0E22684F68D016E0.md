## Preserved Official Text

The Star Atlas Showroom is the first playable product of Star Atlas built in Unreal Engine 5, allowing players to interact with the metaverse in full 3D with blockchain-integrated gameplay. It showcases cutting-edge visuals using Nanite and Lumen technologies, and serves as a development hub where new features and play modes are continuously introduced.
Choose Your Character
You no longer play as a preset character. Instead, you can now choose any Crew Member you own to use as your avatar in the Showroom
There are two ways to get a Crew Member:
Buy a Crew Pack from the Star Atlas Marketplace and open it to reveal one or more randomized crewBrowse Crew Packs
Buy individual Crew directly on secondary marketplaces like TensorBuy Crew on Tensor
Once you own a Crew Member, you can select them in-game to represent you in the UE5 world
Main Game Modes Available
1. Galia (Open World)
Explore the Showroom’s full galaxy experience:
Summon your owned ships and fly freely
Navigate to asteroids and mine resources
Dock and land at space stations
Discover new gameplay features added regularly
2. Training Mode
Practice and improve your gameplay in solo sessions:
Flight Trainer – Race
Flight Trainer – Dogfight
Gunplay Trainer
Other foundational modes to learn the controls
3. Fleet Command
Access the SAGE map from within the Showroom
View the current state of SAGE v0
See fleets and shipsNote: You cannot yet perform SAGE actions from this interface
4. Multiplayer Sessions

Features of the Showroom
Fully immersive Unreal Engine 5 environment using Nanite and Lumen
Blockchain wallet integration (Solana-compatible)
Summon your own NFTs — ships and crew — into the world
Use a demo ship (Floyd Liner) if you don’t own one
Integrated photo mode for cinematic screenshots
Dynamic lighting, exploration, and easter eggs
