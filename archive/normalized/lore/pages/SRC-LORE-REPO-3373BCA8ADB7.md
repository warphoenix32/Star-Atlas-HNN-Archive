## Named Hunts

### The First Great Hunt (~2527)

Ekkro Nimrod led all fourteen founding members against a Void Shrike nest threatening Communion's agricultural station. Nine days, thirty creatures eliminated, three Trackers lost. The operation proved the DAC model.

[Ashroot Grove](../factions/ashroot_grove.md) contracted three thousand Trackers — the largest single deployment in DAC history — for a six-week perimeter purge across Old Grove's continental border, working alongside twelve thousand Vigil Guard troops. ### The Gulch Maw Incident (~2609)

Two Wardens died before Eko identified its pattern: it hunted by resonance, drawn to active electronics.

Eko ordered all equipment powered down. The team tracked CC-012 for three days using nothing but sight, sound, and [Mierese](../species/mierese.md) olfactory senses.
