# Official Star Atlas Newsroom Extraction Batch 001

This batch extracts high-value historical facts from official Star Atlas newsroom articles. An official announcement establishes what ATMTA publicly stated at the time; roadmap language is not treated as proof of later delivery.

## 2021-06-24 — Seed round announcement

**Source:** https://experience.staratlas.com/newsroom/star-atlas-news/star-atlas-closes-seed-round-with-serum-leading-funding

The official newsroom published an announcement titled **“Star Atlas closes seed round with Serum leading funding”** on June 24, 2021. Michael Wagner was identified as Star Atlas co-founder and CEO. The currently retrievable page preserves the title, date and attribution but not the substantive financing terms, so amounts and participant details remain pending separate recovery.

## 2022-11-07 — Breakpoint roadmap and product announcements

**Source:** https://experience.staratlas.com/newsroom/star-atlas-news/cosmic-alpha-burst-star-atlas-unveils-roadmap-graphic-novel-sage-and-more-at

At Solana Breakpoint 2022, ATMTA publicly presented a multi-product development strategy and several named milestones:

- Project S.C.R.E.A.M. was publicly renamed **Star Atlas: Golden Era (SAGE)**.
- SAGE was described as a top-down, WebGL-based grand-strategy game sharing loops with later Star Atlas seasons.
- The Showroom was described as the first UE5 module and as available through the Epic Games Store.
- ATMTA presented an episodic seasonal roadmap, including a Showroom pre-season and eight later seasons.
- F-KIT was presented as an open-source Unreal Engine integration toolkit for Solana programs.
- Star Atlas: CORE was announced as an episodic narrative project, with the first chapter scheduled for November 7, 2022.
- Quarterly economic reports were presented as an established transparency practice.
- Atlas Brew was identified as a recurring Wednesday community discussion forum.

The article also reported a community gathering around Breakpoint and stated that more than 60 guilds were active at the time. That guild-count claim is an official contemporaneous statement, not independently verified membership data.

## 2022-11-09 — CORE public release

**Source:** https://experience.staratlas.com/newsroom/star-atlas-news/thrilling-to-the-core-star-atlas-releases-epic-graphic-novel

ATMTA announced the public release of **Star Atlas: CORE**, an episodic graphic novel. The article states that Chapter 1 became available on November 7, 2022.

The narrative was described as taking place shortly before the Convergence War and following a MUD protagonist after a catastrophic expedition in the High Risk Zone. The article names Tim McBurnie as lead artist and author and Matt Medney as Star Atlas head writer.

The initial commercial and collection model included:

- individual chapter releases;
- collectible covers;
- additional lore in final pages;
- an ARC Cypher puzzle spanning the planned catalog;
- an announced total of 18 chapters;
- a hardcover and digital compilation reward for completing the puzzle;
- a related ship-skin reward.

The article also introduced the **Voice of Iris** as an in-universe phenomenon: alleged guidance or transmissions experienced by people across the galaxy, with no direct evidence in the text that the phenomenon was literally connected to Iris.
