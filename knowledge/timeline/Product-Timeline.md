# Product Timeline

This chronology separates product announcements, roadmap statements, releases, revisions, and supersessions. A documentation date proves that the documentation existed on that date; it does not prove that the documented feature first became available then.

## 2021

### 2021-08-04 — Galactic Marketplace reported fully operational

**Description:** ATMTA announced that the Solana-based Galactic Marketplace had become fully operational. **Significance:** This is the earliest archived official release anchor for the repository's trading layer. **Affected entities:** Galactic Marketplace, Star Atlas, Solana. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-A465C97640C55838](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-A465C97640C55838.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Release claim from an official announcement. **Superseded:** Yes; a replacement marketplace was announced in 2022.

### 2021-09-07 — Galactic Asset Offering launched

**Description:** ATMTA announced the Galactic Asset Offering as a new generation of Star Atlas NFTs. **Significance:** The offering established the early ship-and-asset distribution program. **Affected entities:** Galactic Asset Offering, Galactic Marketplace, Star Atlas assets. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-B2C1AA5D0C8E9D15](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-B2C1AA5D0C8E9D15.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Official launch announcement; later asset waves are separate sale events. **Superseded:** No.

### 2021-12-17 — Faction Fleet / SCORE released

**Description:** ATMTA announced its inaugural on-chain gameplay experience, called SCORE in the announcement and Faction Fleet in later product language. Aephia independently dated the release to December 16 in its maintained guide. **Significance:** This was the first released on-chain gameplay loop and introduced ship enlistment for ATLAS rewards. **Affected entities:** SCORE, Faction Fleet, ATLAS, ships. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-0AED270D51ACD552](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-0AED270D51ACD552.md), [SRC-AEPHIA-21D7FE432327A762](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-21D7FE432327A762.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** The one-day discrepancy is retained: official publication on December 17; community guide states December 16 release. **Superseded:** Yes; its economic role was later wound down during the Starbased transition.

### 2021-12-24 — Faction Fleet operating instructions published

**Description:** ATMTA published instructions for pledging ships to factional fleet service. **Significance:** The publication documents the operational phase following the release announcement. **Affected entities:** Faction Fleet, SCORE, ships, ATLAS. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-CFB3A87BF1E48DDE](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-CFB3A87BF1E48DDE.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Implementation guidance, not a second release. **Superseded:** Yes.

## 2022

### 2022-07-22 — Replacement Galactic Marketplace announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced a new Galactic Marketplace after the 2021 operational release. **Significance:** This marks a major revision of the asset-trading layer and should not be collapsed into the original marketplace launch. **Affected entities:** Galactic Marketplace, ATLAS, Star Atlas assets. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-0C5AA46E01E189EC](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-0C5AA46E01E189EC.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Official announcement; the source title does not by itself establish the exact deployment instant. **Superseded:** No.

### 2022-07-22 — StarPath referral program announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced StarPath as the official referral program; Aephia published same-period operational coverage. **Significance:** StarPath added a formal user-acquisition and referral layer to the ecosystem. **Affected entities:** StarPath, Galactic Marketplace, Star Atlas community. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-8C3F18E4BEFF48B2](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-8C3F18E4BEFF48B2.md), [SRC-AEPHIA-A24F426C397362EE](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-A24F426C397362EE.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Announcement and community-observed availability are preserved as distinct evidence. **Superseded:** No.

### 2022-09-29 — Pre-alpha Showroom access instructions published

**Description:** ATMTA published both a Showroom overview and instructions for entering the pre-alpha environment. **Significance:** These publications document the transition from presentation material to a user-accessible UE5 module. **Affected entities:** Showroom, Epic Games Store, UE5. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-57027457FD1317B4](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-57027457FD1317B4.md), [SRC-OFF-340128795CCA03A9](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-340128795CCA03A9.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Access documentation precedes the next day's explicit launch announcement. **Superseded:** Yes; later Showroom releases revised the module.

### 2022-09-30 — UE5 pre-alpha Showroom launched

**Description:** ATMTA announced the UE5-powered pre-alpha Showroom on the Epic Games Store. **Significance:** This is the official release anchor for the first public UE5 Star Atlas module. **Affected entities:** Showroom, UE5, Epic Games Store. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-C6B9C26055452AF8](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-C6B9C26055452AF8.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Release, not merely roadmap intent. **Superseded:** Yes; R2.1 followed in 2023.

### 2022-11-07 — S.C.R.E.A.M. renamed SAGE in Breakpoint roadmap

**Description:** At Solana Breakpoint, ATMTA presented Project S.C.R.E.A.M. as Star Atlas: Golden Era (SAGE), a browser-based grand-strategy product, alongside a wider multi-product roadmap. **Significance:** This is the principal naming and roadmap transition from S.C.R.E.A.M. to SAGE. **Affected entities:** S.C.R.E.A.M., SAGE, Showroom, F-KIT, CORE. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-22181D98D7A1B870](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-22181D98D7A1B870.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Roadmap announcement; it is not a SAGE release. **Superseded:** No.

### 2022-11-09 — CORE Chapter 1 release reported

**Description:** ATMTA announced that CORE Chapter 1 had been publicly available since November 7. **Significance:** CORE became the first released episodic graphic-novel product in the Star Atlas universe. **Affected entities:** CORE, Gyun, MUD, Convergence War. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-1336E035AB9A6C39](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-1336E035AB9A6C39.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** The release date is November 7; November 9 is the announcement date. **Superseded:** No.

### 2022-11-18 — Golden Era Start Sequence roadmap published

**Description:** ATMTA published the Golden Era Start Sequence as a phased path toward SAGE-related gameplay and economic systems. **Significance:** It formalized a staged delivery model after the Breakpoint announcement. **Affected entities:** SAGE, Claim Stakes, Golden Era Start Sequence. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-7102D44F3A9B9DB6](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-7102D44F3A9B9DB6.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Roadmap plan; phases require separate execution evidence. **Superseded:** Partly; later implementations diverged and evolved.

### 2022-11-29 — Claim Stake and CSS land-sale phase announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced the second Start Sequence phase covering CSS land plots and Claim Stakes. **Significance:** This separated the asset-sale phase from later resource-generation functionality. **Affected entities:** Claim Stakes, CSS land plots, SAGE economy. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-AC23D0459AB9A1AC](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-AC23D0459AB9A1AC.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Announcement and sale planning, not proof of later in-game integration. **Superseded:** No.

## 2023

### 2023-04-26 — Escape Velocity public alpha began

**Description:** Aephia's contemporaneous guide dates the limited public Escape Velocity alpha to April 26; ATMTA described the on-chain movement technology the following day. **Significance:** Escape Velocity tested real-time on-chain ship movement and acted as a bridge toward SAGE. **Affected entities:** Escape Velocity, SAGE, Solana, ATLAS. **Sources:** [SRC-AEPHIA-29A1AF8C2ABBD4D0](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-29A1AF8C2ABBD4D0.md), [SRC-OFF-C189056066EBFEA7](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-C189056066EBFEA7.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Aephia records a limited test window ending July 7; the official source confirms the technology announcement. **Superseded:** Yes; SAGE Labs followed.

### 2023-06-02 — Showroom R2.1 released

**Description:** ATMTA released Showroom 2.1 with the Volant Station racing track, expanded flight-ready ships, marketplace integration, test flight, photography, and updated dogfighting. **Significance:** R2.1 moved the Showroom beyond static presentation into a broader playable UE5 module. **Affected entities:** Showroom, Volant Station, Galactic Marketplace, UE5. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-FC61779037A23908](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-FC61779037A23908.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Official release claim. **Superseded:** No; later UE5 builds continued to evolve.

### 2023-06-08 — Player-driven resource-economy transition announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced Claim Stake sales in ATLAS pairs, increased Escape Velocity resource generation, and the planned end of unlimited DAO R4 sales. **Significance:** This marks a deliberate shift from administrator-supplied resources toward player-generated inputs. **Affected entities:** Claim Stakes, R4, Escape Velocity, Faction Fleet, Galactic Marketplace. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-D023F6DAFA12F9AA](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-D023F6DAFA12F9AA.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Announcement and confirmed sale listing are distinguished from completion of the wider economic transition. **Superseded:** No.

### Estimated 2023-09 — SAGE Labs launched

**Description:** Later Aephia reporting places the launch of SAGE Labs in late September 2023; HNN reported two weeks after launch that the alpha had generated unusually high Solana transaction activity. **Significance:** SAGE Labs introduced sustained on-chain mining, crafting, transport, and market gameplay. **Affected entities:** SAGE Labs, SAGE, Solana, ATLAS. **Sources:** [SRC-AEPHIA-3D6BA2ECA269970E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-3D6BA2ECA269970E.md), [SRC-HNN-B7CADCEE3659B6AB](../../archive/source-records/campaign-charlie-hnn/SRC-HNN-B7CADCEE3659B6AB.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Exact release day is not established by the archived official corpus. **Superseded:** Partly; Starbased revised the economic loop.

## 2024

### 2024-04-02 — Starbased economic phase scheduled and community-observed

**Description:** Aephia reported Starbased for April 2 as a replacement economic engine and documented the expected end of Faction Fleet emissions and removal of mining ships from Faction Claims. **Significance:** Starbased shifted rewards toward active SAGE play and materially revised the prior passive-reward economy. **Affected entities:** Starbased, SAGE Labs, Faction Fleet, Faction Claims, ATLAS. **Sources:** [SRC-AEPHIA-3D6BA2ECA269970E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-3D6BA2ECA269970E.md), [SRC-AEPHIA-BF39BD68F68A5FB2](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-BF39BD68F68A5FB2.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Community-source release evidence; no matching dated official release page survives in Campaign Delta. **Superseded:** No.

### Estimated 2024-12 — SAGE 3D launched

**Description:** Aephia's year-end review reports that SAGE 3D launched in mid-December as an intended successor to placeholder SAGE Labs, while noting missing quality-of-life features and continued parallel relevance of SAGE Labs. **Significance:** This marks an attempted transition from the earlier 2D interface toward a 3D SAGE experience. **Affected entities:** SAGE 3D, SAGE Labs, Starbased. **Sources:** [SRC-AEPHIA-C9AE050CD9C1886D](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-C9AE050CD9C1886D.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Month-level community retrospective; exact release day remains unresolved. **Superseded:** No.

## 2025

### 2025-06-05 — C4 publicly discussed as a product evolution

**Description:** Intergalactic Herald published a curated recap of a Star Atlas Economic Forum focused on C4, followed by Atlas Brew recaps describing C4 as the next evolution of Star Atlas. **Significance:** These records establish public discussion of C4 before the official PTR documentation appeared. **Affected entities:** C4, SAGE, Star Atlas Economic Forum, Atlas Brew. **Sources:** [SRC-IH-0E70509CB73938D1](../../archive/source-records/campaign-bravo-intergalactic-herald/SRC-IH-0E70509CB73938D1.md), [SRC-IH-0BC8CB9C78AD008A](../../archive/source-records/campaign-bravo-intergalactic-herald/SRC-IH-0BC8CB9C78AD008A.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Curated summaries of linked community events; discussion is not release evidence. **Superseded:** No.

### 2025-07-23 — Holosim documented as playable by Aephia

**Description:** Aephia published a player guide describing Holosim as a free-to-play browser strategy MMO with fleet, starbase, mining, transport, crafting, and combat systems. **Significance:** This is the earliest dated archived guide demonstrating community access to Holosim. **Affected entities:** Holosim, SAGE Starbased, fleets, starbases. **Sources:** [SRC-AEPHIA-A26222BF7C6B58DA](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-A26222BF7C6B58DA.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Guide-publication date; exact first release date remains unresolved. **Superseded:** No.

### 2025-12-02 — Official Fleet Rentals documentation published

**Description:** Star Atlas Support published a Fleet Rentals article set covering rental creation, duration, expiration, fees, fleet scores, crew, and resource handling. **Significance:** By this date the official support corpus treated Fleet Rentals as an operational MVP. **Affected entities:** Fleet Rentals, fleets, crew, ATLAS. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-7F41E99909FE23BD](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-7F41E99909FE23BD.md), [SRC-OFF-F6BFD210BB78F1B3](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-F6BFD210BB78F1B3.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Documentation publication is not proof that December 2 was the feature's first release date. **Superseded:** No.

### 2025-12-02 — Official Holosim documentation suite published

**Description:** Star Atlas Support documented fleet management, automation, zATLAS, starbases, mining, transport, crafting, combat, quests, characters, and persistence in Holosim. **Significance:** This provides an official dated functional snapshot after Aephia's July guide. **Affected entities:** Holosim, zATLAS, fleets, starbases. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-4D11337D80379C85](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-4D11337D80379C85.md), [SRC-OFF-0BFA050EF12E2E17](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-0BFA050EF12E2E17.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Current documentation, not historical release proof. **Superseded:** No.

### 2025-12-03 — Official Starbased documentation snapshot published

**Description:** Star Atlas Support documented Starbased movement, mining, crafting, starbase maintenance, council-rank progression, and local markets. **Significance:** This confirms the officially supported Starbased feature set by late 2025. **Affected entities:** Starbased, SAGE, starbases, local markets, council ranks. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-A6A4A97A9D09AA1E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-A6A4A97A9D09AA1E.md), [SRC-OFF-EF4F170FCDBC4B94](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-EF4F170FCDBC4B94.md), [SRC-OFF-1206858B54C3E67E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-1206858B54C3E67E.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Documentation snapshot, not a backdated launch claim. **Superseded:** No.

## 2026

**Description:** Star Atlas Support published instructions for entering the C4 public test environment, creating a profile, importing test assets, navigating the map, creating fleets, and reporting bugs. **Significance:** This is the first archived official evidence of user-accessible C4 testing. **Affected entities:** C4 PTR, SAGE, Galia Expanse, Claim Stakes, fleets. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-E061D3A6454697AB](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-E061D3A6454697AB.md), [SRC-OFF-DE76DDC4E8B8A28E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-DE76DDC4E8B8A28E.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** PTR implementation, not mainnet or production release. **Superseded:** No.

### 2026-06-03 — C4 PTR mining, movement, and scanning documented

**Description:** Follow-up support articles documented mining, fleet movement, and scanning in C4 PTR. **Significance:** These records establish implemented test-environment loops separately from the June 2 access announcement. **Affected entities:** C4 PTR, mining, fleets, scanning. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-952AB5D6D09DD6BF](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-952AB5D6D09DD6BF.md), [SRC-OFF-707BAD732E32BEAE](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-707BAD732E32BEAE.md), [SRC-OFF-4F4170FA1199794B](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-4F4170FA1199794B.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Test implementation only. **Superseded:** No.
