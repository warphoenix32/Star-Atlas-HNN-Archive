## Preserved Official Text

To interact with the Star Atlas universe via play.staratlas.com, you’ll need to create a player profile. The process is quick and easy, just follow the steps below.
📝 Note: You’ll need a Solana wallet already funded with SOL to complete this process. Learn how to create one in our guide:👉 What is a Solana Wallet and how do I get one?
Step 1: Visit PLAY
Go to https://play.staratlas.com
Accept the cookie prompt if it appears
Step 2: Open the Profile Menu
Click on the profile icon located in the bottom-left corner of the screen
Step 3: Choose Your Faction
You'll be prompted to select a faction:
MUD
ONI
USTUR
Choose carefully—this choice is permanent unless you register a new wallet
Learn more about each faction in our guide:👉 Understanding Factions in Star Atlas
Step 4: Approve the Wallet Transaction
Approve the wallet transaction when prompted.
⚠️ A small amount of SOL is required to register your wallet on-chain
Congratulations! You have now set up your Star Atlas profile for https://play.staratlas.com/
