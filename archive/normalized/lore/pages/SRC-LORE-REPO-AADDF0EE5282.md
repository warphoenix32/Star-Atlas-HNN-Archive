## Overview

- **Name**: Manus Ultima Divina (MUD)
- **Nature**: A unified government of humanity, rooted in divine revelation and led by the **Holy Family**.
- **Philosophy**: Expansion, Dominion, and the "Promised Paradise" (Cataclysm).
- **Self-Image**: God's Chosen People, destined to rule the stars.

---

## History & Origins

### 1. The Pre-History (Solar Punk Earth)

- **Earth State**: A sustainable **Solar Punk Utopia**. People lived well, but it was static/stagnant.
- **The Doom**: A meteor was on collision course (50 years out). Humanity was unaware/unprepared due to the suppression of innovation for stability.
- **The Visitor**: **God Ahr** was actually a **Photoli Traveler** exploring far from Galia. He saw two paths: let them die in peace vs force innovation/survival.

### 2. The Revelation (Vincenzo & Ahr)

- **The Meeting**: Ahr met **Vincenzo Assis Gotti** (a man of charity and innovation) incognito.
- **The Spark**: Vincenzo said *"While there is life, there is hope"*.
- **The Intervention**: Ahr revealed the coming doom and inspired Vincenzo to lead humanity.
- **The Strategy**: Force rapid innovation and unification to build an Ark.

### 3. The Schism (ECOS Truth)

- **The Opposition**: **ECOS** discovered Ahr was an alien.
- **The Rebellion**: They viewed Vincenzo's plan as **"Alien Domination"** and the resource extraction as the murder of Earth.
- **The Result**: Vincenzo had no choice but to unify humanity through a **Religious/Organizational System** (MUD) and consume Earth's resources to build the **Mothership Serene**.

### 4. The Exodus

- **~2120**: **Ahr visits planet Earth** — through illusion, humanity is tricked into forming a space-based religion, plundering the planet, and leaving permanently
- **~2140**: Humanity advances towards the stars; Planet Earth is left an empty husk
- **Guidance**: Ahr traveled with the MUD until they reached the **Exodus Sector** in Galia

### 5. The Expansion

- **~2399**: MUD finds the **Exodus Sector** in Galia Expanse and establishes their base
- **~2400**: **Last appearance of God (Ahr)** to the MUD — he leaves with the promise of returning once they find their true home
- **Settlement**: Mothership Serene became the heart of the empire
- **~2401**: **ECOS dissidence** — ECOS leaves towards Old Grove Sector
- **~2410**: MUD establishes first safe zone colonies — **Valiant-I, Aurora-II, Verzan-III, Amora-IV**
- **~2411**: The **Translucid Pox** wipes out 8% of MUD population before being contained on Valiant-I
- **~2411**: The **Pearce Council** is established on Amora-IV, becoming the bastion of MUD defenses
- **~2417**: **Pearce** is born on Amora-IV
- **~2426**: MUD expands into the Medium Zone — colonies: **Mycenas-V, Xianyang-VI, Redam-VII, Pergamos-VIII, Abyd-IX**
- **Aggressive Colonization**: Seized system after system, overthrowing local cultures. Expansion halted only by ONI/Ustur borders.

### 6. The Convergence War (Book I Era)

- **~2510**: Explorer **Gyun Oman** discovers the Cataclysm (limitless Genesium). He illegally **sells the data to all 3 rival factions** — MUD, ONI, and Ustur — each believing they are the sole recipients.
- **Reaction**: MUD sees this as the fulfillment of the *Revelation*.
- **Fracture**: Patriarch **Vincenzo Gotti** dies. His heir **Charon Gotti** inherits a divided empire.
- **Outcome**: MUD forces were undone by the war, retreating to their home sector (Exodus).
- **"Plan B"**: MUD leadership (High Council) has a secret contingency plan to ensure humankind thrives even if the Council of Peace collapses.

## Government & Structure

### The Holy Family (Gotti Lineage)

- **Status**: Divine right to rule. Descendants of Vincenzo are the only ones fit to guide MUD.
- **Succession**: Vincenzo -> Izek -> ... -> Charon Gotti Jr.

### Internal Factions (The Fracture)

The discovery of the Cataclysm turned MUD's unity into an arena:

1. **The Holy Servants (The Church)**: View Cataclysm as **Sacred Ground**. Demand consecrated claiming.
2. **The Pearce Council (Military/Colonial)**: View Cataclysm as **Strategic Singularity**. Seek control.
3. **Scientia (Scientists)**: View Cataclysm as **Existential Threat**. Urge caution.
4. **The Synod (Merchants)**: View Cataclysm as **Logistics & Trade**. Control shadows.
5. **The Senate**: Weakened political body pleading for restraint.

### Ex-Colonies (The Medium Zone)

- **Worlds**: Redam-VII, Mycenas-V, Abyd-IX, Pergamos-VIII, Xianyang-VI.
- **~2522**: All five colonies — **Redam, Mycenas, Xianyang, Pergamos, and Abyd** — **break out from MUD civilization**, starting their own independent governments in the post-Convergence War chaos.
- **Status**: Abandoned during the war. Now contested ground between MUD re-integration efforts and ECOS influence.

## Culture & Society

### Metahumans

- **Nature**: Individuals with **technological modifications** (meta-organs) for power/longevity.
- **Status**: Wealthy MUD often have some augmentation.
- **Symbolism**: Represents humanity's adaptability and relentless pursuit of improvement.

### Language: Divina Lingua

- Derived from ancient Earth languages.
- Rich in spiritual/philosophical terminology.
- **Dialects**: Outer worlds speak derivations seen as "subversive" by orthodox priests.
