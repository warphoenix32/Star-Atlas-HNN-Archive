- Docking and undocking your fleet in Starbased allows you to safely manage your assets, refuel, or prepare for your next mission.
- How to Dock Your Fleet Locate Your Fleet Your fleets are shown at the bottom of your screen.
- If hidden, click “Hide/Show My Fleets Overview” on the middle right to view them.
- Warp to a Starbase (If Needed) If your fleet is not already at a Starbase, warp or subwarp your fleet to your chosen Starbase location.
- Select “Dock” Click on your fleet, then choose “Dock” to safely station your fleet at the Starbase.
- Approve the Transaction Confirm the docking transaction in your wallet.
- How to Undock Your Fleet Go to Your Docked Fleet Locate your fleet at the Starbase where it is currently docked.
- Select “Undock” Click on your fleet, then choose “Undock” to send it back into space.
- Approve the Transaction Confirm the undocking transaction in your wallet.
