## Extracted article text

Welcome to our 154th newsletter on Star Atlas! This weekly newsletter, published by Aephia Industries, focuses entirely on the development of this ambitious game. Here, we try to aggregate all the newsworthy tidbits revealed [mainly by the team] throughout the past week.
Last week started off quietly, but everything changed when the team launched the much-anticipated Golden Carnival on Friday! This exciting, 16-week-long event easily took center stage, and as a result—and lack of other news— this week’s issue is almost entirely dedicated to all things Carnival!
Let’s dive in!
Star Atlas – Golden Carnival 2024 – Announcement Banner
Golden Carnival
The Golden Carnival 2624 is live! The team introduced the ticket crafting recipe this past Friday and published a comprehensive article to get people up to speed!
This second year, the event will last 16 weeks, from November 1, 2024, to February 21, 2025, with a weekly drawing each Friday. This means there will be double the number of drawings compared to last year.
So, here’s how it works in a nutshell: There are new Golden Ticket 2624 assets (SFTs) that can be crafted in-game in SAGE Labs: Starbased. For the next 16 weeks, every Friday (starting November 8th), a raffle will take place where those who submitted their tickets can win prizes from that week’s prize pool. However, it’s not just a single raffle. A drawing will take place that day for every prize in the pool. Of course, the more tickets you submit, the better your odds of winning!
2624 Changes
For this second edition, the team spruced things up a bit in two ways:
Loot size increased
Ship manufacturer focus
Mega weeks
We’ll discuss these further below, but let’s start with the Golden Tickets.
Golden Tickets
The team has introduced a new crafting recipe into SAGE Labs: Starbased, resulting in a Golden Ticket 2624 asset (remember that Star Atlas takes place 600 years into the future).
This recipe is only available at the Central Space Station of your Faction! The following materials are required to produce a single Golden Ticket:
Titanium
230 units
Toolkits
137 units
Framework
103 units
Electronics
82 units
Power Source
69 units
Electromagnet
51 units
Field Stabilizer
34 units
Radiation Absorber
26 units
Particle Accelerator
21 units
Star Atlas – Golden Ticket 2624 – Recipe
You can craft (and mine the related raw resources) these ingredients yourself, or you can acquire these at the Galactic Marketplace. The same goes, of course, for the Golden Tickets themselves. You can craft them, or you can buy them. If you craft them, instead of partaking in the raffles, you can also sell your tickets on the marketplace.
Submitting your Tickets
Like last year, the team has partnered with the folks from Hedgehog to facilitate the raffles. Later this week, you can submit tickets for the raffle that will take place that Friday. You will be able to submit tickets from the moment this platform goes live up to 5 minutes before the drawings begin.
Note that you can submit as many tickets as you want. Every ticket submitted is burned and can not be reclaimed (neither before nor after the raffle).
Starting with week 2, ticket submissions will open up shortly after the previous week concludes (likely on Monday).
Winning Prizes
The raffles will occur every Friday at 18:00 UTC (10 AM PT / 1 PM ET / 19:00 CET). Each drawing will start with the least expensive item, and every minute, a new raffle will take place for the next most valuable item on the loot table.
Update: The above was corrected November 11th as the previous description was wrong
Claiming Prizes
Any winnings must be claimed on the Hedgehog website after that week’s drawing has concluded. You will not automatically receive these!
Discord Event
The team will host a get-together on the Star Atlas Discord every Friday, starting 30 minutes before the drawings kick-off. Show up 30 minutes early for minigames that may result in you winning some Golden Tickets!
Loot Size
This year, the team added 4 million USDC in VWAP prizes to the total loot pool for this event! Note that VWAP means that this is the price at which the team sold/sells these items. It does not reflect the current (often much lower) prices you can find on the marketplace.
Ship Manufacturer Focus
Instead of adding a random mix of ships and assets to each drawing, the team now sorted these into pools containing ships of one or two manufacturers only. This allows people who’d like a chance to win ships from that manufacturer to consolidate their bets in the respective weeks.
There will be 3 Manufacturer-focused weeks in each 4-week cycle. The manufacturer focus for each of these will be, in order:
Pearce & Calico — These weeks have the most prizes due to the pool having been padded with 130 different crew packs (containing five crew each). Include the 69 ships, and you have a whopping 199 prizes that can be won! If you like to win anything, no matter what, these weeks are likely interesting for you!
Ogrika & Busan — An assortment of 91 Busan and Ogrika ships.
Fimbul — This includes the Fimbul, Fimbul BYOS, and Fimbul ECOS ships. The Fimbul BYOS Tankship is the big prize here! There are 99 ships in total and an additional 50 Silver (5) Crew Packs on top for 149 prizes that can be won.
Star Atlas – Golden Carnival 2024 – Schedule & Loot tables
Mega Weeks
There were a few weeks with more loot than others the first time around. This time, the team has made this very clear from the get-go, with clear labeling and visualization. These Mega Weeks will take place every 4th week. Just as important, these weeks will give double the loot of an ordinary week, which makes things very easy to reason about.
The Mega Weeks will all be unique. No two will be the same:
Mega Week 1 — The team has defined the loot table for this week (lower right on the picture above).
Mega Week 2 & 3 — The community gets to vote on the ships that will be made available for these two weeks, guaranteeing that these will be different from the other Mega Weeks
Mega Week 4 — A Mystery Mega Week where the team will surprise us with… something! You’ll have to wait and see. The team’s description reads: “the unpredictable grand finale, where you’ll have to trust the cosmic currents for the ultimate reward.“
