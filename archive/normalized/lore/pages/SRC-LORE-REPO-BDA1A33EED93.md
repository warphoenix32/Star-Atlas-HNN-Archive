## Overview

Frostsun is a sector locked in an **eternal wintry embrace**. Its star was **harvested by an ancient civilization** using crystal energy amplifiers — that civilization is gone, and the star is now in its **final centuries**. Below the frozen surface, **Profound Punaabs** have carved thriving underground metropolises, but they are not alone in the depths.

---

## The Star: Crystal-Farmed

### Ancient Civilization

- An ancient, now-destroyed civilization **"farmed" the sector's primary star**
- Used a **formation of crystals to amplify the star's energy output**
- The civilization was **destroyed** — their identity lost to time
- The star, **already consumed to a great extent**, is now in its **last centuries**
- The crystal formations remain, giving the dying star the appearance of a **golden-white orb cocooned in shimmering crystal**

### Environmental Impact

- The star's decay has cast a **chilly pall** over all planets
- Once-thriving ecosystems reduced to **frozen landscapes and desolate wastelands**
- Surface life is no longer viable on the main sector planets
- **Only subterranean life** is possible, sustained by **planetary core heat**

---

## The Profound Punaab Underground

### Colony Status

- **Formally a colony of Akenat** — the Punaab homeworld
- Loyal to the **ONI Consortium**, but if forced to choose, **would follow Akenat**
- The **preeminent Punaab enclave** outside their native Evernat

### Architecture

- Follows the **Profound Punaab aesthetic**: structures of **gold, bronze, and red**
- But the ecosystem is **very different from Akenat** — no fungal forests or geysers
- Underground cities sustained by **planetary core heat**
- Thriving metropolises beneath the ice

### Economy

- Produces and exports **vast quantities of food and minerals** to Akenat
- Critical supply chain for the Punaab homeworld
- Underground agriculture and mining operations

## The Lost Scouts & Lt. Wenn Saga

### The Mission

- During the **Convergence War**, a brave cadre from Frostsun ventured into the **HRZ**
- Became **scouts for ONI** — mapping the High-Risk Zone
- **~2518**: Frostsun sends **reinforcements to support the ONI faction** in the HRZ war. After a successful early campaign, they managed to **colonize the rich world of Gerio** in the HRZ

### The Silence

- **~2524**: The Punaabs of Frostsun **lose contact** with their brethren in Gerio after the chaos that follows the factions' retreat from the HRZ in the aftermath of the Convergence War
- **Five decades** without a word since
- Fate unknown to the Punaabs of Frostsun

### The Vigil

- **Active rescue operation** with concrete plans — not just passive hope
- They need **more information** to proceed
- This information arrives via the **Lt. Wenn Saga** *(storyline TBD)*
- The Punaabs **amass resources and hone readiness**, waiting for the intelligence they need
