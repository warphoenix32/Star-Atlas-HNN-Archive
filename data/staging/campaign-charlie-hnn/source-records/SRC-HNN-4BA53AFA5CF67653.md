In a recent turn of events, the moderators of the r/Solana and r/ Unrealengine5 subreddits removed Star Atlas content.
The posts in question contained recently released Star Atlas content video showcasing Star Atlas Unreal Engine 5 work in progress development.
Normally, Unreal Engine 5 developer Epic Games and Solana are huge proponents of Star Atlas. However, the removal of Star Atlas’s post may show the limits of their support.

Star Atlas Censored on Reddit for NFT Association
When asked why Star Atlas posts were removed from the Unreal Engine 5 channel, Star Atlas content managers were cited for being a project associated with NFTs by moderators. While Reddit itself does not prohibit the discussion of NFTs on its site, subreddits may impose stricter rules.
Since Unreal Engine 5 can apply to both traditional and blockchain games, it is reasonable for moderators to restrict discussion centered or associated with NFTs.
When asked for reasoning why Star Atlas posts were removed from the Solana channel, no comment was given. Star Atlas marketing managers were baffled by the position taken by Solana moderators.
Star Atlas and the Solana Foundation have enjoyed a close relationship in the past. The removal of Star Atlas content is a noticeable deviation in the normally warm partnership.
