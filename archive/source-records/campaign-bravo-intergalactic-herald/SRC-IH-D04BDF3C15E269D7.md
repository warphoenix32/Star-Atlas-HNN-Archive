The Zink Alpha Season is now live, running for approximately six weeks, marking a pivotal moment for the Star Atlas ecosystem. Participants can earn Zink XP through various in-game activities such as playing SAGE and HoloSim, purchasing from the primary marketplace, and completing matches in Unreal Engine 5. Locking ATLAS and POLIS tokens also contributes to daily XP accrual, with Epic Games account activity updating every 2-4 hours, while other sources are near real-time. Note that all economic parameters are under active review and may be subject to change.
