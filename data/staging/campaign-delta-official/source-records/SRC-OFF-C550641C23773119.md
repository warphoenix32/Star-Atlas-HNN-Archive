## Preserved Official Text

Crew are essential characters in Star Atlas that serve both strategic and immersive roles across multiple gameplay experiences. Whether you're managing fleets in SAGE Labs or exploring the galaxy in the UE5 game, Crew are a core part of your operations
In SAGE Labs (Browser Game)
Required for Fleet Deployment: Every ship has a crew requirement. You’ll need the correct number of Crew to operate a fleet.
Used in Operation: Assign Crew to crafting tasks. The more Crew you allocate, the faster the crafting process completes.
In the MMORPG UE5 Game (Epic Games)
Play as Your Crew: In the 3D Star Atlas experience, Crew members can be used as your playable avatar, adding personalization and immersion as you explore the galaxy.
How to Get Crew
1. Crew Packs on the Marketplace
Buy Crew Packs directly on the Star Atlas Marketplace using ATLAS or USDC. Once purchased, open them on the PLAY site to reveal the individual Crew inside.
2. Buy Individual Crew on Secondary Market
If you prefer to pick specific Crew, you can trade individual Crew directly on Tensor, a secondary marketplace for Solana NFTs.
