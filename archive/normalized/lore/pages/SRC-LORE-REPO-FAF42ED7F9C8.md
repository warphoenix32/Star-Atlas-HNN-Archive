## Biology & Nature

- **Age**: Ancient and enigmatic species, often considered the **original conscious life-forms of the universe**.
- **Appearance**: Unique beings of light with **partially physical bodies**. No two look the same.
- **Accessories**: Do not use clothing/accessories (ancient nature renders them unnecessary).
- **Mastery**: Unparalleled mastery over physics, time, energy, and light.

### Visual DNA (from Concept Art)

- **Humanoid silhouette** with extended proportions — elongated limbs, towering physique
- **Translucent marble-like skin** — grey/white with luminous white veins running like organic circuits
- **Internal light** — glow emanates from within, tracing lines across the body
- **Featureless head** — angular, almost helmet-like cranial structure, no visible eyes/mouth
- **Geometric/runic glyphs** float around their bodies — circles, arcs, cosmic symbols — like an aura of mathematics
- **No clothing** — the body itself IS the presentation; sculpted like living marble with inner radiance
- **Muscular but ethereal** — simultaneously physical and intangible

### Light-Shaping (Shape-Shifting)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Photoli can **change their appearance by molding their light**. This explains how Om visits the Lumikir incognito — he reshapes his luminous form to appear different or diminished.

- They can alter their visual form by reshaping the light they emit and the structure of their partially-physical body
- This ability ranges from subtle alterations to potentially full disguise
- Enables Photoli like Om to move unrecognized when needed

## Society & Hierarchy

Photoli society lacks a central government. They are highly self-sufficient.

### Two Tiers

1. **Sih Rihim** (The Ascended): Beings of pure light with immense power and wisdom.
2. **Alumni** (The Students): Younger Photoli striving to master elements for transformation into pure light.

### The Travelers (Sub-faction)

- Reject the pursuit of ascension and strict orthodoxy.
- **Role**: Observers who find joy in subtly influencing other species' growth (without direct interference).
- **Philosophy**: Distance themselves from Ascended/Alumni machinations to avoid harm.
- **Action**: Might inadvertently accelerate a civilization's progress by centuries.

---

## Essence Trading & Governance

- **System**: Identify power/voting through the sacrifice of their own life force (**Life Essence**).
- **Awareness**: Every Photoli knows exactly when they will die (dissipate into natural light).
- **Voting**: Collective decisions are settled by pouring essence into a **rayanite crystal** during an **Iris Majesty cycle**.
  - A single Photoli can influence weighty decisions if willing to sacrifice significant life span.
- **Dispute Resolution**: Settled by essence transfer.

### Prohibition of Conflict

- **Rule**: Direct conflict between Photoli is **strictly forbidden** (Andromeda legacy).
- **The ONI Paradox**: They justify ONI membership as a means to an end. Startling revelation: **A relevant faction of Photoli has a hidden agenda** — to use the ONI's expansion to eventually acquire access to **Iris** (eternal energy source). They are playing the "long game".

---

## Dark Photoli (The Exiles)

- **Nature**: Predatory entities that grow stronger the longer they survive.
- **Why they don't unite**: Arrogance and paranoia. They fear being drained by each other.
- **Weakness**: Born weaker than traditional Photoli (due to lack of natural light), so they cannot yet openly challenge the Photoli/Galia.
- **Status**: 7 known individuals, functioning as dangerous loners/outcasts.
- **~2558**: A **powerful Photoli turns dark** and begins plotting the **conquest of Iris** for himself. He later strikes a deal with Ingbus (`canon/geography/sectors/ka_dara.md`) (~2569)

---

## The Ritual of Ascension

- **Process**: For other species, it is a **gradual transmutation**.
  - Requires immense resources, time, compatibility, and luck.
  - The individual slowly gains Photoli properties and powers.
- **Mechanics**:
  1. Willing/sentient candidate.
  2. Essence of Pure Light + Energy Source + Catalyst.
  3. Chant/Ritual binding.

## Locations

  - **Clarification**: Located in the HRZ, but **NOT inside the Cataclysm**.
  - Iris denies them entry to the Cataclysm proper.

## History

- **~2080**: Photoli **cross into Galia** through the **Night Window Sector**, draining the star's energy to recharge after their journey from Andromeda.
- **~2086**: Photoli **Om** is saved by primitive creatures in MRZ-27 — he grants them knowledge, and they rename themselves the **Lumikir**.
- **~2095**: Photoli inter the **Ah'Rihvael** family — an ancient Photoli family whose time was over — beneath the oceans of **Bluvael's** six habitable worlds. They cast an enormous sector-wide illusion to hide the tombs (the **Drowned Vaults**).
- **~2120**: Photoli **Ahr** visits **Planet Earth**, using illusion to manipulate humanity into forming a space-based religion and leaving Earth permanently.
- **~2135**: Photoli discover a powerful creature (the **"Dreamer Below"**) in the heart of planet **Etira** (Yuldun Waste, MRZ-32). Unable to destroy it, they **steal its memories**, seal it in a subterranean pool, and **terraform Etira's atmosphere** to be lethally hostile — preventing anyone from reaching it.
- **~2136**: Photoli drain Night Window's star energy and construct **Dawnbreak** — a hidden fortress in the HRZ.
- **Convergence War**: Joined **ONI Consortium** as "witnesses and wardens".
  - **Public Goal**: Guidance and preservation of life (Du Prah support).
  - **Hidden Agenda**: Positioning themselves to eventually access Iris/Energy.
  - **The Titan Secret**: A disguised Photoli taught **House Garveil** how to build the Titan's power source in the HRZ.
