Exclusive FTX and Raydium Spaceship Skin NFTs Drop to Commemorate Record-Shattering Token Listing

Star Atlas

14 hours ago·4 min read

The unique FTX ship skins, authenticated by FTX, are available on the FTX NFT Marketplace as epic limited NFTs. The epic Raydium ship skin, authenticated by Raydium, will be made available to those providing liquidity on the platform.

To immortalize the ground-breaking listing of Star Atlas (ATLAS) and Star Atlas DAO (POLIS) tokens on the FTX exchange (IEO) and Raydium (IDO) in September 2021, Star Atlas is releasing ultra rare spaceship skins into the metacosmos.

Metaverse and blockchain fans as well as NFT collectors can obtain the four available skins in two ways.

Raydium Skin

To be eligible to receive the Raydium skin, a user must provide liquidity and stake a minimum of 0.01 LP Tokens in the ATLAS-RAY Fusion pool or 0.0001 LP tokens in the POLIS-RAY Fusion pool. These LP token amounts were equal to approximately $2,000 of liquidity (including both sides of LP) at the time of writing. The LP tokens need to remain staked for 10 days between September 11, 20:00 UTC (1 PM PDT) and September 21, 2021 at 20:00 UTC (1 PM PDT). Users that meet these criteria will unlock and be eligible to receive one Raydium skin per pool, and up to two maximum if staking the minimum amount in both pools. The skins will be airdrops to wallets of qualifying liquidity providers.

Note: While the dollar value of LP tokens may fluctuate with price, any user staking the minimum number of LP tokens for the stated period will be eligible. Raydium skins will be directly airdropped to eligible wallets.

FTX Skins

The way to obtain the FTX skins listed below is by purchasing them directly on the FTX market at an initial price of $88 per skin. The skins are available in a limited quantity of 500 units each, and will stay on the secondary FTX market. They will be accessible to their owners on the Solana blockchain in the weeks following the end of the sale.

Exclusive FTX & Raydium Spaceship Skins

FTX NFT #1: FTX Beyond

For those who like originals, this exclusive Opal Jetjet classic skin extends the signature FTX look and feel beyond the limits of reality. The flashy accent will make sure that the eyeballs keep on turning towards you even when you’re somewhere deep in outer space…

Get it on FTX.

FTX NFT #2: FTX Night

This bold nocturnal look puts the exclusive FTX Night Opal Jetjet skin into a league of its own. It allows your personality to shine through while making a clear statement about your favorite exchange.

Get in on FTX.

FTX NFT #3: FTX Moon

The third jubilatory skin design merges the excitement of deep space exploration with the exhilarating success of your trading strategy. Decked in the same colors as the glowing lunar surface, this exclusive Opal Jetjet skin will set your mood just right for an exciting, bright future ahead.

Get it on FTX.

RAYDIUM NFT: Radium Defy

Here comes a real show-stopper. The most exclusive Pearce X5 Skin glows in the colors of Raydium itself. Its defiantly energetic design affirms your spaceship fly-through on the final frontier as playful and thrill-seeking. This skin won’t just make you stand out in the crowd, it will make the crowd stand apart for you.

Provide liquidity on Raydium to qualify for this this NFT.
