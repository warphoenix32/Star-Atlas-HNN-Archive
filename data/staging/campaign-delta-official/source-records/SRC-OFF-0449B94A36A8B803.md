## Preserved Official Text

zATLAS is the main in-game currency of Holosim. Almost every action in the game requires a small amount of zATLAS, making it one of the most important resources to manage.
How to earn zATLAS:
Complete quests: Tutorial, Storyline, Expertise, and Daily quests reward zATLAS.
Sell contracts: crafting and selling contracts is a steady way to generate income.
Daily check-in: logging in daily grants zATLAS, with larger bonuses if you own a Battle Pass.
What zATLAS is used for:
Operating fleets (self-destruct).
Crafting ships, components, and other items.
Marketplace, buying and selling items with other players or NPCs.
