Star Atlas Economic Forum-December 2025 from Star Atlas TV – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BMnuFdop94E
The Star Atlas Econ Forum introduced a new “Open Market Operations” (OMO) proposal, a three-part plan designed to restructure the game’s economy for long-term growth and stability. Automata will strategically sell existing ship inventory for USDC at prices above current market values, generating revenue without further diluting the market, with sales quantities carefully limited based on market health. Conversely, Automata will use ATLAS from the emission curve to purchase ships from players, which will then be permanently burnt to reduce overall supply and bolster secondary market prices. Crucially, USDC revenue generated from these sales will be used to offset ATLAS/POLIS token liquidations on DEXes. Once a set revenue threshold is met, 30% of additional OMO revenue will be allocated to ATLAS buybacks. The proposal also includes introducing new craftable ship models in SAGE with ATLAS buy orders, revamping existing Fimbul Mamba Ex and Calico Maxhog recipes, and initiating a 60-day period of 0% marketplace fees starting in December. This comprehensive strategy aims to create a positive economic feedback loop, supporting player growth, Zink utility, and asset values, while preparing Automata for sustained revenue generation during favorable market conditions. This is a proposal open to community feedback, emphasizing its role in setting the stage for future prosperity rather than a sudden, magical fix.
