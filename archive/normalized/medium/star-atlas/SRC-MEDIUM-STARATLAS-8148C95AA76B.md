## Preserved Article

From December 1–4, Star Atlas will inspire the art and music festival produced by Abracadabra, taking over Art Basel week in Wynwood Miami, Florida, and launching FTX Pay integration in the metaverse.

*Star Atlas at Miami Art Week in Florida /// Next-level NFT, Music and Metaverse Experiences /// December 1–4, 2021*

During the Miami Art Week, Star Atlas is curating one of the largest social in-game experiences for gamers globally as a co-sponsor for the highly anticipated music event in unison with head sponsor FTX US, and unveiling Galactic Marketplace integration with FTX Pay.

During Miami’s art week, Wynwood Studios , the first ever NFT Art Party Exhibition, will present a four-day experience bringing together music, art, & crypto technology in the heart of Miami’s art district. Each night will showcase major musical performances, an NFT Art experience, and exclusive NFT drops, all presented by unique brands aiming to bridge the worlds of music, crypto and art. Powered by the new FTX NFT Marketplace , one of the world’s largest art events will also facilitate the launch of commemorative Star Atlas metaverse NFTs featuring soundscapes by BLOND:ISH for event participants.


## The Star Atlas Program Activities

At the historic week-long event, Star Atlas is hosting a lineup of activities, including bespoke installations featuring the next-generation gaming metaverse, emerging from the confluence of state of the art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video game, and decentralized financial technologies.

Also on the program are a Star Atlas Live Twitch stream on Wednesday at 6 PM EST, a geo-tagged AR moment on the Toe Jam stage, the Star Atlas Alien AR Face Filter and a hands-on experience with Star Atlas Arcade terminals at Abracadabra. All these experiences and more are in store for the participants in Miami.

The program will feature top worldwide artists, including BLOND:ISH and Seth Troxler on Wednesday (Abracadabra), Damian Lazarus and Nic Fanciulli on Thursday (We Art the Future), Diplo on Friday (Higher Ground), and Guy Gerber and Kaz James on Saturday (Hurry Up Slowly).

For the very first time, Star Atlas will also be unveiling futuristic visual experiences with its Transcendence moving artwork, created with next-gen technology that was until recently inaccessible to humans.


## The Abracadabra Giveaway

For those in the Star Atlas community who were not quick or lucky enough to have gotten their own entry pass, Star Atlas has offered 10 general admission tickets worth a total of $4000 for the Abracadabra event. Participation in the online giveaway gave everyone a fair chance to enter the contest and win a ticket of their own, opening the doors to an unforgettable metaverse experience in Miami, Florida.
