## Preserved Official Text

Star Atlas is a next-gen space metaverse built on the Solana blockchain. Whether you're here to explore, trade, or earn, this guide shows you exactly how to get started, from jumping into the free UE5 game to setting up your fleet in SAGE
Start with the Free-to-Play UE5 Game
The fastest way to jump into Star Atlas is through the UE5 Game, available on the Epic Games Store. It's free to play, and you don’t need to own any NFTs to start exploring.
What You Can Do:
Fly demo ships or your own ships
Use crew avatars to explore
Play solo or multiplayer modes: combat, racing, zone control & more
Mine asteroids in the open world
Race or explore Volant Station, the MUD faction’s central hub
How to Join:
Join the Star Atlas Discorddiscord.gg/staratlas
Go to the
🔑┃game-access-key
channel (under “Start Here”)

/key
commandA bot will send you your unique Epic Games key.
Redeem the Key on Epic Games Launcher
Open Epic Games Launcher
Log in or create an account
Go to your name → "Redeem Code"
Enter your game key
Play-to-Earn with SAGE (Browser Game)
SAGE is Star Atlas’ browser-based strategy game built entirely on the Solana blockchain. It’s a true play-to-earn experience, where you can generate value by exploring the galaxy, mining resources, crafting items, and managing fleets all through on-chain actions
Here’s what makes SAGE unique:
Explore space and discover valuable regions of the Galia Expanse
Mine and extract resources like Ore, Carbon, Hydrogen, and more
Craft materials and components for use in future gameplay
Earn ATLAS and resources through efficient fleet and economy management
List your fleet for rent and earn passive income from other players
To get started in SAGE, you’ll need:
A Solana wallet (e.g. Phantom)
A small amount of SOL for transaction fees
ATLAS to buy your first assets on the Star Atlas Marketplace
A Ship, a Crew member, and R4 resources (Fuel, Food, Ammo, Toolkits)
You can also rent a fleet from other players using Fleet Rental if you don’t want to buy ships right away
Other Core Features of Star Atlas
Fleet Rental
Create a fleet in SAGE and rent it to other players on the Fleet Rental platform for passive income
Free-to-Play Holosim Browser Game
Holosim is Star Atlas’ Free-to-Play browser-based strategy game built entirely on the Atlasnet blockchain (Solana Fork)
Galactic Marketplace
Buy and sell ships, crew, resources, claim stakes, collectibles, and more
Use ATLAS or USDC to transact
Faction Claim
Stake Claim Stakes to extract R4 resources (Fuel, Food, Ammo, Toolkits) over time. Great for passive income and resource accumulation
DACs (Guilds)
Join or create a Decentralized Autonomous Corporation (DAC) to play with others, form alliances, and take on big challengesplay.staratlas.com/dac
Star Atlas DAO
Lock POLIS to gain voting power (PVP) and participate in decentralized proposals that shape the metaverse
Lock POLIS or ATLAS to earn daily POLIS rewards (higher with POLIS)
Lock ATLAS to reduce fees when trading on the Galactic Marketplace and SAGE’s Local Marketplace
