## Overview

**Opal** is the galaxy's youngest manufacturer, forged from a remarkable synthesis of **MUD firepower** and **flawless Ustur engineering**.

- **~2558**: Opal Industries is **founded** in the **Azut sector** from a collaboration between Ustur and MUD

## Key Facts

- **Visual Identity**: Distinctive **marine blue** finishes.
- **Design Philosophy**: Ships are as deadly as they are enchanting — blending ancient MUD aesthetics with Ustur resilience.
- **Inspiration**: Designs channel the grandeur of bygone eras, invoking spirits of ancient relics and breathing life into likenesses of **extinct creatures**.
- **Balance**: Lethal armaments (MUD martial wisdom) + unshakable structural integrity (Ustur craftsmanship).

## Infrastructure

- **Opal Stream**: Massive orbital solar-powered infrastructure in Eternity Sector powering the Starpath gate.
- **Doers Harbor**: Principal port on Ioki, only one with long-distance warp infrastructure.
- **HQ**: Azut Sector (Francor Metropolis).

## Season 0 Impact

- Tufa converged on Opal Stream first, shutting down the Ioki-Galia connection and crippling the Ustur fleet.
- Reclaiming Opal Stream is the **last grand objective** — allows reinforcements.
