## Overview

The Yuldun Waste, to the uninitiated, appears to be a **barren expanse** within Galia. Devoid of bustling life, its planets are treacherous — either **aggressively hostile to life** or shrouded in **tumultuous atmospheric tempests** that deter even the most seasoned captains from daring a descent.

Yet while its surface seems vacant, the sector isn't entirely desolate. Some planets sustain **legitimate economic activity and normal populations** under **Ustur domain**, while the criminal underworld operates in the shadows alongside them.

---

## Environment

### Planetary Conditions

- **No habitable worlds** — all planets are either actively hostile or storm-shrouded
- Atmospheric tempests prevent safe landing on most surfaces
- Appears barren to casual observation — this is part of its value

### The Ancient Station

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Suspended amidst the cosmic void is an **ancient space station of unknown origin**. Its origins are lost to time. It has been repurposed into a **clandestine refuge**.

- **~2496**: Criminals from different species begin gathering in MRZ-32 — they take hold of the ancient decrepit space station and make it a **refuge for criminals**
- Origins **lost to time** — no one knows who built it or why
- Repurposed into a haven for **outlaws, fugitives, and shadow operators**
- Within its shadowed corridors and dimly lit chambers: **dark bargains, plots, and illegal salvage yards**
- Everything happens beyond the prying eyes of galactic authorities

---

## Economy & Culture

### Legitimate Economy

- Some planets in the sector have **normal populations** conducting legitimate economic activity
- These worlds follow **Ustur domain** governance
- The sector is not purely criminal — it has a dual nature

### Shadow Economy (BMAH-Coordinated)

- The criminal operations and illicit locations are **coordinated by the Black Market Auction House (BMAH)**
- The broader galactic economy overlooks this sector
- Those in the know understand: the currency of **secrets and shadows** is sometimes more valuable than any mineral wealth
- Clandestine trade, black market goods, and information brokering

### Illegal Salvage Yards

- Extremely popular in this region
- Everything that arrives is **disassembled and reassembled** for resale
- Stolen ships, war debris, contraband tech — all broken down and rebuilt into untraceable goods

### The Price of Sanctuary

- For those desperate enough — whether to hide or to hunt — Yuldun Waste offers sanctuary
- But **always at a price**
- The station operates on its own rules, outside galactic law

### Planet Etira — Former BMAH Capital

- **Etira** was the **last capital of the Black Market Auction House (BMAH)** — destroyed during the Never Alone incident (~2624)
- Atmosphere was **terraformed by the Photoli (~2135)** to be lethally hostile — originally to seal the **"Dreamer Below"**, a powerful creature imprisoned in its core
- The **Dreamer Below is now dead** — destroyed during Never Alone. Its corpse was **looted by the Dark Photoli Bah**, who retrieved a silver gem from within
- The **COP invaded Etira** post-Never Alone, destroying the BMAH's subsurface bunkers
- The Church of the Dreamer Below — a memory-stealing cult founded by **Herdus.soul** — is **destroyed**
- Etira is now effectively **abandoned**
- Full details: `canon/geography/worlds/etira.md`
