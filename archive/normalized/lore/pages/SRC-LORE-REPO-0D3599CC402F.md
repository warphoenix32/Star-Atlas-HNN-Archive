## Overview

Everstorm stands as a testament to the profound power of Galia's galactic ecosystem. The sector is **drenched in cosmic currents emanating from Iris**, inciting its local star into a tumultuous dance of relentless solar flares and coronal mass ejections. The legacy of distant Iris will forever shape its destiny.

---

## The Storms

### Iris-Driven Phenomenon

- **Cosmic currents from Iris** agitate the sector's star into constant instability
- Relentless **solar flares** and **coronal mass ejections** bombard the sector
- Planets develop **robust magnetospheres** as a natural defense
- Result: every habitable world is cloaked in **perpetual electrifying storms**
- The Iris currents are in **constant motion** throughout the system
- Without proper knowledge and technology, flying here is **extremely dangerous**

### Atmospheric Bounty

- The atmospheric upheaval creates environments abundant in **rare Galia resources**
- These resources form the economic foundation of the sector
- Extractable only by those who can survive — or harness — the storms

## The Nimbus Spheres

### Origin

- **~2487**: **Fimbul Engineers from Free Harbors** discover the unique sector Everstorm in MRZ-25
- Upon seeing the non-stop electrical storms, they decide on researching ways of **harnessing the potential endless energy** of the sector
- **Fimbul = neutral manufacturer, native to the MRZ** (not faction-aligned, founded ~2471 in Free Harbors)
- Driven by the potential concealed within the chaos

### Technology

- **~2504**: After many attempts, Fimbul engineers finally develop **ionic shields** capable of withstanding the tempest of Everstorm — the foundational breakthrough enabling permanent habitation
- **~2509**: Fimbul Engineers create the **Sky Fortress** — the **first and largest Nimbus Sphere**, a floating city sustained by the force of Everstorm's tempest. However, the sector **stops receiving support from Free Harbors** due to rising tensions that will lead to the Convergence War
- **~2513**: The Sky Fortress manages to survive the Convergence War in **relative peace**, shielded by the sector's tempests. They begin **exporting energy cells to Free Harbors** as a means of sustenance
- Created the **Nimbus Spheres** — their ingenious solution to survival (built upon the ionic shield technology)
- **9 city-sized Nimbus Spheres** spread throughout the sector (the Sky Fortress being the first and largest)
- Serve as **bastions against the storm's fury**
- Ingeniously **harness the storm's energy** — transforming adversity into sustainability
- Protective bubble-cities that shield settlements while generating power from the storms

### The Democratic Oasis (Functional)

- The Fimbul engineers' ambition: a **democratic oasis** within the spheres
- A sanctuary **untouched by external forces and influences**
- **Already functioning** in the current canon timeline
- 9 self-governing city-spheres — a confederation of storm-sheltered democracies

### The New Threat (~2624)

- **~2624**: New **strange and hostile life forms** start appearing in Everstorm
- The local government begins **hiring captains and crews** to dispose of them
- These creatures are a **species brought by the Dark Photoli** to the MRZ as an **experiment**, and **let loose in Everstorm**

## Navigation Hazard

> [!WARNING]
> The Iris currents are in constant motion. Without specialized knowledge and technology, flying in Everstorm is extremely risky.

- The storms and Iris currents shift unpredictably
- Only experienced pilots with storm-navigation tech can traverse the sector safely
- Nimbus Spheres serve as safe harbors between dangerous transit routes
- A major contributor to the MRZ classification
