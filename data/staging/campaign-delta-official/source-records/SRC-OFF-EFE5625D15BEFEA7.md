Star Atlas: Powered by the People ​
This is the community logo for creators building with Star Atlas IP Use it proudly to show your work is part of the Powered by the People initiative
Why Use This Logo? ​
This badge tells the world your project is built with love for the Star Atlas universe and that it follows the Terms of Service You’ll need to include it on your product, website, or marketing if you’re building with our IP
You may not use the official "Star Atlas" logo or wordmark, those are reserved trademarks For example: 🚫 “Star Atlas Bedsheets” → Not allowed ✅ “Punaab Bedsheets” → Totally fine
Customization Guidelines ​
You can recolor the triangle and box behind “Powered by the People” to match your style, just keep the two shapes the same color If you want to edit the logo in other ways (resizing, animation, stylizing), please reach out first at legal@staratlas.com
Not Feasible to Use the Logo? ​
If there’s a reason you can’t include the logo in your final product (e.g. limited space or print constraints), just let us know. We’ll find a solution.
