## Overview

**Akalma Exile** is a somber shadow of its former glory — the sector where the disgraced **House of Akalma** (Sogmian) was banished following their failed coup during the **Vega Fall** upheaval.

## Key Lore

### The Exile

- **House of Akalma**: A Sogmian House banished after their failed coup during Vega Fall.
- Lingered in isolation for cycles, their sector silent save for internal murmurs.

### Bekalu's Vengeance

- **~2523-01**: Following the **assassination of Paizul**, the **House of Akalma was framed** to be involved — they were the most convenient **scapegoat** for the Sogmian wrath, and **fabricated evidence** of their participation was created
- Despite being **genuinely innocent** of the assassination, Akalma could not escape the manufactured narrative
- **Bekalu** marshaled the Sogmian armada with the titan ship **"The Last Stand"** at the vanguard
- **Devastated the entire sector** in retribution

### The Reckoning

- As fires dimmed, a gut-wrenching realization: the Sogmians had **mirrored House Akalma's sin** — succumbing to unchecked emotions.
- **Bekalu's last-minute mercy**: Allowed a handful of Akalma fleets to retreat into the **High-Risk Zone**.
- The once-mighty House of Akalma **faded into obscurity**.

### The Dynasty Endures (HRZ)

- Despite their exile, House Akalma **retained its dynastic structure** deep in the High Risk Zone
- **~2600**: **Jefos**, prince of House Akalma, is **born in the HRZ**. At the moment of his birth, the **Cosmic Currents** release an **intense storm** — a supernatural phenomenon that marks his arrival
- **~2621**: Jefos, with a crew of other **young Akalma Sogmians**, **leaves the safe haven** in the High-Risk Zone and ventures into the **Medium Zone**, looking for a way to **redeem House Akalma**

### The Edict and the Haunting

- A **self-imposed Sogmian edict** forbids their kind from ever setting foot in the Akalma Expanse again.
- **MUD** filled the power vacuum, extending influence over the territory.
- The sector is littered with **ruins and ancient burial grounds**.
- Rare gems dot the landscape, but miners tread cautiously.
- **The Curse (LITERAL)**: When cosmic currents surge, **mournful cries of departed Sogmians** literally echo through the vastness — a **supernatural phenomenon** enabled by the cosmic currents (manifestations of Iris's mind). Not legend, not metaphor.
