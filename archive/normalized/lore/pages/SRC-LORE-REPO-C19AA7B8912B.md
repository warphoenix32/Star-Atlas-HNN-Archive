## Overview

Glimmering at the edge of the known Galia Expanse, **Ophek Oasis** is the ultimate interstellar **Sin Sector** — hedonism meets high stakes, with thematic pleasure planets.

## Key Lore

### Ophek Revel — The Maestro of Mirage

- **Species**: Human.
- **Title**: "Merchant Prince" — a recognized title in Galia for exceptional merchants. Open to all species, but **most commonly pursued by High Punaabs**.
- **Convergence War Role**: Daring freighter + the most sought-after **information broker**. **~2513**: Begins trading **HRZ information** during the war.
- **~2522**: **Retires from information trading** and sets out to build his vision of the perfect intergalactic Casino. After months of research, he decides to settle in **MRZ-8**.
- **~2542**: Civil war breaks out in MRZ-8, but with an **outstanding diplomatic play**, Ophek stays out of the conflict. Once all the other sides are exhausted, he **seizes power in the sector**.
- **~2545**: Ophek renames the sector **"Ophek Oasis"** and, using the fortune he acquired during the Convergence War, creates a **peaceful oasis** amidst the post-war chaos.
- **Vision**: Used war-amassed wealth to sculpt an unparalleled **pleasure dome** transcending all known luxury.
- **Skills**: Sharp-minded steward, diplomat par excellence, silver-tongued persuader.
- **Autonomy**: Bought political independence through **bribes and influence trafficking**. Has deep connections across factions.

### The Oasis

- **Thematic planets**: Each dedicated to different forms of indulgence.
- **Strategic remoteness**: Untouched by major galactic powers.
- **~2571**: After much **bribery, diplomacy, and favors**, Ophek Oasis is formally **recognized as an independent territory** by the **Council of Peace (COP)**
- **Economy**: Uses **ATLAS** as base currency. Casino operates with **proprietary chips convertible to ATLAS**.
- **Clientele**: Weary souls escaping Faction life + thrill-seekers.
- **~2602**: Ophek Oasis becomes the **biggest "entertainment" sector in the galaxy** — its reputation as the ultimate destination for indulgence is unmatched
- **~2624**: **Ophek Revel dies**. His **children inherit control** of the Oasis — each of them rules a **different form of entertainment** in the sector

- **Protection**: Remoteness + information leverage + political bribes

## Lore Note: Merchant Prince Title

> The title "Merchant Prince" is a galactic recognition of exceptional mercantile achievement. While any species can earn it, **High Punaabs** most commonly pursue it due to their natural mercantile culture. Ophek Revel is among the few humans to hold it.
