*Don’t you wish your jetjet was hot like mine?*

If you can make your way to the shores of the ancient MUD dominion known as Miami Beach this weekend, you will feel the buzz and excitement that can only come from race weekend and FTX Off the Grid.

This three-day festival, brought to you by FTX with event partner Star Atlas, is an ode to crypto, culture, and the blistering speed of the present and future. The best part for our visitors from across the galaxy: daytime entry is free!

The event will take place at 10th Street and the beachfront, directly on the sands of South Beach, just beyond the iconic, pastel-hued Art Deco buildings lining Ocean Drive. There you will find 165,000 square feet of oceanfront space where the crypto and natural worlds will meet.

When you arrive, be sure to check out the Star Atlas Video Game Lounge, where our retro arcades will give you a chance to relive gaming experiences of the past while our metaverse previews will offer a taste of the future.

Members of the Star Atlas team will be offering fantastic merch giveaways and much more!

You can expect a variety of attractions, from the Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 car on Ocean Drive to DJs keeping the party going non-stop. After dark, the party continues with a three-night ticketed concert series called ‘Sunset on the Sand’ with special performances from Disclosure, Kaytranada, and Jamie XX.

The event will occur from 16:00–21:30 (UTC) Friday — Sunday. If you can’t attend in person, be sure to check out the Star Atlas livestream on Twitch , taking place Friday and Saturday from 18:00–21:00 (UTC) / 2PM — 5PM (ET).

*Sand, heat, cars, and speed in a three-day festival directly on the legendary Miami Beach, and you, explorer, are Star Atlas guests to enjoy everything this epic event has to offer.*

Other events on tap include:

FTX NFT Art Galleries & Creation Stations | Connecting fans to the FTX and blockchain ecosystem, guests will have unrivaled access to create, discover, and purchase NFTs throughout the event. The event also features a collection of unique one-of-one NFTs for purchase by a local artist. Guests can also create their own NFTs, which can be minted and pressed onto swag on-site for an unforgettable takeaway.

IWC Big Pilot Exhibition | IWC, the official timekeeper of FTX Off The Grid Miami Beach, will participate in the event with the return of their Big Pilot Exhibition, alongside an exclusive IWC lounge where they will entertain guests partners, and friends of the brand.

Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Garage I Integrating the Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team into the festival’s heart, fans will get the chance to get up close and personal with the team’s F1 car.

Motorsport Games | Guests can test their on-track driving skills in the unbelievably realistic racing simulators from Motorsport Games. Featuring the popular racing game, rFactor2 guests will have the opportunity to go head-to-head with their friends on an iconic global racetrack.

FTX Off The Grid will offer guests free access during the day and ticketed concert performances by night . Register for an exclusive NFT ticket and find more information on the line-up, activities, and schedule at www.ftxoffthegrid.co m .
