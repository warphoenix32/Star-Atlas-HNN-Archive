Star Atlas Guilds Shutting Down
The original and popular Star Atlas Guilds website is shutting down, an inadvertent result of the new Star Atlas Intellectual Property policy. The rollout of the Star Atlas IP policy is intended to open up opportunities to the Star Atlas community to develop with and around the Star Atlas intellectual property framework.
However, a stipulation of the policy is that the words “Star” and “Atlas” cannot be used conjunctively in a project’s name, including it’s website domain address.
Hence, the problem for Star Atlas Guilds lies in both its namesake and web address.
Upon further inquiry by Hologram News, it was determined that Star Atlas Guilds was directed to change its name to conform with Star Atlas IP policy or to cease operations.
Star Atlas Guilds is managed and run by Star Atlas community member Dodger. In our brief discussion with Dodger, they mentioned that currently there are no plans to either change the service’s name or transfer management to another party.
A Focal Point of Guilds
Star Atlas Guilds is a popular website used by Star Atlas players to search, learn, and connect with the many Star Atlas guilds. Star Atlas Guilds has been serving the community for a significant period of time. Its closure will leave a service capability gap in the community until a similar service is provided.
On the website, players can search for guilds based on a number of criteria including faction allegiance and gameplay activity. Each guild description provided substantial information about a guild along with multiple points of contact.
It is unknown if there are plans to preserve the valuable content contained within the website.
