## Classified Claims

- Quests in Holosim are the main way to learn the game and progress your character.
- Starting them is simple and guided from the very beginning.
- Steps to start a quest: Look for the Quests icon: you’ll find it in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
- When a new quest is available, it will glow to catch your attention.
- Open the Quests menu: click the icon to see the available questlines: Tutorial, Storyline, Expertise, and Daily.
- Select a quest: choose the quest you want to begin and click on it for details.
- Follow the objectives: each quest will explain what you need to do, such as forming a fleet, mining resources, or completing combat actions.
- Claim your rewards: once you finish the objectives, return to the Quests menu and click “Claim Rewards” to receive your XP, crew, or zATLAS.
- Starting quests is the fastest way to get familiar with Holosim’s mechanics and earn the resources needed to grow your fleets.

## Preserved Official Text

Quests in Holosim are the main way to learn the game and progress your character. Starting them is simple and guided from the very beginning.
Steps to start a quest:
Look for the Quests icon: you’ll find it in the bottom-right corner of the screen. When a new quest is available, it will glow to catch your attention.
Open the Quests menu: click the icon to see the available questlines: Tutorial, Storyline, Expertise, and Daily.
Select a quest: choose the quest you want to begin and click on it for details.
Follow the objectives: each quest will explain what you need to do, such as forming a fleet, mining resources, or completing combat actions.
Claim your rewards: once you finish the objectives, return to the Quests menu and click “Claim Rewards” to receive your XP, crew, or zATLAS.
Starting quests is the fastest way to get familiar with Holosim’s mechanics and earn the resources needed to grow your fleets.
