## Registry rules

- A proposal publication establishes a request, not approval.
- A reported passing vote establishes an approval claim, not execution.
- Execution requires an official execution record, transaction, deployed change, or equivalent direct evidence.
- Conditional schedules remain roadmap statements until delivery is independently established.
- Community transcripts with unknown speakers may document discussion but cannot support speaker-specific attribution.
