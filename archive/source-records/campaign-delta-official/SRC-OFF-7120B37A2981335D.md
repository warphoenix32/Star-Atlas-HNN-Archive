## Preserved Official Text

What’s a Solana Wallet?
A Solana wallet is a secure app or browser extension that lets you manage your crypto tokens (like SOL, ATLAS, or POLIS) and NFTs on the Solana blockchain
You’ll need one to:
Log in to blockchain games like SAGE
Buy or sell items on the Star Atlas Marketplace
Store and use Star Atlas NFTs (ships, crew, etc.)
Why Choose Backpack?
Backpack is a free and user-friendly Solana wallet, perfect for new users. It works as:
A browser extension (Chrome, Brave, Edge)
A mobile app (iOS and Android)
A Centralized exchange
Backpack is trusted by the Star Atlas team and used across the Solana ecosystem.
How to Create a Backpack
1. Go to backpack.app
Click Download and choose your browser or mobile platform
2. Install & Open
Add the extension (or install the app), then open Backpack.
3. Create New Wallet
Click Create New Wallet and follow the steps:
4. Select the Chain
Choose the blockchain you want to use.Since Star Atlas runs on Solana, select: Solana → Set Up Wallet
5. Create a Password
Set a password to protect access to your wallet
6. Your Backpack Wallet Is Ready
You're now ready to use your Backpack wallet.
For more information, visit their official support center: https://support.backpack.exchange/wallet
