    About This Wiki

    This wiki documents the lore of Star Atlas, a grand strategy game set in a distant future where three galactic factions — MUD (humans), ONI (alien consortium), and Ustur (sentient androids) — vie for resources, influence, and survival.

    The content is organized from official canon sources — lore sheets, in-game narratives, and published materials — synthesized into a cohesive and navigable universe reference.
