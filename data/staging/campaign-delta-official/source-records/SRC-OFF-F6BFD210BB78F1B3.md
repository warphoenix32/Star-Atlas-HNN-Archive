## Classified Claims

- When you rent out your ships through the Fleet Rental platform in Star Atlas, a 10% fee is applied to each rental transaction.
- This fee ensures the continued development and sustainability of the platform.
- Fee Breakdown The 10% Fleet Rental fee is split among multiple contributors: 50% – Core developers of the lending program 15% – Third-party contributors (UI/UX, frontend dev, and original negotiators) 30% – ATMTA (Star Atlas core development team) 5% – Star Atlas DAO (goes to the DAO Treasury) This breakdown supports the infrastructure and incentivizes ongoing improvements to the rental system.
- Important Notes The renter pays the full listed price, but 10% is taken as a fee, so the lender receives 90% of the rental income.
- The fee is automatically deducted at the time of rental.
- The DAO’s share of this fee supports decentralized governance and can be tracked in the DAO Treasury.

## Preserved Official Text

When you rent out your ships through the Fleet Rental platform in Star Atlas, a 10% fee is applied to each rental transaction. This fee ensures the continued development and sustainability of the platform.
Fee Breakdown
The 10% Fleet Rental fee is split among multiple contributors:
50% – Core developers of the lending program
15% – Third-party contributors (UI/UX, frontend dev, and original negotiators)
30% – ATMTA (Star Atlas core development team)
5% – Star Atlas DAO (goes to the DAO Treasury)
This breakdown supports the infrastructure and incentivizes ongoing improvements to the rental system.
Important Notes
The renter pays the full listed price, but 10% is taken as a fee, so the lender receives 90% of the rental income.
The fee is automatically deducted at the time of rental.
The DAO’s share of this fee supports decentralized governance and can be tracked in the DAO Treasury.
