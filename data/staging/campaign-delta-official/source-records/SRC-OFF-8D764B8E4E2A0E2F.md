Faction Fleet was the first on-chain gameplay system in Star Atlas, launched in 2021. By enlisting your ships and keeping them resupplied with R4 (Fuel, Food, Ammo, Toolkits), you earn passive ATLAS rewards over time
Looking for Higher Passive Income?
Today, the most rewarding passive income method for your ships is Fleet Rental
Create a fleet in SAGE
List it on the Fleet Rental platform via play.staratlas.com
Other players pay ATLAS to use your ships
You earn rewards while keeping ownership
