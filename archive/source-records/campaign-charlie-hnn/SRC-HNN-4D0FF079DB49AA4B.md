With the help of Ju from Gargantua Industries, The Hologram has two additional minor but important updates to the study of this ancient text.

When the first draft of the decoded language was first published, the was room for open debate regarding the usage of the characters “U” and “W”. At the time, historical texts provided inconstant and interchangeable usages of each character. Additionally, due to the low quality resolutions of these texts, it was originally believed that the “W” character was a slight variation of the “U” character”.
