## Preserved Official Text

Joining a DAC (Decentralized Autonomous Corporation) in Star Atlas is a great way to connect with other players, share resources and take on collective goals in the metaverse. Follow these steps to explore and become part of a DAC
Step 1: Connect Your Wallet
Go to play.staratlas.comClick the wallet icon at the bottom-left of the screenSelect and connect your wallet
Step 2: Access the DAC Platform
Once connected, click on the DAC logo on the bottom-left of the interfaceThis opens the DAC Platform, where you can browse all registered DACs
Step 3: Browse DACs
Use filters and tags to explore different DACsEach DAC has its own profile, mission, and valuesLook for DACs aligned with your gameplay goals—whether you're into exploration, combat, trade, or mining
Step 4: Submit a Join Request
Once you’ve found a DAC you like:
Visit its profile page
Click Join DAC
Write a short message (optional), then submit your application
Note: Some DACs may require approval before accepting new members
Step 5: Await Approval
If your application is accepted, you’ll be officially onboarded into the DAC.From there, you can start collaborating with other members and taking part in group activities!
