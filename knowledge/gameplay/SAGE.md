# SAGE
SAGE is the Star Atlas browser-based, on-chain strategy product family. SAGE Labs and later named phases belong to that family, but roadmap labels and shipped builds must be kept separate.

## Identity and scope

An official support record documents SAGE Labs as a browser-based 4X economic strategy surface involving mining, crafting, resource movement, and faction competition on Solana. That record supports availability at its captured date. [SRC-OFF-0A646AE069AFFBA5](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-0A646AE069AFFBA5.md)

## Lifecycle

Official Discord on 2022-04-29 described SCREAM as prototyping work (`SA-DISCORD-ANN-F863D82FBAF30DF9`). A November 2022 official publication then described the renamed SAGE program as roadmap work. Together they establish development history and terminology, not delivery of the later SAGE Labs build. [SRC-OFF-22181D98D7A1B870](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-22181D98D7A1B870.md)

Official Discord announced a September 21 launch date on 2023-09-15 and stated on 2023-09-21 that SAGE Labs was live (`SA-DISCORD-ANN-6DF1790F1F2402FA`; `SA-DISCORD-ANN-A37A8B7B245E7776`). For Starbased, an April 2, 2024 plan was delayed on that date; the official live record is April 4 (`SA-DISCORD-ANN-9FFFF91025488D09`; `SA-DISCORD-ANN-76D9861DCB3FE798`; `SA-DISCORD-ANN-CCF4600A4C08F697`). This preserves planned date, delay, and release as distinct states.

Atlas Brew later discussed SAGE Labs in release language at `SRC-ATLAS-BREW-0034`, 00:02:33–00:04:33 (`SEG-ATLAS-BREW-0034-0002`). Because speaker identity is not normalized, the segment supports public discussion and chronology but no named-speaker attribution. [Segment evidence](../../archive/semantic/atlas-brew/segment-index.json)

## Current state

As of 2026-07-17, official captured material supports `LIVE / UPDATED` for the SAGE product family, with SAGE Labs live on 2023-09-21 and the Starbased phase live on 2024-04-04. It does not prove that every roadmap phase, interoperability claim, or proposed mechanic has shipped.

## Research gaps

The exact public launch of SAGE 3D and the build-level relationship among SAGE Labs, Starbased, SAGE 3D, and C4 remain research questions. Feature status should be tied to a specific environment and date.
