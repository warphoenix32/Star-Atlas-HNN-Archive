## Preserved Official Text

Seamlessly acquire ATLAS and POLIS with a credit card or by swapping tokens on Solana
Looking to fuel your Star Atlas journey with ATLAS and POLIS tokens? The Star Atlas Marketplace now makes it easier than ever, offering two options to fund your wallet:
💳 Buy with a credit card via Topper
🔄 Swap Solana tokens via Jupiter
📝 Note: You must already have a Solana wallet to use either method. Learn how to create one in our Phantom Wallet guide
Access the Wallet Menu
Go to play.staratlas.com
Click the wallet icon in the bottom-left corner
Scroll down to “Manage your wallet’s fund”
Choose your preferred option: Topper or Jupiter
Option 1: Buy with a Credit Card using Topper
Topper allows you to buy ATLAS, POLIS, or SOL directly with your credit or debit card in supported currencies (USD, EUR, etc.).
🔹 How to use Topper:
Click Launch next to Topper
A popup window will appear. Follow these steps:
Select your currency (e.g., USD, EUR)
Enter the amount you want to spend
Choose the token you want to receive: ATLAS, POLIS, or SOL
Proceed to identity verification (KYC):
Log in or create an Uphold account
Provide required ID and verification documents
Add your credit or debit card
Review and approve the transaction
Once approved, the tokens will be ready to be withdrawn to your preferred Solana wallet address
📝 Note: If you're new to Solana, check out How to create a Phantom Wallet
Option 2: Swap Tokens Using Jupiter
If your wallet is already funded with SOL or USDC, you can use Jupiter to swap directly on-chain.
🔹 How to use Jupiter:
Click Launch next to Jupiter
A popup window will appear—click Connect to link your wallet
Choose the token you want to swap (e.g., SOL or USDC)
Select your desired token (ATLAS or POLIS)
Click Swap to complete the transaction
Jupiter is a decentralized exchange aggregator that finds the best route across Solana DEXs to execute your trade efficiently.
