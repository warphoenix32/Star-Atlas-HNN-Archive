## Preserved Official Text

Minimum Requirements
These are the baseline specs to run the Showroom at a functional level:
CPU: Quad-core Intel or AMD, 2.5GHz or faster
Memory: 8 GB RAM
Graphics Card: Nvidia GTX 1080, AMD RX 5700 or faster
Storage: At least 20 GB of free disk space
Recommended Specs (High Fidelity)
For the best visual experience and smoother performance:
CPU: 8-core Intel or AMD, 3.5GHz or faster
Memory: 32 GB RAM
Graphics Card: Nvidia RTX 2070 or better
Storage: SSD recommended for optimal load times
To experience the full graphical potential of the Showroom using Unreal Engine 5, a high-end GPU is strongly recommended.
⚠️ Mac Compatibility
At this time, macOS is not supported in the Showroom. You’ll need to use a Windows PC or other compatible hardware to access the UE5 experience
