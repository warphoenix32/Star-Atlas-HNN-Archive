## Preserved Official Text

Star Atlas is a next-generation space exploration game and metaverse powered by blockchain technology and Unreal Engine 5.Set in the year 2620, it invites players to explore a vast, persistent galaxy where they can own, trade, and use digital assets as part of an open economy.
Whether you're flying ships in 3D space, building fleets, managing resources, or participating in decentralized governance, Star Atlas blends immersive gameplay with Web3 ownership and community collaboration.
Play Across Multiple Experiences
Star Atlas isn’t just one game, it’s a growing ecosystem of interconnected gameplay experiences:
UE5 Game (Epic Games)Fly your ships, explore the galaxy, and engage in multiplayer combat and racing. You can use crew avatars, mine asteroids, and immerse yourself in the future of 3D space gaming
SAGE (Browser-based 4X on-chain game)Explore, craft, and battle with your ships and crew in a fully on-chain strategy game built on Solana
Holosim (Free-to-Play Browser-based 4X on-chain game)Explore, craft, and battle with your ships and crew in a fully on-chain strategy game built on Atlas net
Fleet RentalCreate a fleet in SAGE, then list it for rent on Fleet Rental platform so other players can pay to use your ships. Earn passive income without lifting a finger
Galactic MarketplaceBuy and sell Star Atlas NFTs ships, crew, resources, collectibles and more, using ATLAS or USDC
Faction Fleet & Faction ClaimStake your ships or Claims Stake for passive rewards like ATLAS and R4 resources
DAC PlatformCreate or join a DAC (Decentralized Autonomous Corporation) directly from the Play site. Collaborate with other players, build your reputation and prepare for future shared governance and gameplay
Star Atlas DAOLock POLIS or ATLAS to earn rewards, reduce fees, and vote on proposals that guide the future of the Star Atlas ecosystem
Choose Your Faction
At the heart of Star Atlas are three powerful factions vying for control:
The ONI
A consortium of alien species who, after nearly exterminating one another in war - set aside their differences in the hopes of a more bountiful life in space.
The Ustur
Spawned by an advanced AI, they are a collective of sentient, soft-formed androids with a developed sense of identity, culture, and fashion.
The MUD
A faction of humans who were led by a god-like alien being plunder all of the Earth's remaining resources to set off for the promise of the stars.
Your faction determines your allies, rewards and narrative in the metaverse
Own and Trade In-Game Assets
Every ship, crew member, mining rig, collectible, or resource in Star Atlas is an NFT—stored in your wallet, tradable on the Star Atlas Marketplace, and usable in various game modes
Assets are interoperable across platforms and have direct utility in gameplay, from flying and crafting to staking and upgrading
