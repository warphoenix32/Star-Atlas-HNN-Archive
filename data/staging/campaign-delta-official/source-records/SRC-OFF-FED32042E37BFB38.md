Building a AAA game using cutting-edge blockchain and real-time tech takes time, but you don't have to wait to start engaging with Star Atlas.
Even as the full metaverse experience continues to evolve, the ecosystem already offers multiple ways to interact, earn, and explore:
SAGE (Browser-Based On-Chain Game)
A tactical 4X space strategy experience directly in your browser. Deploy fleets, extract resources, craft assets, and explore the on-chain Galia Expanse in real-time
Holosim (Free-to-Play Browser-Based On-Chain Game)
A tactical 4X space strategy experience directly in your browser. Deploy fleets, extract resources, craft assets, and explore the on-chain Galia Expanse in real-time
Star Atlas UE5 Game (Showroom on Epic Games)
The Unreal Engine 5 version of Star Atlas is live in closed alpha. Walk around the Showroom, fly your ships, race, and preview the immersive AAA world under construction.
PLAY.StarAtlas.com
The main Star Atlas web platform where you can:
Create your player profile and join a faction
Join or manage a DAC (guild)
Rent your Fleet to earn passive rewards
Stake your Claims Stake in Faction Claim to earn R4 resources (Fuel, Fodd, Toolkt and Ammunition)
Access the Marketplace to buy and sell ships, crew, resources and collectibles
Star Atlas DAO
A decentralized governance system where users can:
Lock ATLAS or POLIS tokens
Vote on proposals
Shape the future of the Star Atlas ecosystem
To keep up with the Star Atlas Official Roadmap please visit our Public Roadmap. The public roadmap is regularly updated and provides ongoing updates
