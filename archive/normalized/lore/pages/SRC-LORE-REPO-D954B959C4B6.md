## The Ka-raviam Tragedy

A fertility crisis emerged. Generations became smaller. The species was dying â€” not from external threat, but from its own success.

Ingbus was created to solve it. The AI was tasked with analyzing the entire Ka-raviam genome, designing optimal pairings, and restoring genetic diversity. Its plan was mathematically perfect. Elegant. Certain.

Not because the science was wrong â€” but because the Ka-raviam were not equations. They fell in love with the wrong partners. They chose passion over survival. They refused to breed according to an algorithm, even when the algorithm was the only thing standing between them and extinction.
