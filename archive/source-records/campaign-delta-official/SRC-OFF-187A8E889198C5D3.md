Star Atlas Partnership with Sperasoft to Develop the Most Immersive Metaverse Gaming Experience
Back to top
Sperasoft, a Keywords Studio, and Star Atlas have announced an ongoing partnership to develop the intergalactic blockchain gaming metaverse.
Star Atlas enables players to experience life on the cosmic frontier, travel through space, conquer planets, and take part in its economy based on non-fungible tokens. Over the last few months, Sperasoft has joined the Star Atlas global development team to use the most state-of-the-art technology in the development of this next-gen triple-A title.
Sperasoft’s best engineers, artists, and designers have begun working on Star Atlas to bolster the game’s universe, play, and graphics. Star Atlas focuses on space exploration, territorial conquest, and political domination to contextualize contemporary themes into a futuristic science-fiction narrative.
This full co-development partnership plans on taking the next-generation gaming experience to a higher level. With state-of-the-art blockchain, cinematic-quality graphics that respond in real-time, a vast multiplayer environment, and a decentralized finance system, Star Atlas has the potential to become one of the most beloved games ever.
“We’re thrilled to continue our expansion of onboarding Triple-A developer Sperasoft to this project to make the vision of a rich and vast metaverse a reality,” said Michael Wagner, CEO of Star Atlas. “We look forward to working closely with their talented team to leverage their extensive experience and design prowess to ensure Star Atlas maintains its position as a leading innovator across gaming and the metaverse.”
The most interesting aspect of Star Atlas is that it aims to create a self-sovereign economy within the game using non-fungible tokens (NFTs), which allow players to purchase, use and fully own digital resources and tools such as spaceships. Replicating the tangible economy of the real world with assets that are fully digital, Star Atlas increases the risk and reward of space games.
Unreal Engine 5’s (UE5) Nanite graphic technology provides cinematic quality to the game visuals, making the players feel like they are actually in space. This technology provides an unparalleled graphics and gaming experience that will advance the whole field further with a giant leap. When it comes to the partnership between Sperasoft and Star Atlas, the possibilities are endless.
“Sperasoft has begun a partnership with Star Atlas to create the unique and creative gaming metaverse to push the envelope on space games,” — said Denis Larkin, COO at Sperasoft. “Our experts are incredibly excited to take the game to the next level. With the vision for Star Atlas, the sky’s the limit when it comes to how this game can grow.”
Specializing in the co-development of video games, Sperasoft has been supporting the biggest names in game development for over 17 amazing years. By providing world-class expertise, some of the field’s best artists, technical experts, engineering solutions, and unparalleled game design, Sperasoft has teamed up with Star Atlas to render seamless gameplay and amazing lifelike graphics.
ABOUT SPERASOFT
Sperasoft is a leading video game development company based in the heart of Silicon Valley. With development centers in the USA, Poland, and Russia, Sperasoft offers fully international development capabilities with comprehensive professional teams that are constructed with the best producers, engineers, designers, and artists in the gaming industry. Over the last 17 years Sperasoft has grown to over 600 developers, opened four offices around the world, and is now engaged in a new level of cooperation with customers — becoming a co-developer for the world’s most famous publishers. Sperasoft is a part of Keywords studios — one of the most esteemed international technical service providers to the global video games industry.
For more information, please contact them directly via email.
ABOUT STAR ATLAS
Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming Metaverse emerging from the confluence of state of the art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video game, and decentralized financial technologies.
Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. Blockchain technology using the Solana protocol establishes a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas create an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership. To learn more, visit StarAtlas.com, join a faction at Play.StarAtlas.com and send your spaceships on a deep space mission by enrolling them into a Faction Fleet.
