## Preserved Official Text

Scanning allows your fleet to search space for SDUs, which can be used in crafting recipes.
This guide explains how to prepare a scanning fleet, move it to the area you want to scan, and start a scan sweep.
Note: C4 PTR is still in development. Scanning behavior, scan patterns, costs, and results may change over time.
Before you start
Before scanning, you need to create a fleet and move it to the area where you want to scan.
If you need help with those steps, check these guides first:
How to Create and Use a Fleet in C4 PTR
How to Move a Fleet in C4 PTR
To scan, your fleet needs:
Fuel to move
Repair Kits to perform scan sweeps
Make sure your fleet has both before you start.
Step 1: Prepare your fleet
Before starting a scan:
Create your fleet.
Add the ships you want to use.
Add enough Fuel for travel.
Add enough Repair Kits for scanning.
Undock the fleet.
Your fleet must be undocked before it can scan.
Step 2: Move your fleet to the scan location
Once your fleet is ready, move it to the area where you want to scan.
You can use your normal fleet movement options, such as:
Warp
Subwarp
Warp Gate when available
When your fleet arrives, keep it undocked and select it.
Step 3: Open the Scan menu
To begin scanning:
Select your fleet.
Open the fleet action menu.
Click Scan.
This opens the Scan Sweep view.
Step 4: Choose a scan pattern
In the Scan Sweep view, you will see several scan patterns.
Each pattern has its own scanning characteristics, the selected pattern affects the scan area and the cost of the scan.
For example, different patterns may change:
The size of the scan area
The signal value
The amount of Repair Kits required
To start a scan:
Review the available scan patterns.
Click the pattern you want to use.
Confirm the scan by clicking Ready.
Step 5: Start scanning
After selecting a scan pattern and confirming it, your fleet will begin the scan sweep.
The goal of scanning is to find SDUs, which can later be used for crafting recipes.
Depending on future updates, scanning behavior, results, and follow-up actions may change.
Summary
To scan in C4 PTR:
Create a fleet.
Add Fuel and Repair Kits.
Undock the fleet.
Move it to the area you want to scan.
Select the fleet.
Click Scan.
Choose a scan pattern.
Click Ready to begin the scan.
Scanning is used to find SDUs for crafting, so it is an important part of the broader resource and production loop
