June 14, 2023 — In the wake of the publication of PIP-X, Polaris Fuel has accelerated its business model of providing stabilized R4 prices in Star Atlas. Akin to historical organizations like OPEC, Polaris Fuel is comprised of members that uniformly agree to economic condition under which to purchase and sell commodities such as Food, Fuel, Ammo, and Toolkits.
Press enter or click to view image in full size
Polaris Fuel Proposes Fixed Prices
Under the Polaris Fuel Model, members would agree to a fixed buy and sell prices of resources.
Polaris Selling Price (PSP): The Polaris selling price will be the price determined by the community present in the project as the “fair price”, achieving a beneficial balance for both the producer and the final consumer. The launch price of the project will be the original selling price of Star Atlas until recently:
FOOD: 0.0006144 Atlas
FUEL: 0.0014336 Atlas
AMMO: 0.0021504 Atlas
TOOLS: 0.0017408 Atlas
Polaris Purchase Price (PPP): We will use a pre-determined purchase price to acquire resources in the game. At the time of our launch the price will be calculated by subtracting 10% from the PSP (selling price). This 10% difference will go to cover the Star Atlas market fees, as well as Solana’s fees, while the rest will be added back to the liquidity to expand and strengthen it. The model does not aim at an economic profit as the main goal, but at a stabilization of the liquidity pool and a minimal surcharge on the original provider.
Polaris Fuel Member Benefits
Such an economic operation model requires serious participation from members. To incentivize participation, Polaris Fuel has published the following member benefits.
Obtaining resources at preferential prices
By joining the Polaris Fuel Project, members will have priority access to resources at competitive prices thanks to the Polaris purchasing system. Organizations working in partnership with Polaris will receive priority in the sale of resources and preferential prices in the future.
Contribution to a fair economy
Members directly contribute to the expansion and strengthening of liquidity in Atlas and resources. This not only benefits all members of the cooperative, but also creates a stronger, more stable and fairer market for the Star Atlas community.
Decision-making
Both the price of products and important decisions on the future of the company will be taken in consensus with its members. Discussion and voting channels will facilitate this system. Founding members and representatives of Polaris teams will always have a say in important decisions.
Incentive Program: For its most committed members, Polaris Fuel will maintain a vibrant structure that will incentivize its members through a merit-based membership system consisting of3 levels:
Silver: Basic level by registering as a Polaris supplier and supplying PSP with a minimum supply of 50k during the month.
Gold: Next level of efficiency for those members most involved, with a higher incentive system, access to dedicated channels and a greater say in decision making. Requirement: supply >100k bi-weekly.
Platinum: The highest level of production for those truly dedicated to this profession. To gain entry to this level members must provide 500K of resources per month, or remain at the Gold level for 3 consecutive months.
Polaris Aims to Provide Economic Stability and Subsidization
Polaris Fuel aims to revolutionize resource mining and trading in the vast Star Atlas universe. The cooperative is dedicated to mining, refining, buying and selling resources with the goal of creating stable and fair prices. Polaris Fuel is a clan-and-faction agnostic group and seeks to integrate any member of the community.
At launch, the Polaris Fuel system is intended to strengthen the economy, placing fairness over profits.
Their mission statement: “We believe that in the metaverse there is an opportunity to do things differently from traditional markets, and we believe that if we stay true to our ideals, we can grow in an organic way and become a successful and sustainable project.”
