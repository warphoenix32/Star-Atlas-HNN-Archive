## Preserved Official Text

Explore, Fly, Fight, and Race at the Heart of MUD Territory
Volant Station is the central multiplayer hub and starting zone for the MUD faction in the Star Atlas UE5 game, available on the Epic Games Store
What Can You Do in Volant Station?
Volant Station offers multiple gameplay activities for both solo and multiplayer players:
Explore Freely: Navigate the station and surrounding platforms on foot or using a jetpack
Summon Ships: Spawn your owned ships at designated pads and take off
Race: Compete on the racetrack solo or in multiplayer sessions
Meet Other Players: Socialize in open multiplayer lobbies
Jetpack Mobility
All players are equipped with a free jetpack by default, allowing vertical movement and easier exploration across the Volant Station environment. Mastering this is especially helpful for reaching hidden areas or gaining a tactical edge in combat zones.
Lore
Volant Station is the MUD faction’s central stronghold, orbiting above their territory in the Galia Expanse. It's a strategic outpost for exploration, research, and fleet deployment and now a hub for training the next generation of explorers
