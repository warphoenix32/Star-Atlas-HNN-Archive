The Star Atlas Q1 2026 State of the Economy report reveals a period of macroeconomic contraction, with Star Atlas tokens declining roughly 80% between September 15, 2025, and March 5, 2026. Despite these price headwinds, the core player base remains engaged, recording 8,109 active citizen accounts and a resilient Starbased DAU averaging 700–900. Total full-period GDP reached $1.8M in USDC and 4.22B ATLAS, with token burns remaining the dominant economic output. A significant divergence emerged in asset markets: while resource prices collapsed 70–85% in USDC terms, non-functional assets—specifically Access passes and Crew—saw strong appreciation. Ship prices followed a unique trend, declining in USDC but rising in ATLAS terms. Regarding market health, the deployment of Open Market Operations (OMO) in early 2026 successfully curtailed Over-the-Counter (OTC) trade activity, which dropped from 12% to 7.2% of total volume. Total ecosystem asset value is estimated at $127M, with an additional $55M held in currencies. This data suggests that while external market conditions remain challenging, the internal economy remains stable and structurally intact as players position themselves for future product releases.
