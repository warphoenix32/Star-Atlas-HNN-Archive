## Preserved Official Text

If you’ve enlisted ships in Faction Fleet (SCORE) and want to withdraw them, here’s how it works:
What You’ll Need
SOL in your wallet to cover transaction fees
Toolkits to repair your ships (required for withdrawal)
Withdrawing automatically repairs your fleet using Toolkits from your wallet.
Steps to Withdraw Your Fleet
Go to play.staratlas.com/fleet
Click Manage Fleet
Click Manage Ship
Click Withdraw Entire Fleet
⚠️ You cannot withdraw ships individually. You must withdraw the entire fleet assigned to that faction.
