# Star Atlas Library Audience and Navigation Map

The public Library begins with reader questions, not repository folders. Eight gateways lead into a planned thirty-page foundational narrative portfolio. Detailed dossiers and research records remain available below that introductory layer.

## Reader gateways

| Gateway | Reader purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Start Here | Orient a first-time visitor without requiring prior knowledge of Star Atlas. |
| The Star Atlas Universe | Explore Galia, its factions, peoples, conflicts, places and canon. |
| Games and Experiences | Understand the playable products, tests and product families. |
| Ships and Economy | Explore ships, ownership, tokens, resources, production and trade. |
| History | Follow the project through major eras, releases, setbacks and turning points. |
| People and Community | Meet the players, guilds, creators, journalists and community traditions. |
| Governance and Institutions | Understand ATMTA, the DAO, Foundation, Council and consequential proposals. |
| Research the Archive | Reach detailed ledgers, registries, evidence records and preserved sources. |
