## Preserved Official Text

Bringing your ships into Starbased is a critical step after creating your character, enabling you to navigate the vast expanse of the Star Atlas universe with your fleet. Here's how to import your ships into the game:
Navigate to Your Faction's Central Space Station: After connecting to Starbased and creating your character, locate and click on the Central Space Station specific to your faction. For example, if you are part of the ONI faction, click on the ONI CSS. USTUR and MUD factions will click on USTUR CSS and MUD CSS, respectively.
Access the Port of Entry: Within the new menu that appears on the left side of your screen, find and click on "Port of Entry." This action will take you to the next step in the process of importing your ships.
Select Your Ships to Import: A list of all the ships in your connected wallet will be displayed. Here, you can select the ship or ships you wish to import into Starbased, specify the quantity for each, and then click on "Deposit to Starbase."
Confirm the Transaction: Your wallet will prompt you to confirm the import transaction. Once you approve it, your ships will be successfully imported into Starbased.
