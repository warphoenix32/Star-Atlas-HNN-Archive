Additionally, a major technical correction was implemented for z.ink subscriptions; a long-standing bug that swapped Alpha and Aura multipliers has been fixed, resulting in the retroactive adjustment of millions of zXP and recalculated airdrop progress. These updates prioritize economic stability and refined combat mechanics as the community prepares for the next phase of progression.
