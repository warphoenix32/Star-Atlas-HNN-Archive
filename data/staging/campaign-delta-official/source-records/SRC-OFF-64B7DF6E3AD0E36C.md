## Preserved Official Text

In Star Atlas, structures are a class of NFT assets used to build, extract, produce, or manage operations across the metaverse.
They are the backbone of many gameplay systems—including planetary resource extraction, crafting, station management and infrastructure control
Types of Structures
Structures include (but are not limited to):
Claim Stakes – Used to claim land on planets and enable resource extraction.
Mining Drills – Harvest raw materials over time from planetary plots.
Power Plants – Provide energy to support other structures.
Space Stations – Potential hubs for storage, crafting, repairs, or economic activity.
Each structure type has specific utility depending on the gameplay loop it’s tied to such as Faction Claims or future economic operations
