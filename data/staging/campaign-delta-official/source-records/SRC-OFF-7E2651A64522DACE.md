## Preserved Official Text

Quests are the core of progression in Holosim. They guide you through the game, unlock new features, and provide rewards like crew and zATLAS.
There are four main types of quests:
Tutorial Quests
Introduce the basic mechanics of Holosim, such as managing fleets, saving your game, and using the menus.
Storyline Quests
The main campaign of Holosim. These quests take you across different aspects of the game, including mining, crafting, combat, and exploration. Completing them unlocks major rewards and marks your progress in each Chapter.
Expertise Quests
Unlocked after progressing through the Storyline. These quests allow you to specialize in a career path (mining, combat, scanning, or merchant) and earn special permits to craft advanced ships.
Daily Quests
Repeatable missions that refresh every day at 00:00 UTC. Daily quests provide steady rewards and permits, encouraging you to log in regularly.
