## Governance

- Council Review: The PIP is discussed and approved/rejected by the Council.
- Foundation Submission: An approved draft is sent to the Star Atlas Foundation.
- Voting: If approved by the Star Atlas Foundation, the PIP goes up for voting on govern.staratlas.com.
- DAO Vote: The PIP is either approved or rejected by the Star Atlas DAO members.
- Implementation: If approved, the PIP is sent back to the Star Atlas Foundation for implementation.
