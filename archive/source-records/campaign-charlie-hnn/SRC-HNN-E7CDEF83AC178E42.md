HolOpinion – The voice of the community
Posted by FowlAC | May 25, 2022 | Community
Today we are excited to launch our commentary section – HolOpinion. The Star Atlas community is rich with amazing people and ideas but also strong opinions. HolOpinion is here to give those voices a forum for discussion.
Guidelines:
HolOpinion is open to all members of the Star Atlas community.
Commentary can be written in any language although we prefer English. In addition to the non-English version we may ask for an English translation
Topics are open although it needs to be relevant to the Star Atlas community.
HolOpinion is not a forum for disputes between individuals or groups. We will not tolerate personal attacks or hate speech of any kind.
Submissions should be between 400-1200 words.
Send submissions to: opinion@thehologram.io
The Hologram reserves the right to edit your submissions for clarity and length.
Submissions accepted for publication may include additional terms.
Building a metaverse is no easy task and the journey to a fully functional AAA game will be long and require many hard conversations. Our hope is that through HolOpinion, the Star Atlas community can provide productive feedback to the developers along the way. Only together can we build the metaverse that we all want.
