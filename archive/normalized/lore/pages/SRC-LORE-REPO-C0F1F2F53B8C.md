# Etira (Planet — Yuldun Waste, MRZ-32)

**Location**: Yuldun Waste Sector (MRZ-32)

---

## Overview

Planet Etira is the **last capital of the Black Market Auction House (BMAH)** and one of the most deliberately isolated worlds in Galia. Beneath its hostile, terraformed atmosphere lies a secret so old that only the **Photoli** know the full truth — and they have ensured that no one else ever reaches it.

---

## The Dreamer Below

### Discovery (~2135)

- During their early exploration of Galia, the **Photoli** discovered a **strange and powerful creature** buried in the heart of planet Etira
- The creature possessed abilities the Photoli had never encountered
- They could **not destroy it** — not because it was truly immortal, but because the Photoli **did not know how** to kill it
- The Photoli decided to **seal the creature** instead

### The Sealing

- The Photoli **stole the creature's memories**, stripping away its consciousness and identity
- They **confined it to a subterranean pool** deep within Etira's core
- To prevent anyone from reaching the creature, the Photoli **terraformed the planet's atmosphere** to be **lethally hostile** to all known species
- This is the **third confirmed case of Photoli cosmological manipulation** (alongside draining Night Window's star and casting the Bluvael illusion)

### The Name

- The creature's name in Galia is the **"Dreamer Below"**
- Even stripped of its memories, the creature's **psychic influence bleeds through** — indirect, dreamlike, unsettling
- The creature is **not truly immortal** — the Photoli simply lacked the means to destroy it

### Current State

- **The Dreamer Below is dead.** During the Never Alone incident (~2624), the entity was destroyed
- The Dark Photoli **Bah** visited the corpse afterward, **looting a silver gem** from within the Dreamer's remains and storing it in a pocket dimension
- The psychic influence that once leaked from the sealed creature has **ceased**
- Etira is now a hostile world with a dead god rotting in its core

---

## The Cult of the Dreamer

### Origin

- Over the centuries, the Dreamer Below's **indirect psychic influence** has reached susceptible minds across the sector
- A **fanatical cult of memory stealers** was founded — inspired unconsciously by the creature's nature
- The cult seeks to **resurrect the Dreamer Below** by gathering enough stolen memories to reconstruct its consciousness

### Methods

- Cult members practice **memory theft** — extracting memories from unwilling victims
- They believe that by feeding enough memories to the creature, they can awaken it
- Their practices mirror the Photoli's original act of sealing the Dreamer — a dark echo of the method used to imprison it

### Threat Level

- The cult operated in the shadows of Yuldun Waste's criminal underworld
- Their true purpose was unknown to most — even the BMAH considered them eccentric
- **Post-Never Alone**: Herdus was killed by Saand, the Church was exposed, and the Dreamer was destroyed. The cult is effectively **defunct**

---

## The BMAH Capital (Former)

### Origin (~2515)

- **~2515**: During the Convergence War, **Eknok**, a notorious **war criminal**, crash-lands on planet Etira
- He and his crew discover how to **bypass the planet's atmospheric storm**
- They establish a **black market hidden from the rest of the galaxy**, exploiting Etira's hostile atmosphere as natural security
- This operation grows into the **Black Market Auction House (BMAH)**

### Destruction (~2624)

- During the **Never Alone** incident, Saand sabotaged the BMAH gathering, killed Herdus.soul, and exposed the Church
- The **Council of Peace** invaded Etira with military-grade atmospheric shielding, **destroying the BMAH's subsurface bunkers**
- The BMAH is now **in transit** — scattered across Galia, searching for a new permanent base under Garonthe's leadership
- Etira is effectively **abandoned** — no longer a functioning capital for any organization
