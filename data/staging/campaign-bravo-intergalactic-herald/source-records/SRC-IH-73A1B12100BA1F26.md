SAGE Labs (Starbased)
SAGE Labs (Starbased) in a version of Star Atlas that is playable in a web browser. You can move around your ships to mine and scan for resources. Then you can move the cargo and craft other components.

Local Markets are a new feature in SAGE Labs (Starbased) which allow for a marketplace at each starbase. Resources and materials can be brought and sold at the starbase.
