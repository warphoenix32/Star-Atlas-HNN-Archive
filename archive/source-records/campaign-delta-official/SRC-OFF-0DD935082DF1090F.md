## Preserved Official Text

This article provides a detailed guide on how to rent a fleet in Star Atlas. Designed to help you navigate the Fleet Rentals system efficiently, the guide covers everything from account setup and fleet selection to finalizing your rental agreement. Follow these steps to secure a fleet that meets your in-game needs and start your mission with confidence.
Step 1: Account Setup
Before renting a fleet, ensure that you have created your account at based.staratlas.com. During the account creation process, you will select your faction and set your username. Most users will have this step completed prior to renting
Step 2: Navigate to the Fleet Rentals Section
Open your web browser and go to play.staratlas.com.
In the side navigation bar, click on the Fleet Rentals icon to access the rental marketplace.
Step 3: Browse Available Fleets
Within the Fleet Rentals section, navigate to the Fleets Available to Borrow area. Here you can view all fleets currently available for your faction. This listing provides essential information about each fleet, including the ships contained, their condition, and other operational details.
Step 4: Evaluate Your Fleet Options
When reviewing the available fleets, consider the following criteria:
Fleet Composition:Assess which ships are included in the fleet and whether they are suited for your mission—be it mining, scanning, or transport.
Resource Readiness:Verify that the fleet comes stocked with fuel, ammo, and crew if you plan to use it immediately without adding personal resources.
Fleet Score:Verify the fleet score of the fleet you're renting compared to other fleets. Learn more in this article about how fleets scores are calculated: Article
Rental Cost:Compare the daily rental cost in Atlas to ensure it aligns with your budget and mission requirements.
Step 5: Select Your Rental Duration:
You can rent a fleet for up to 24 days
Remember, you have the flexibility to cancel at any time and receive a prorated refund based on the remaining days.
Step 6: Confirm Your Rental
Once you have selected the appropriate fleet and rental duration, click the Rent Fleet button. Upon confirmation, the following will occur:
The full rental amount is paid upfront and held in escrow.
The fleet is temporarily transferred under your account control, enabling you to start using it in SAGE immediately.
Step 7: Begin Your Mission
With the fleet now under your control, you can proceed with your mission—whether that means warping to a new location, engaging in mining operations, or executing any other in-game tasks. Your rented fleet will be available until the rental period expires or is canceled, ensuring you have the flexibility needed for your strategy.
