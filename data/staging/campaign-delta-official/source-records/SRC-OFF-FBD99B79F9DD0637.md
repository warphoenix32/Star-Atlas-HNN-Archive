## Classified Claims

- Claim Stakes enlisted in Faction Claims produce and store R4 resources Fuel, Food, Toolkits, and Ammunition.
- However, each stake has a maximum storage capacity based on its tier.
- Once this cap is reached, production pauses until you claim your rewards.
