## The Sih Rihim

The Photoli's own answer to the Dark Photoli threat: an elite hunter unit that has existed for as long as the mutation itself. The Sih Rihim are selected from the most powerful Photoli warriors, trained specifically to detect and combat entities that can veil themselves from normal perception.

Their methods are classified by the Photoli Council. What is known: they work in pairs, they never stop hunting, and they have killed five Dark Photoli across galactic history. The surviving five represent not failure but the extraordinary difficulty of hunting beings that can teleport between star systems and hide in pocket dimensions.
