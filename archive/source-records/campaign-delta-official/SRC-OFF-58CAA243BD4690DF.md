## Preserved Official Text

In the current MVP release of Fleet Rentals, you can rent a fleet for a minimum of 1 day and up to 24 days.
This rental period gives you flexibility depending on your needs, whether you're planning a short-term mining operation or a multi-day strategic run across the Galia Expanse.
Future updates aim to extend rental durations up to 3 months, allowing for even more long-term planning and fleet utilization.
