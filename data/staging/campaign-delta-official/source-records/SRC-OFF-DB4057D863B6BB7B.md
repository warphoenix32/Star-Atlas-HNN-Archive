## Classified Claims

- In SAGE, you can engage in trading through two primary methods: creating new orders or fulfilling existing ones.
- This guide focuses on the latter, how to fulfill existing buy or sell orders in both the Local and Galactic Marketplaces Accessing the Marketplaces: Galactic Marketplace: Accessible from Central Space Stations (CSS) such as ONI CSS, USTUR CSS, and MUD CSS.
- Navigate to the Galactic Marketplace via the station's menu.
- Local Market: Available at starbases within the Galia Expanse.
- Select the Local Market option from the starbase's menu.
- Fulfilling an Order: Select the Item: Browse or search for the item you wish to trade.
- Toggle Between Buy and Sell Orders: On the right side of the marketplace interface, you'll find a button labeled "Seller" or "Buyer".
- Click this button to switch between viewing active sell orders (if you're buying) and buy orders (if you're selling).
- Choose an Order to Fulfill: Review the list of available orders, noting the quantity and price per unit.
- Select the order that best matches your trading criteria.
- Execute the Transaction: Click the "Buy" or "Sell" button associated with the chosen order.
- Confirm the transaction and approve it through your connected wallet.
- Important Considerations: Orders in the Galactic Marketplace are accessible across all CSS and in https://play.staratlas.com/ Orders in the Local Market are specific to the starbase where they were created
