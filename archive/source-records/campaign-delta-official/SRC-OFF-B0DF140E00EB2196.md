## Classified Claims

- Importing your crew members into SAGE is an essential step to start using them for various operations, such as ship management or crafting.
- Follow the steps below to easily import your crew and make them available for gameplay.
- Step-by-Step Instructions to Import Crew Members: Connect to SAGE: Begin by logging into SAGE and navigating to your Port of Entry, which is located in the Central Space Station.
- Access Your Wallet Inventory: Go to the Wallet Inventory section.
- Here, you'll find all the assets connected to your account.
- Select the Crew Section: Click on the CREW tab in your wallet inventory.
- This will display all the crew members currently in your wallet but not yet imported into SAGE.
- Choose Crew Members to Import: Select the crew members you want to bring into SAGEby clicking on them.
- Deposit to Starbase Crew Quarters: After selecting the crew members, click on DEPOSIT TO STARBASE CREW QUARTERS.
- A prompt will appear, requesting confirmation of the transaction.
- Confirm the Transaction: Confirm the transaction in your connected wallet.
- Once the transaction is approved, your crew members will be deposited.
- After completing these steps, your crew members will be available in the Central Space Station and ready to be used for any activities within SAGE, whether it's operating your fleet or managing crafting tasks.

Importing your crew members into SAGE is an essential step to start using them for various operations, such as ship management or crafting. Follow the steps below to easily import your crew and make them available for gameplay.
Step-by-Step Instructions to Import Crew Members:
Connect to SAGE: Begin by logging into SAGE and navigating to your Port of Entry, which is located in the Central Space Station.
Access Your Wallet Inventory: Go to the Wallet Inventory section. Here, you'll find all the assets connected to your account.
Select the Crew Section: Click on the CREW tab in your wallet inventory. This will display all the crew members currently in your wallet but not yet imported into SAGE.
Choose Crew Members to Import: Select the crew members you want to bring into SAGEby clicking on them.
Deposit to Starbase Crew Quarters: After selecting the crew members, click on DEPOSIT TO STARBASE CREW QUARTERS. A prompt will appear, requesting confirmation of the transaction.
Confirm the Transaction: Confirm the transaction in your connected wallet. Once the transaction is approved, your crew members will be deposited.
What Happens Next?
After completing these steps, your crew members will be available in the Central Space Station and ready to be used for any activities within SAGE, whether it's operating your fleet or managing crafting tasks.
