## Classified Claims

- Using crew members for crafting operations in SAGE is straightforward and similar to other in-game tasks.
- To begin crafting, you'll need to navigate to your starbase and initiate the crafting process.
- Step-by-Step Guide to Crafting with Crew in SAGE Access Your Starbase Operations: Go to the "Starbase Operations" section within SAGE to view the available crafting options.
- Select the Item to Craft: Choose the specific item you want to craft.
- Make sure you have the necessary resources and materials for the crafting process.
- Determine the Quantity: Enter the number of items you wish to craft.
- This will help you plan your crafting strategy and resource allocation.
- Assign Your Crew: Assign the desired number of crew members to the crafting task.
- All crew members currently have the same utility in SAGE, so the system will randomly select crew from those available at the starbase.
- The number of crew members you assign will affect the crafting speed when producing multiple items simultaneously.
- Start Crafting: Once everything is set, click the "Start Crafting" button to begin the process.
- Your assigned crew will start working on the items, and the progress will be displayed in real time.
- Additional Tips: Crew Assignment: If crafting a single item, adding more crew members won’t speed up the process.
- However, when crafting multiple items (e.g., crafting two units of fuel simultaneously), allocating more crew members will result in faster completion compared to using fewer crew members.
- Crafting Time: Larger quantities or more complex items may take longer to craft, so plan accordingly.

## Preserved Official Text

Using crew members for crafting operations in SAGE is straightforward and similar to other in-game tasks. To begin crafting, you'll need to navigate to your starbase and initiate the crafting process.
Step-by-Step Guide to Crafting with Crew in SAGE
Access Your Starbase Operations: Go to the "Starbase Operations" section within SAGE to view the available crafting options.
Select the Item to Craft: Choose the specific item you want to craft. Make sure you have the necessary resources and materials for the crafting process.
Determine the Quantity: Enter the number of items you wish to craft. This will help you plan your crafting strategy and resource allocation.
Assign Your Crew: Assign the desired number of crew members to the crafting task. All crew members currently have the same utility in SAGE, so the system will randomly select crew from those available at the starbase. The number of crew members you assign will affect the crafting speed when producing multiple items simultaneously.
Start Crafting: Once everything is set, click the "Start Crafting" button to begin the process. Your assigned crew will start working on the items, and the progress will be displayed in real time.
Additional Tips:
Crew Assignment: If crafting a single item, adding more crew members won’t speed up the process. However, when crafting multiple items (e.g., crafting two units of fuel simultaneously), allocating more crew members will result in faster completion compared to using fewer crew members.
Crafting Time: Larger quantities or more complex items may take longer to craft, so plan accordingly.
