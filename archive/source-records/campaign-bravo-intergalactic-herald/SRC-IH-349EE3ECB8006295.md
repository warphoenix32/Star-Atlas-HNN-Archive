The major highlight of Star Atlas Weekly Newsletter #230 is the prototyping of regional governance for SAGE C4, a POLIS tokenomics milestone enabling players to allocate Polis Voting Power (PVP) toward local policies like crafting fees. Testing for SAGE C4 will advance through a closed, NDA-backed beta before deploying to the z.ink testnet PTR. Meanwhile, Holosim received patch v1.2.28, upgrading combat layouts by faction, fixing visual diagnostics, and adding a command-view manual refresh button. Developers are rebuilding Holosim’s combat mechanics around direct RPC reads to minimize indexer delay, testing real-time laser combat while replacing the prisoner-of-war mechanic with a rule that destroys and tows stranded fleets to the Central Space Station. Future Holosim expansions will introduce marketplace interfaces and initial drone systems, including the Calico Med, Opal Wamp, and Fimbul Naga drones. Finally, following a secondary voting round, five community members—Saint-Just, Lerinor, Atlas Theory, Dinarei, and Bohemian—were officially selected as the inaugural players for the upcoming Embercoil TTRPG campaign, which recently held its first character-building session.
The Atlas Brew 205 from Star Atlas TV – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qg75t70QNuE
In the latest Atlas Brew (#114), the Star Atlas development team announced a strategic shift in focus, prioritizing the upcoming C4 Public Test Realm (PTR) release over further major HoloSAGE updates to unify the community’s testing efforts. During the stream, live gameplay footage demonstrated C4 PTR mechanics, including subwarping, fleet actions, and user interface enhancements showing ship stats like shields, cargo, and fuel. Additionally, CEO Michael Wagner joined to detail the upcoming “Installing Democracy” module. This landmark governance feature allows players to upgrade starbases to Tier 5 to unlock diplomatic wings, enabling POLIS holders to delegate PVP shares and participate in local starbase revenue distribution. Wagner also discussed the revolutionary introduction of 24/7 AI-powered player agents that can autonomously manage assets and execute resource arbitrage on a player’s behalf, providing a massive boost to the in-game economy. Finally, the team invited the community to “The Lore Keepers” event tomorrow to dive deeper into the game’s evolving lore and RPG campaign mechanics.
Lore Keepers 21 from Star Atlas TV – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=of7gPCCU0Z4
Star Atlas has officially confirmed its highly anticipated Town Hall event for Thursday, May 30, 2024, at 16:00 UTC, where developers will share ecosystem updates across their product suite, with a spotlight on Project Sollus, SAGE, and Companion. Alongside this major event, the community’s “The Lore Keepers” custom tabletop RPG campaign, *Embercoil*, is gearing up for its first official session set on the high-risk frontier planet of Evisco. The gamemaster detailed a custom D6-based ruleset featuring core attributes like Body, Reflex, Mind, Instinct, and Presence, tailored to the unique traits of Star Atlas species—such as the highly customizable MUD, mecha-piloting Punabs, and honor-bound Sogmians. In an interactive community call-to-action, members are invited to suggest rival NPC factions, such as the Jorvik pirates or MUD security forces, to enter the Evisco ruins and compete against the player party for valuable “liquid light” rewards. This campaign offers players and spectators alike an immersive way to engage with the deep lore of the Star Atlas metaverse.
