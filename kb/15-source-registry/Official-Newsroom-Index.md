## Quality and archival notes

- The newsroom page contains duplicate or test entries, including a 2022 Sandbox test item and a 2026 staged Vimeo test. These should not be treated as substantive historical releases without article-level review.
- The page order is not strictly chronological; dates above were normalized from the published index.
- Every item should eventually receive a source record and, where significant, an event entry.
- Official announcements document what ATMTA or Star Atlas publicly stated at the time. They do not alone prove implementation, adoption, financial performance, or continued status.
