## Overview

**Azut** stands as a beacon of progress and harmony. After awakening on [Ioki](../geography/sectors/eternity.md), the Ustur recognized the need for a **new home** — Ioki was deemed **too sacred and potentially perilous** for a bustling civilization hub. Azut offered a harmonious blend of natural beauty and resource abundance, becoming the Regency's largest urban center outside the homeworld.

Colony established **~2384**, Azut grew rapidly into the administrative heart of the [Ustur Regency](../factions/ustur.md) — a hub of commerce, education, and transit connecting the inner colonies to the wider galaxy.

---

## Francor Metropolis

The crown jewel of Azut is **Francor Metropolis** — a **planet-wide city** constructed **~2435** that blazes with billions of **Arco crystals**:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Scale** | Planet-wide — the Regency's largest urban center outside Ioki |
| **Arco Crystals** | Both **functional** (power generation, computational processing) and **aesthetic** (visual splendor) |
| **Role** | Cultural, spiritual, and technological center |
| **Architecture** | Ustur design philosophy — harmonizing technology with the Path of Enlightenment |
| **Industry** | Headquarters of **Opal Industries** (MUD/Ustur collaboration) |

### Why Azut, Not Ioki?

The Ustur built their civilization hub away from Ioki for deeply spiritual reasons:

- Ioki houses the **Pool of Reincarnation** — every Ustur is born from it at death; it cannot be risked
- The **Temple** beneath [Uvana](../geography/sectors/eternity.md) is too dangerous for mass habitation (a massive alien metal maze of unknown origin)
- Ioki's barren surface and unique geology (salar deserts, pinkish oceans) make large-scale urbanization impractical
- Azut offered the resources, climate, and space for a fully realized Ustur civilization

## Society

Azut's inhabitants uphold the **Path of Enlightenment** with unwavering dedication — a population that is both technologically advanced and spiritually rich. The sector serves as:

- A hub of **economic activity** (Opal Industries, transit commerce)
- A center for **philosophical growth** (Path of Enlightenment practice)
- The **educational gateway** for young Ustur before they choose a cultivation sect
- A **transit hub** connecting the inner Ustur colonies to the wider galaxy

### The Still Water Doctrine

The [Still Water Doctrine](../factions/still_water_doctrine.md) — a cultivation sect focused on intellectual inquiry and knowledge accumulation — maintains a strong presence on Azut. Their scholars and researchers complement the metropolis's academic institutions, making Azut one of the most intellectually vibrant worlds in Galia.
