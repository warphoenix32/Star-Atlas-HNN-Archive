## Biology & Nature

- **Metagenic Anomalies**: Born during the creation of Iris (collision of 7 dwarf planets).
  - **Metagenic** = substances capable of **self-alteration, adaptation, and transformation on a molecular or atomic level**. This is the unifying property across every Tufa in Star Atlas — regardless of brood, all Tufa materials operate on a metagenic level
  - This is why Tufas are so powerful and also why they are so **precious** as resources
  - Different broods of Tufas exist across Galia Expanse — they do not all behave the same or follow the same logic, but the metagenic core is universal
- **Diet**: Feed on **Genesium** (Free Energy generated by Iris).
- **Dependence**: Cannot stay away from Iris's influence for long.
  - Less complex Tufa = shorter survival time away from Iris.
  - **Genesium Tanks**: Prepared structures to hold pure genesium for distant campaigns (e.g., Season 0).
- **As Resources**: Killing higher-tiered Tufas with very concentrated **genesium reserves** yields rare metagenic materials that can be used to craft extraordinary ship components and equipment. These materials are among the rarest in the galaxy and require specialized skills to synthesize

### Creation Formula

- Tufa are created when **3 variables** combine in stable proportions:
  1. **Direct Genesium radiation**
  2. **Organic matter**
  3. **High concentration of minerals**
- Genesium is **"the glue"** that binds the Tufa together
- Most Tufa created during Iris flare cycles are simply **undone** — the proportions are wrong, overwhelming the body or lacking enough conscience for basic tasks
- No two Tufa are the same — their creation doesn't follow a genetic tree

### Infestation Mechanics

- **Infestation**: Inject genesium into technological devices or creatures to **fuse** with them.
  - **Assimilation**: Use target's tech to empower vessels. Sacrifice lowest level Tufa ("Eggs") for this.
  - **Fusion**: Can absorb other beings. Lower-ranking Tufa routinely fuse/absorb others for **daily chores** — they have nearly no individual sense of self
- **Complexity scaling**: The more complex or large the structure, the **higher-ranking** the Tufa must be to infest it. Complex structures (like ships) require high-ranking Tufa to **coordinate the assimilation** and unify different Tufa masses
- Tufa build their colonies and civilization by **infesting other species' technology**

## Hierarchy & Society

### The 7 Titans (The Fathers)

- **Origin**: Each of the **7 Dwarf Planets** that collided with Iris became a Titan.
- **Nature**: Highly intelligent, god-like power, absolute control over their brood.
- **Self-Perception**: Titans envision themselves as **actual gods** — they rarely communicate with inferior Tufa or other beings, except through their **Listeners**
- **Mental Link**: Communicate via radiation beams (perceptible only to Tufa). Even barely intelligent Tufa can understand a Titan's desire and act accordingly
- **Genesium Synthesis**: Only Titans (and some Awakened) can synthesize genesium spontaneously near Iris.
- **Feudal System**: Titans replenish high-ranking officers ("feudal lords") who then control the brood. If high-ranking Tufa spend too much genesium creating lesser Tufa, they must return to their Titan for replenishment — or devour other Tufa / find natural genesium veins
- **Status**: 6 are in the Cataclysm. **1 escaped** (Thionae) and hides in an asteroid field near Iris.
- **Critical Vulnerability**: If something happens to the **rift inside Iris**, all Tufa and Titans would **collapse**. The Titans know this — finding a solution to this existential dependency is a core motivation

### The Broods

- **Variability**: Traits reflect the Titan's personality (some uniform, some chaotic).
- **Listeners**: Special breed tasked with directing the mindless hordes on behalf of their Titans.
- **Awakened Tufa**: High-ranking, fully developed sense of self. Only Awakened Tufa and Titans can synthesize genesium spontaneously
- **Lesser Tufa**: Hive-mind, no sense of self, expendable.
- **Loyalty**: Most Tufa are born from their Titan's own bio-matter, mineral matter, and conscience. Loyalty to their Titan is **absolute** — a Titan can reclaim the fragment of conscience from any Tufa born from them within their territory

## The Chalbok Brood (Season 0 Antagonist)

- **Progenitor**: **Chalbok** (Master Tactician, Cold, Revenge-obsessed).
- **Nature**: The **most hostile** brood. Highly tactical and organized.
- **Motivation**: Revenge for the Convergence War (Chalbok's brood was heavily harvested).
- **Composition**: Small variety of species but unmatched synergy. High number of **sentient Tufa** (Quality > Quantity).
- **Ioki Invasion**: Led by Chalbok's **5 Direct Sons** (each unique).

## Mechanics

### Control Points

- **Function**: Fragments of genesium established to sustain Tufa presence far from Iris.
- **Dynamics**: Grow if unchecked (spawning higher Tufa). If destroyed, local Tufa wither/die.
- **Deployment**: Low-tier points fall randomly from the sky during invasions.

## History

- **~2440**: **The Birth of the Cataclysm** — a rogue planet dense in ORE draws in 7 child planets to collide in a remote region; the collision creates free energy (**Genesium**) and births the 7 Titans
- **~2441**: **Iris** acquires a certain degree of self-awareness
- **~2443**: The **7 Titans** awaken to full consciousness — each a fragment of the 7 colliding dwarf planets, exposed to Iris's nascent awareness. They perceive themselves as gods and begin establishing feudal control over the emerging Tufa ecosystem
- **~2447**: First **non-aware Tufa** are born from Iris (no self-awareness)
- **~2457**: The first **Tufa with awareness** are born — Iris perceives them as "Her Children"
- **~2460**: A conscious Tufa discovers the tribes of **Oxir**; the **Cult of Iris** begins among the Oxir people
- **Convergence War**: Harvested for Genesium by galactic factions. Shielded by Sogmians.
- **~2525**: A **Conscient (Awakened) Tufa** stays behind after the Cataclysm is sealed, founding the **Gate Garrison** — a formidable collective whose mission is to **ensure no one ever reaches Iris again**. The Gate Garrison becomes a multi-species bounty-hunter organization operating from the HRZ, allied with Xianyang-VI, and **actively working to prevent Ingbus from returning to Iris's realm**.
- **Season 0 (The Invasion of Ioki)**:
  - **Commander**: Chalbok (serving his own Brood needs).
  - **Objective**: Seize the **Reincarnation Pool** in the Ustur Temple + search for **artifacts related to the Ustur creation period**.
  - **Reason**: He sensed residual energy akin to Iris and sought it as a **substitute power source** for his brood — a solution to the Tufa's existential dependency on the Iris rift.
  - **Note**: He does NOT know the Ustur origin secret; just sensed the raw power.
