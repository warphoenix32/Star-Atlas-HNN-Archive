Join the Community Hub on Discord
Before diving into the available tools, consider joining our official Discord server. This is the central hub for community discussions, theorycrafting, and resource sharing.
Community-Created Tools and Resources
Star Atlas has a passionate and creative community building tools, guides, and automation systems that make your journey through SAGE Starbased easier and more efficient.
Whether you're optimizing your fleets, navigating the map, automating workflows, or learning the fundamentals, these community tools are here to help.
Gameplay Tools & Automation
EVE Eye – Starbased Map & Fleet Management Tool
https://atlas.eveeye.com/A versatile companion tool for SAGE Starbased that helps you visualize the universe, monitor your assets, and streamline many parts of your gameplay
ATOM – Automation Tool
https://medium.com/@atom_tool/atom-play-star-atlas-sage-without-limits-e6bc35282cf2ATOM is a community-built automation system for SAGE Starbased that allows players to streamline complex gameplay actions.With Atom Core (open-source) and Atom Cloud (web platform), players can create automated “routes” for tasks such as fleet movement, mining loops, resource transport, crew logistics, and other repeatable workflows, all without manually executing every action.
Ideal for players managing multiple fleets or long-term operations.
SLY Assistant – Browser Automation Script
https://github.com/Swift42/SLY-AssistantThe SLY Lab Assistant is a community-made browser script that automates certain repetitive workflows in SAGE Starbased. Installed through TamperMonkey, it can help simplify tasks such as scanning loops, fleet resupply routines, and other basic fleet operations, allowing players to reduce manual clicking during long sessions.
Best suited for players who enjoy lightweight automation directly in their browser.
SA Dispatcher
https://sa-dispatcher.com/A management assistant for organizing and dispatching your fleets.
Perfect for medium to large fleet operators.

Optimize your mining fleet and prepare for the unique challenges in SAGE Labs with NANCY, a specialized tool that offers data-driven recommendations.Visit NANCY
