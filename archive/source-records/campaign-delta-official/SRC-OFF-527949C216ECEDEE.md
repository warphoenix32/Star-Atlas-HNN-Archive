## Classified Claims

- Mining in Starbased is essential for collecting resources that fuel your fleet, crafting, and Starbase upgrades.
- Preparing Your Fleet Undock your fleet in a sector with a Starbase where mining is possible.
- In empty sectors, you can only scan for SDUs mining is not available there.
- Starting Mining Select Your Fleet & SectorNavigate to the sector where your fleet is located and select the fleet you wish to mine with.
- Initiate MiningClick “Start Mining” from the fleet menu.You’ll see: Available resources in the sector Mining rates per resource Estimated time to fill your cargo Review the details, then click “Initiate Mining” and approve the transaction in your wallet.
- Stopping Mining When your cargo is full or you wish to relocate: Click “Harvest Resources and Stop Mining.” Approve the transaction in your wallet.
- Your mined resources are now in your fleet’s cargo.
- Unloading Resources Dock your fleet at a Starbase to unload your mined resources into your Local Inventory, ready for crafting or trade.
