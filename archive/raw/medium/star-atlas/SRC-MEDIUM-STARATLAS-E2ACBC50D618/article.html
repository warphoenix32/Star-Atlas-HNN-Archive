Star Atlas Metaverse Expands to Every Street and Screen of the World with Aliens

Star Atlas is teleporting a dancing alien into the homes and streets around the world. With advanced distributed AR technology, anyone can now teleport an alien android to dance with to any location in the world at the touch of a button by visiting ustur.staratlas.com. The AR experience was released as part of the next growth phase of the Star Atlas metaverse, launched exclusively at the Bitcoin Conference, June 4th-5th 2021 in Miami, Florida.

Simply by clicking a link or scanning a QR code on their smartphone, the phone users can summon a sentient extraterrestrial through their web browser, Instagram or Facebook app at ustur.staratlas.com/miami.

Star Atlas is also giving metaverse enthusiasts the ability to become aliens themselves by turning their faces to Ustur (on Instagram or Facebook) and Photoli (on Instagram or Facebook), two mysterious alien species. Using advanced Face Filter technology, anyone can now morph into a metaverse extraterrestrial or use it in their video call, stream or on Instagram/ Facebook story.

The alien, Ustur, is dancing and grooving to the tune produced by Jeff Peters, a songwriter and producer for top artists like Machine Gun Kelly, Steven Tyler, 24kGoldn, iann dior and Ty Dolla $ign. He has written and produced with the top names in songwriting, Grammy winners and Billboard top 10 writers. His extensive opus of songs and compositions have been used for the Academy Awards, the Voice, Good Morning America, HBO, the LEGO Ninjago Movie, and all major broadcast networks. As an accomplished arranger he orchestrated Nas’ Illmatic for a 50 piece orchestra and arranged over 50 songs for the Hipster Orchestra. For Star Atlas, he composed the metaverse beat with a smooth digital vibe that befits an android alien, and giving the tune distinct character with a re-imagined take on the “first contact” if it were to happen in 2021.

To mark entering a new phase of its exponential growth at the Bitcoin Conference, Star Atlas is also releasing a Star Atlas Arcade Game, the project with roots in its Star Atlas Arcade competition for Solana ecosystem developers. The arcade game, running online on the Solana blockchain, is going to be playable online and at a venue during the Bitcoin conference in Miami, FL.

Star Atlas Alien Dance Challenge

Accompanying the release of the dancing alien, Star Atlas is issuing an Alien Dance Challenge to all citizens of the world with the objective of good fun and keeping future intergalactic relations peaceful, and adding a prize pool of 1500 USDC to reward the best entries.

Anyone can participate in this challenge by teleporting the dancing alien to their vicinity using the smartphone app, recording a video with the dancing Ustur and posting it on Instagram or Facebook by tagging the Star Atlas profile (@staratlasgame on Instagram or @StarAtlasMMO on Facebook) to share the intergalactic fun.

If making the Ustur Alien Dance part of your Instagram or Facebook Story, save the story and send it to us.

If you are tech-savvy, you can also upload the video to Twitter instead and tag @StarAtlas.

If you have your eyes on the prize, make sure the video falls into one of the three prize-winning categories below.

The best contestants will win a prize in one of the following three categories:

  1. A prize for the best Ustur Dance Duo, awarded for dancing in tandem with the Ustur
  2. A prize for the best dance with an Elder, awarded for an Ustur dance with a senior person
  3. A prize for the best Ustur Dance Location, awarded for the best and most creative physical location of the Ustur’s dance, for example the top of the Eiffel tower.

Star Atlas will select the winners based on originality and community feedback, and announce the prizes on June 11 2021.

Meet the Aliens

The dancing alien that also appears on one of the face filters belongs to the Ustur, a race of androids spawned by an advanced, multi-generational AI who sought to express themself through physicalized form. They are mechanical beings with a developed sense of identity, culture and fashion. Their anatomy articulates in a relatable way to organic substrate beings to convey demeanor and to emote. They enjoy establishing bonds with all intelligent life and satisfying higher spiritual exploration.

The second alien appearing on the face filters is a member of the Photoli, a race of highly advanced aliens of light. Their natural state is navigating the universe in the form of light energy. Their ability to maintain a corporeal form with amorphous anatomical shapes and halos conveys expressions to other species. During galactic explorations, they are known to be confused with deities by more primitive civilizations. One Photoli rogue scout in particular played a pivotal role in advancing humankind to the stars.

ABOUT STAR ATLAS

Star Atlas is a virtual gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state of the art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video game, and decentralized financial technologies.

Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic-quality visuals. Blockchain technology using the Solana protocol established a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. NFTs obtained and traded within Star Atlas creates an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership. To learn more, please visit staratlas.com
