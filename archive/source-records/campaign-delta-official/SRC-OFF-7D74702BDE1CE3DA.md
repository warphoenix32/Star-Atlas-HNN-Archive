## Preserved Official Text

Faction Fleetfeature allows you to stake your ships to earn passive rewards. Enlisting a ship is quick and easy—and you can earn ATLAS while your assets are actively contributing to the Star Atlas economy.
Enlisting a Ship for the First Time
If this is the first time you're enlisting a specific ship model, follow these steps:
1. Go to the Faction Fleet
Visit: https://play.staratlas.com/fleet/
2. Click on “Ship Enlistment”
You'll find this tab near the top of the Faction Fleet page.
3. Choose Your Ship
Scroll through the available ships in your wallet.Click the “Enlist Ship” button under the ship you want to stake.
4. Set Enlistment Details
A popup will appear where you can:
Select the quantity of ships to enlist
Add resources (Ammunition, Food, Fuel, Toolkits) as neededYou can choose how much of each to load into your fleet.
5. Click “Enlist Ship”
Once your options are set, click the “Enlist Ship” button.
6. Approve the Transaction
Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Once approved, your ship(s) will be active in the Faction Fleet.
Enlisting More of a Ship You Already Have in Faction Fleet
If you already have some ships of a specific model enlisted and want to add more to that existing fleet, follow these steps:
1. Go to the Faction Fleet
Visit: https://play.staratlas.com/fleet/
2. Locate Your Existing Fleet
Find the fleet containing the ship model you want to add more of.
3. Click “Manage Fleet”
This will open the detailed view of your current fleet.
4. Open the “Manage Ship” Tab
Click on the Manage Ship tab within the fleet interface.
5. Enlist More Ships
Under the “Enlist More Ships” section, enter the quantity of additional ships you’d like to add to this fleet.
6. Click “Update”
Once you’ve set the quantity, click the Update button.
7. Approve the Transaction
Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Your additional ships will now be enlisted into the existing fleet.
💡 Looking for a better way to earn with your fleet?
Faction Fleet is a legacy feature and will eventually be phased out.If you're looking for a more sustainable way to earn passive income with your ships, consider renting them out to other players on the marketplace
