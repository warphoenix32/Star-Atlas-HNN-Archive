## Overview

Located on the outermost rim of the recognized ONI territories, **Night Window** presents an intriguing anomaly. Its primary star has **gone dark well before its expected life cycle**, plunging its surrounding planets into an unending night. Against all astrophysical understanding, the star has ceased luminosity prematurely — a mystery that has drawn researchers from the [Iris Academy](../factions/iris_academy.md) and the [Council of Peace](../factions/council_of_peace.md).

---

## The Photoli Connection

> *"The energy harvested from this star enabled individual Photoli to explore far beyond Galia, ultimately leading to the discovery of Earth (~2120)."*

**Confirmed (Aeon Timeline):** Night Window was the **landing zone for the Photoli fleet** when they first crossed into Galia (~2080). The [Photoli](../species/photoli.md) entered our galaxy through Night Window, then spread outward. Vast reserves of planetary resources remain seemingly untouched — drained of **stellar energy** instead.

The energy harvested from this star enabled individual Photoli — like **[Ahr](../species/photoli.md)** — to explore far beyond the Galia Expanse, ultimately leading to the discovery of Earth (~2120) and the chain of events that brought humanity to the stars.

## 🔴 Archon Secret: The Photoli Harvest

- **The Truth**: The Photoli **deliberately extinguished the star** to recharge/replenish the energy they spent traveling to Galia from the Andromeda galaxy
- **Who Knows**: Only the Photoli themselves. No other species suspects this
- **Implication**: The Photoli killed an entire star system's light for their own survival. This aligns with their hidden pragmatism beneath the peaceful facade
- **Scale**: Extinguishing a star is an act of energy manipulation that dwarfs even the [Cataclysm](../geography/the_cataclysm.md)'s phenomena. It reveals the Photoli as the most powerful species in Galia — and the best at hiding it

## Life in Darkness

In the millennia since the star went dark, an entire **ecosystem has developed in adaptation to permanent darkness**:

- All sorts of creatures exist, most dangerous
- Populated areas are safe; **unpopulated zones are avoided** by everyone
- Bioluminescent organisms have filled the ecological niches left by photosynthetic life
- The Mierese (with their superior senses) and certain Punaab breeds navigate better than most in Night Window's perpetual twilight

## Political Influence

### ONI Frontier Sector

Night Window sits on ONI's outer rim — a buffer zone between ONI Safe Space and the uncharted beyond. The Photoli maintain a quiet presence here, drawn back to the sector of their arrival for reasons they do not publicly share. The Iris Academy research station provides a legitimate cover for investigation into the dead star — investigation that the Photoli subtly ensure never reaches the true conclusion.
