## Preserved Official Text

In SAGE Labs Starbased, Local Markets are starbase-specific trading hubs that allow players to buy and sell items directly within the game environment. Each starbase in the Galia Expanse operates as an independent market, with prices influenced by local supply and demand dynamics.
Key Features of Local Markets:
Starbase-Specific Economies: Each starbase functions as a distinct market, creating unique economic conditions across the galaxy. Prices for goods can vary between starbases based on localized supply and demand.
In-Game Transactions: Players can conduct trades without removing assets from the game, streamlining the process of buying and selling items within the SAGE Labs Starbased environment.
Career Opportunities: The introduction of Local Markets paves the way for new professions, such as freighting, where players transport goods between starbases to exploit price differences and maximize profits.
