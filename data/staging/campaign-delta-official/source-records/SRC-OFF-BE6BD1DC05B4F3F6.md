## Preserved Official Text

The Star Atlas Showroom now supports in-game purchasing of ships directly from the Galactic Marketplace, without leaving the Unreal Engine 5 environment
How It Works
Unlock your wallet in the Showroom
Go to any terminal in the game world
Open the Marketplace tab
Browse available ships and complete your purchase
Ships you buy in-game are instantly added to your inventory and can be summoned and flown right away.
What Can I Buy?
🛸 Ships only (for now) other asset types like crew or resources may be added in the future
Why It Matters
This feature removes friction between discovery and gameplay. You no longer need to leave the game to visit the Marketplace website. It’s a step toward fully integrated in-game commerce in Star Atlas.
