## Overview

Eol-Garadar stands as a **shining beacon**, renowned as the home of **noble heroes and champions of liberty**.

In a galaxy where many sectors yielded to avarice, subjugation, and turmoil, Eol-Garadar emerged as a stalwart defender of hope and order — even **outside the welfare of the Council of Peace** — in the aftermath of the Convergence War.

> [!NOTE]
> **COP Jurisdiction Canon**: Eol-Garadar is not rebelling against the COP nor deliberately neutral — the COP's jurisdiction simply **does not function in the MRZ**. Eol-Garadar fills the justice vacuum by doing the best they can on their own terms.

---

## Foundational Principles

### Universal Rights

Eol-Garadar embraces all, irrespective of past allegiances or political leanings. Its **only demand** is adherence to foundational laws framed as:

> **Essential rights intrinsic to all sentient beings** — transcending cultural, temporal, or political divisions.

### The Tenets

- **Freedom of expression**
- **Poetic justice** — not rigid legalism
- **Disdain for rigid formality**
- **Strong belief in individual responsibility**

> **Alignment: Chaotic Good.** Eol-Garadar follows justice from the heart, not from written code. Think D&D Chaotic Good — they do what is right even when it breaks convention.

---

## Military Doctrine: The DAC

Eol-Garadar's military operates as a **Decentralized Autonomous Corporation (DAC)** — a decentralized army whose members are **voted and elected through governance wars.**

With unwavering commitment, the DAC dispatches its **valorous legions** against slavers and other malevolent forces — **not for riches or glory, but in pursuit of the greater good.**

This altruistic ethos magnetizes countless souls from diverse origins, each aspiring to contribute energies and resources to a shared noble purpose.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The DAC structure means Eol-Garadar's military leadership is not inherited or appointed — it is **earned through governance participation**. This is decentralized warfare with democratic legitimacy.

---

## Political Standing

- Their principled stand has **drawn the ire of numerous adversaries** across the ideological landscape
- The unyielding spirit of Eol-Garadar has, thus far, **repelled any threats**
- A populace **deeply trusting of its leadership**, and a leadership **utterly devoted to its people**
- This makes Eol-Garadar an **indomitable presence** on the political stage of Galia

### Liberation Origin (~2523)

- **~2523**: Eolus and the Garadar DAC discover a sector under the **oppressive rule of the Pavo Royal Family**
- Following the death of the Pavo Royal Family during **The Night** (~2522), the sector's oppressive apparatus begins to fracture
- After a **long conflict** against the remaining loyalists, Eolus and the DAC join forces with the **civilian population**
- The civilians unite with the Garadar DAC, and the sector becomes **Eol-Garadar** — the **first DAC-owned sector** in Galia Expanse

> [!NOTE]
> Eol-Garadar doesn't reject the COP — the COP simply **cannot reach the MRZ**. Eol-Garadar fills the void with its own brand of justice.
