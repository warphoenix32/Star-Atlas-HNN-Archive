Star Atlas To Sponsor Play to Earn Game Festival
Star Atlas will illuminate the metaverse as an event sponsor for the Play to Earn Game Festival. The festival, which will take place on July 12th in the popular virtual world Cryptovoxels, will be a celebration of all things blockchain gaming with a special focus on the development of the play-to-earn economy.

For blockchain gaming enthusiasts, investors, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders alike, the event is an essential opportunity to connect with major players in the blockchain gaming and NFT space. They can also discover the newest games in the play-to-earn ecosystem. This July’s event marks the third Play to Earn Game Festival since the inaugural event in November. The last event in March garnered 13,000 visitors, instantly making it the most popular location in all of Cryptovoxels.
This month’s festival will without a doubt resonate with our intergalactic community. Visitors can expect the festival to be an interactive event in a virtual space station. Here visitors can solve puzzles to advance to the next stage in a story-driven experience. Participants are able to join the festival from any web browser across desktop, mobile, and VR platforms.
The Play to Earn Game Festival is all about bringing together innovators within the blockchain gaming community. The event offers the most exciting projects in the space a chance to share their work with a larger audience. We can’t wait to see what Star Atlas brings to the event and we’re excited for our fans to have a chance to interact with such a major player in the play-to-earn space, said Robert Hoogendoorn, founder of Play to Earn.
As an event sponsor, Star Atlas will have a prominent presence at the festival. Fans and visitors will also be eligible for exclusive giveaway prizes featuring the legendary Neil Armstrong meta-poster. Visitors should also keep an eye out for special voxelized items from Star Atlas such as a spaceship on display at the venue.
The Star Atlas metaverse is rapidly growing and we are thrilled to welcome new audiences into our world at the Play to Earn Game Festival. We’re excited to be in such great company with the Play to Earn community and look forward to showcasing the limitless possibilities of our NFTs and our brand to a broader community within the blockchain gaming ecosystem.
Said Michael Wagner, CEO of Star Atlas
ABOUT THE FESTIVAL
The next gen Play to Earn Game Festival will take place starting from 12th of July. The event will take one week.
Visitors will find something new at the festival every day, for example there will be new giveaways. In addition the festival location will change based on community actions, and a story unfolds. Read the prelude story here.
Unlike in old times of gaming conventions, this festival is open 24/7. It’s interactive, fun, and accessible for everybody from a web browser on a smartphone, desktop computer or VR helmet. For more information on how to participate in the festival, visit the Play to Earn festival page.
ABOUT STAR ATLAS
Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming Metaverse emerging from the confluence of state of the art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video game, and decentralized financial technologies.
Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. Blockchain technology using the Solana protocol establishes a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas create an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership. To learn more, visit StarAtlas.com, join a faction at Play.StarAtlas.com and send your spaceships on a deep space mission by enrolling them into a Faction Fleet.
