## Preserved Official Text

A POLIS Improvement Proposal (PIP) is the official format used by the Star Atlas DAO to propose and vote on changes that affect the Star Atlas metaverse.
PIPs are formal proposals that may include ideas for new features, economic policies, DAO processes, or the use of treasury funds. Each PIP outlines the rationale for the proposed change and is subject to community review and on-chain voting by POLIS holders.
While not all community discussions result in PIPs, any impactful change that falls under the governance of the DAO will eventually be structured and voted on as a PIP.
What Makes a PIP Different?
Formal structure: PIPs follow a consistent format to ensure transparency and clarity.
Decentralized input: PIPs are reviewed and debated by the community and the elected DAO Council.
Binding decisions: Once a PIP passes on-chain voting, it becomes an official directive for the Star Atlas ecosystem.
Want to Learn More?
The first DAO Council created a comprehensive knowledge base on PIPs, POLIS Voting Power (PVP), and Star Atlas governance: https://star-atlas-dao.gitbook.io/knowledge-base
