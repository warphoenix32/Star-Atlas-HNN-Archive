## Overview

The most powerful ex-colony in terms of **military might**, **Xianyang-VI** survived post-war turmoil through a pivotal alliance with the **Gate Garrison**, a formidable bounty-hunter collective from the High-Risk Zone. **~2529**: Xianyang pivots into a **military dictatorship** to face the chaotic state of the Medium Risk Zone after the Convergence War.

## Key Lore

### The Gate Garrison Alliance

- **Who**: A formidable **bounty-hunter collective** from the HRZ.
- **Composition**: Human-founded, but **accepts all species**. The best-trained warriors in the galaxy.
- **~2547**: After being **nearly overwhelmed by alien invaders**, Xianyang-VI forges an alliance with Gate Garrison
- **Deal**: In exchange for a strategic foothold in the region, the Garrison provided protective cover and **support for the development of military technology using HRZ materials**
- **Effect**: Deterred aggressors eyeing newly independent MRZ territories.
- **~2554**: The Gate Garrison **formally relocates its headquarters to Xianyang-VI**, cementing the sector as a military powerhouse

### The Xianyang Enforcers

- **Origin**: Xianyang's own **mercenary legion**, inspired by the Gate Garrison's model.
- **Scope**: Operates across **every skirmish line** in Galia Expanse.
- **Rite of Passage**: **~2543**: Xianyang starts **mandatory public army service** — every citizen, upon reaching maturity, must dedicate **one year** to the Enforcers.
- **~2569**: Xianyang-VI becomes the **strongest ex-colony of MUD** in military power and formally creates the **Xianyang Enforcers** as a **mercenary group** present in most of the battlefronts of Galia, to help **finance their activities**
- **Alumni Network**: Many veterans leave and become **independent mercenaries** across the galaxy. This is how the Enforcers' presence spans every conflict — it's not a centralized army but a **galactic network of alumni**.
- **Reputation**: Acclaimed across the galaxy.

### Military Expansionism

- Xianyang has been **intensifying military prowess** and influence across the MRZ.
- Post-Pergamos COP offensive: Xianyang **threatened to openly defy Council resolution**.
- The unfolding political drama is keenly observed across sectors.
- Xianyang stands resilient, anchored in strength.

## Political Influence

- **Alignment**: Independent (military-first)
- **Allies**: Gate Garrison (HRZ bounty hunters, multi-species)
- **Faction**: Xianyang Enforcers (mercenary legion + alumni network)
- **Stance**: Anti-COP, willing to defy Council
