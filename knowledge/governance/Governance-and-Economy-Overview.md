# Star Atlas Governance and Economy Overview

## Governance model

Official current documentation describes Star Atlas as using a dual-token system:

- **POLIS** is the governance token. Holders can lock POLIS for voting power, participate in DAO decisions and influence treasury use and economic policy.
- **ATLAS** is the utility and medium-of-exchange token used in the game economy and for rewards. Official documentation describes it as inflationary by design, with minting and burning intended to respond to game adoption and economic growth.

These descriptions represent current official policy language. Actual authority, implementation limits and the division of control among ATMTA, the DAO and the Star Atlas Foundation require proposal-by-proposal and program-level review.

## DAO portal

The governance portal exposes four major public categories:

- POLIS lockers
- Proposals
- Economy
- Treasury

The proposal system should be indexed independently with proposal ID, author, dates, requested authority or funding, vote totals, quorum rules, execution status and conflicts of interest.

The initial evidence-qualified proposal index is maintained in the [PIP Registry](PIP-Registry.md). Atlas Brew discussed PIP-1 while it was still upcoming, and the official publication later introduced it as a proposal. Neither record alone proves approval or execution. ## Treasury custody and execution boundary

An official support record published 2025-12-02 states that marketplace and SAGE fees fund the DAO Treasury and that ATMTA safeguarded the treasury at that publication snapshot while a multisignature structure was being prepared. This establishes the official description of custody at that date; it does not prove later transfer, audit completion, or proposal execution. Atlas Brew separately discussed Foundation operations in a transcript without normalized speaker attribution, so that discussion is retained as community context rather than authority evidence. [SRC-OFF-BFA6D14D54A9AA6A](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-BFA6D14D54A9AA6A.md) [SRC-ATLAS-BREW-0060, 00:17:53–00:19:02; SEG-ATLAS-BREW-0060-0009](../../archive/semantic/atlas-brew/segment-index.json)

An Atlas Brew claim that a PIP-2 allocation moved after approval remains deferred until a treasury address, transaction, or official execution record is linked. It must not be converted from a discussion of execution into confirmed execution. ## Economic reporting

The DAO portal maintains a historical quarterly-report archive beginning with Q2 2022 and continuing through at least Q2 2026 during this sweep. The portal states that community dashboards are temporarily offline while Star Atlas migrates to an internal data solution powered by Star Atlas infrastructure.

Available report periods discovered:

- Q2–Q4 2022
- Q1–Q4 2023
- Q1–Q4 2024
- Q1–Q4 2025
- Q1–Q2 2026

Each report should be ingested as a dated snapshot. Later reports may revise definitions, methodologies or historical figures.

## Governance evidence rules

- A passed vote does not prove execution; record on-chain or treasury confirmation separately.
- An official proposal is evidence of a request and its rationale, not proof that every supporting claim is true.
- Token-weighted results must not be described as a headcount of community members.
- Abstention must be reported separately from opposition.
- Governance authority described in support materials must be checked against the actual programs, legal entities and executed decisions.
- Treasury values are time-sensitive and should always include date, asset composition and valuation method.
