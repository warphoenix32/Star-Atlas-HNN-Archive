## Preserved Official Text

If you no longer want to generate R4 with your Claim Stake or plan to sell it, you’ll need to withdraw it from Faction Claims first.
1. Go to the Faction Claims page and connect your wallet.
2. Find the Claim Stake you wish to withdraw and click “Manage”.
3. In the Manage Claims tab, click “Withdraw All Stakes”
4. Approve the transaction in your wallet.
⚠️ Withdrawing a Claim Stake will stop its R4 production immediately
