# Campaign Operations

Campaign instructions, audit notes, and promotion reports live here. Machine-readable campaign output is preserved under [`archive/`](../../archive/README.md).

New knowledge campaigns should adopt the [four-stage promotion contract](../docs/SIMPLIFIED-PROMOTION-PIPELINE.md) and consolidate routine status, promotion dispositions, validation, research gaps, and human decisions into one `campaign-report.json` plus a concise Markdown companion. Elevated-risk campaigns may add specialized artifacts when needed. Existing campaign structures remain valid.
