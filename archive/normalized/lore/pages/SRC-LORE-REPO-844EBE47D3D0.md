## Overview

The Zavijava Zone is an enigmatic sector where **time doesn't always obey conventional rules**. Under still-mysterious conditions, temporal distortions unpredictably ripple through the fabric of time, affecting the sector's ecosystem in myriad ways.

---

## History

### Public Knowledge

- Before the Convergence War, Zavijava was a **nondescript sector** of Galia
- **~2531**: After the war concluded and the **COP treaty was ratified**, peculiar time-based phenomena started occurring
- It was as though a **cosmic hex** had been cast upon the sector
- **Publicly, there is no clue** as to what causes the distortions

### The True Origin (Hidden Lore)

> [!CAUTION]
> This information is **not publicly known** in-universe. Only a handful of entities are aware of the true cause.

1. **HRZ relic hunters** stole an **unknown artifact** from one of the worlds dominated by the **Tufa Titan Bklok**
2. They **fled to Zavijava Zone** to hide, taking refuge in the depths of an **underground city**
3. Their plan was to wait for the heat to die down, then transport the artifact to **Yuldun Waste** and sell it through the **BMAH**
4. One of the thieves attempted to **steal the artifact for himself** and flee alone
5. He was **caught in the act** — a fight broke out among the group
6. During the combat, the **artifact was accidentally activated**
7. It began generating the **random temporal anomalies** that have plagued Zavijava ever since

> The artifact remains somewhere in that underground city, still active, still generating distortions — but slowly running out of energy.

---

## Temporal Anomalies

### Manifestations

The anomalies manifest in capricious, unpredictable ways:

- **Time acceleration**: Within a ~2km radius, days may pass as mere minutes
- **Time stasis**: Creatures can be suspended in a temporal freeze — seemingly frozen, only to resume life years later, **untouched by the passage of years**
- Effects are **localized and random** — no pattern has been identified
- No known way to predict, control, or prevent them

### Anomaly Status

- The phenomenon is **stable but weakening over time**
- The artifact's energy is being **spent** and there is **no nearby source to recharge it**
- Eventually, the anomalies will cease entirely — but the artifact itself will remain

### Impact on Daily Life

- Despite the tangible risk of eternal time freeze, inhabitants approach it with **humor and resignation**
- Popular local jest:

> *"The chances of being stuck in time are slimmer than snagging the galactic lottery. But should I ever hit that jackpot, I'll simply relocate from Zavijava, and all will be well!"*

## The Vortex Watchers

### Public Face

- A **coalition of neutral, esteemed scholars and temporal researchers**
- Have established a **research base** in the sector
- **Stated dual objective**:
  1. Decipher the mysteries of Zavijava's time anomalies
  2. Harness the potential power of **time manipulation**
- So far, their efforts have been **in vain**
- The **Council of Peace keeps tabs** on everything happening in Zavijava

### Secret Reality

> [!WARNING]
> The Vortex Watchers are **secretly funded and oriented by the Gate Garrison**. The Gate Garrison is discreetly searching for the artifact within the sector. The Gate Garrison's leader has **extreme personal interest** in recovering the artifact as quickly as possible.

- The "neutral research" is a **front** — the real objective is to locate the Tufa Titan artifact
- The Gate Garrison is the true power behind the Vortex Watchers
- Full Gate Garrison motivations will be revealed with that faction's deep lore

## Narrative Hooks

- **Artifact hunt**: The most powerful object in the sector is buried in an underground city, guarded by temporal anomalies it created
- **Gate Garrison's true agenda**: The bounty hunters aren't just funding research — they're running an extraction operation
- **COP vs. Gate Garrison**: COP monitors Zavijava but doesn't know about the artifact or Gate Garrison involvement
- **Bklok wants it back**: A Tufa Titan's artifact was stolen — does Bklok know where it ended up?
- **Ticking clock**: The artifact is losing energy — when it dies, the anomalies stop but the artifact becomes findable
- **Living time capsules**: People frozen in stasis from years ago could awaken into a completely changed galaxy
