## Overview

Located at the heart of the MUD Medium Zone, **Pergamos** stands as a behemoth of economic prowess — derived from abundant natural resources and deep connections to Galia's shadowy recesses. **~2529**: Formally becomes a **merchant republic**, with the major wealth of the sector concentrated in a few families.

## Key Lore

### The Grey Market

- **Accommodating stance** towards Jorvik and fringe entities.
- **~2542**: Pergamos formally begins **forming alliances with major MRZ factions**, becoming a **major harbor for Jorvik, ECOS, and other outlaw groups**
- **~2553**: Pergamos begins **financing warring factions** in the Abyd-IX conflict, making **tons of ATLAS** from war profiteering (`canon/geography/sectors/abyd.md`)
- **~2574**: The Merchants of Pergamos create the **Shadow Contracts** — a system of **illegal and untraceable trading and financing** instruments designed for the Medium Risk Zone's grey economy
- Trades in the universal currency of **ATLAS** and remains **indifferent to its origins**.
- Stark socioeconomic disparity: **10% of the population** (human aristocracy) controls the biggest share of wealth.
- **~2623**: In the aftermath of Pergamos's loss in the **Council of Peace intervention**, the **leading families** of the sector are **banished**, and a **new government**, compliant with COP jurisdiction, is formed in Pergamos

### The COP Offensive

- **~2623**: **Trigger**: COP (under Vaor Scarka's executive leadership) targeted Pergamos to curtail Jorvik/ECOS influence.
- **Weapon**: The **Mothership Cradle** (COP's Titan — most powerful vessel in the galaxy) was deployed.
- **Result**: Significant losses at the hands of Cradle's overwhelming force.
- **Shockwaves**: Ex-colonies (Mycenas, Xianyang) threatened to intervene if COP persisted.
- **Resolution**: Diplomatic negotiations led by **Redam and MUD Faction** halted hostilities.
- **Legal Counter**: Opos.eldr (Ustur, COP Legal Branch) enacted rulings demanding cessation.

### Current Status

- Amidst rubble and remnants of conflict, Pergamos **deliberates on its future trajectory**.
- The Cradle deployment demonstrated COP's willingness to use overwhelming force even in MRZ.

- **Ruling Class**: Human aristocracy (10%)
- **Key Event**: COP military intervention (Cradle deployment)
- **Diplomatic Allies**: Redam, Mycenas, Xianyang
