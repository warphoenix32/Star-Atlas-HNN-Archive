## Classified Claims

- If you need to remove your crew members from SAGE, whether to reorganize your assets or use them elsewhere, you can do so by following these simple steps.
- Step-by-Step Instructions to Withdraw Crew Members: Repatriate Your Crew to the Central Space Station: Before you can withdraw any crew members, ensure that they are all at the Central Space Station.
- Crew members can only be withdrawn from this location.
- Access Your Starbase Inventory: Once all your crew members are in the Central Space Station, head to the Port of Entry in SAGE.
- From there, click on Starbase Inventory to view your assets stored at the starbase.
- Select the Crew Section: Navigate to the Crew tab in the Starbase Inventory.
- This will display a list of all crew members currently available in the Central Space Station.
- Choose Crew Members to Withdraw: Select the crew members you wish to withdraw by clicking on their names.
- Withdraw from Starbase Crew Quarters: After selecting the desired crew members, click on WITHDRAW FROM STARBASE CREW QUARTERS.
- Approve the Transaction: Confirm the transaction in your connected wallet.
- Once approved, your crew members will be withdrawn from SAGE and returned to your wallet.
- Once the transaction is complete, your crew members will be removed from SAGE and will no longer be available for any in-game operations until you decide to import them again

## Preserved Official Text

If you need to remove your crew members from SAGE, whether to reorganize your assets or use them elsewhere, you can do so by following these simple steps.
Step-by-Step Instructions to Withdraw Crew Members:
Repatriate Your Crew to the Central Space Station: Before you can withdraw any crew members, ensure that they are all at the Central Space Station. Crew members can only be withdrawn from this location.
Access Your Starbase Inventory: Once all your crew members are in the Central Space Station, head to the Port of Entry in SAGE. From there, click on Starbase Inventory to view your assets stored at the starbase.
Select the Crew Section: Navigate to the Crew tab in the Starbase Inventory. This will display a list of all crew members currently available in the Central Space Station.
Choose Crew Members to Withdraw: Select the crew members you wish to withdraw by clicking on their names.
Withdraw from Starbase Crew Quarters: After selecting the desired crew members, click on WITHDRAW FROM STARBASE CREW QUARTERS.
Approve the Transaction: Confirm the transaction in your connected wallet. Once approved, your crew members will be withdrawn from SAGE and returned to your wallet.
What Happens Next?
Once the transaction is complete, your crew members will be removed from SAGE and will no longer be available for any in-game operations until you decide to import them again
