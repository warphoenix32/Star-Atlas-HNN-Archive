## Preserved Official Text

Star Atlas's DAC Platform is not just a static feature, but an evolving ecosystem with exciting upgrades on the horizon. These future enhancements are designed to strengthen collaboration, organization, and governance among Decentralized Autonomous Corporations (DACs), empowering them as key players in the Star Atlas universe.
Treasury Management
Future updates will introduce tools for managing DAC funds and assets more efficiently. Treasury management will become core to enabling DACs to coordinate long-term goals and large-scale initiatives with transparent, on-chain accounting.
Advanced Permissions Control
More granular access systems will be implemented to give DAC leaders the ability to assign roles and responsibilities. These controls will support diverse internal structures—from flat collectives to hierarchical organizations.
Resource Utilization
DACs will be able to pool and allocate shared assets, including ships, tools, and other resources, to complete missions or support members. This adds greater utility to coordinated play and boosts overall efficiency.
Financial Transactions
Planned improvements will enable seamless internal DAC transactions, such as distributing rewards, splitting costs, or paying contributors.
In-game Asset Management
Future integration with gameplay systems will let DACs collectively manage and deploy in-game NFTs like ships, structures, and claim stakes—opening new paths for cooperative strategies.
Democratic Voting Systems
DACs will be empowered with voting frameworks that allow members to have a say in internal decisions. This aligns with the decentralized spirit of Web3 gaming and encourages active participation.
Looking Ahead
The DAC Platform is foundational to Star Atlas’s multiplayer future. These upcoming enhancements will evolve DACs into fully-featured social and strategic organizations that can shape the economy, politics, and history of Galia.
