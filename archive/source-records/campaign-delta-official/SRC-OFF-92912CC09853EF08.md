## Preserved Official Text

When you first launch Holosim, you’ll be asked to set up your character. This step is quick but important, as it defines your name and faction in the game.
Steps to create your character:
Launch Holosim: from holosim.staratlas.com.
Enter your character name: choose carefully, because this cannot be changed later.
Pick your faction: MUD, ONI, or USTUR. Your faction decides your starting location and teammates, but all players have access to the same core gameplay.
Click “Create Character”: your character is now ready.
Optional: Battle Pass: after character creation, you can choose to purchase the Battle Pass or skip it and start playing for free.
Once your character is created, you can immediately begin quests and form fleets to explore the universe.
