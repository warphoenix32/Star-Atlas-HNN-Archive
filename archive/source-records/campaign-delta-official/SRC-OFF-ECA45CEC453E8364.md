## Preserved Official Text

Accessing the Roadmap
To stay informed about the latest developments, visit the official Star Atlas Roadmap: Star Atlas Roadmap
This portal provides detailed insights into current and upcoming features, categorized into phases such as Preseason, Season 0, and beyond. Each phase includes specific initiatives, from gameplay mechanics to infrastructure enhancements.
Key Highlights
Preseason: Focuses on foundational elements, including the introduction of the UE5 Showroom and initial gameplay loops.
Season 0: Expands on gameplay features, introducing new missions, assets, and economic systems.
Season 1 and Beyond: Plans for advanced features like PvP and PvE modes, enhanced DAO functionalities, and the introduction of new careers and professions. Productboard
Each roadmap item includes status indicators, detailed descriptions, and projected timelines, offering transparency into the development process.
