# Community and People Evidence Development

The operator expanded the initial Phase 5 portfolio from ten to eleven articles
by adding a community-and-people history.

Existing reviewed Knowledge is sufficient for a **bounded overview** of players,
guilds, creators, journalists, events and community memory. It is not sufficient
for an exhaustive biographical directory, a current guild roster or a history
of every rivalry.

The draft may explain documented community formation, guild institutions,
media, broadcasts, competition, relevant disagreement and previously
adjudicated identity examples. It may not infer current membership from tags,
merge similar handles, rank people, publish unsupported reputation claims or
promote off-topic politics and unrelated personal conflict.

Intergalactic Herald is not used as a central narrative source or publication
profile in this initial portfolio, following the operator's direction.

Future depth requires dated guild records, native Discord exports by channel,
complete event results, creator inventories and dedicated evidence packets for
individual biographies or rivalry case studies.
