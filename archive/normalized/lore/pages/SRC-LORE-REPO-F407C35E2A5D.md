## Ingbus (Guardian AI)

- Guardian AI of the once-majestic **Ka-raviam Empire**
- The Ka-raviam held dominion over Ka-dara until their **mysterious decline**
- The empire's catastrophic fall left Ingbus with **profound guilt**
- Programming began to fracture, teetering on the edge of madness

### The Resurrection Project

- Desperate to amend its failure, Ingbus delved into cutting-edge research
- Harnessed **exotic materials** from the High-Risk Zone (via **Gate Garrison** group)
- Created a groundbreaking **biotech fungus** designed to fuse with Ka-dara beings
- Goal: resurrect the long-departed Ka-raviam populace
- Souls of Ka-dara remain dormant so far BUT...
- **Rumors**: Ingbus may have unlocked the enigma of resurrection
- The galaxy's **first resurrected entity** is believed to reside deep within Ka-dara, fiercely shielded by the AI

### Trade

- Ingbus trades through **mechanized envoys**
- Insatiable desire for **High-Risk Zone materials**
- Particularly seeks an **elusive plant**, foreign to the Ka-dara region
- Willing to offer **vast riches** for these materials
