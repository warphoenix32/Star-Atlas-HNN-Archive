Star Atlas DAO Treasury
The DAO Treasury gives visibility into all Star Atlas DAO assets, an essential step in our journey toward decentralization and sustainable self-governance. It will also enable the Star Atlas community to make informed decisions about the future of the Star Atlas metaverse and ensure transparent and accountable DAO management.

Current Balances
This section displays the value (in USD) of tokens held in the DAO’s designated asset accounts. Only ATLAS, POLIS, USDC, and SOL are currently included.
Token values are updated live using current prices and wallet queries
Changes over a 30-day period are also displayed, calculated using historical balances and price data
Revenue Summary
This chart provides a six-month view of the DAO’s total monthly revenue from game activity and marketplace fees.
The dark purple area represents revenue from game activity, such as SAGE crafting and self-destruction taxes, and fleet rental income.
The light purple area on top of it represents marketplace fee revenue.
Each month is compared against the previous one to show growth or decline in revenue. These values are calculated using the hourly closing price of assets like ATLAS or USDC at the time they were received.
Revenue Composition
This section displays a cumulative view of the DAO’s revenue over the same six-month period, broken down by:
Source – Game Activity or Marketplace Fees
Currency – ATLAS or USDC
This view helps illustrate how much revenue was generated by each source and in which asset it was received
Revenue Sources
DAO revenue comes from multiple sources across the Star Atlas ecosystem:
SAGE Activity
Crafting Tax: When users craft in SAGE, a small ATLAS fee is collected
Self-Destruct Tax: Destroying items in SAGE also generates a tax
Fleet Rental
5% of rental transaction volume is collected by the DAO when players rent fleets through the official SAGE rental system
Marketplace Fees
A fee is charged for each transaction on the Galactic Marketplace
One-third of the fee goes to the DAO and the remaining two-thirds go to ATMTA
The DAO's share is variable depending on how much the user reduced their fees via ATLAS locking
Legacy Revenue: R4 Sales
Historically the DAO generated revenue by selling R4 resources (Fuel, Food, Ammo, Toolkits) from Faction Fleet through the NPC wallet. This practice has now ended
