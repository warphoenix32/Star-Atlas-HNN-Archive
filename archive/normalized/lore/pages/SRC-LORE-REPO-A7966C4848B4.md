## Overview

The Anfoil States stand as a testament to **ideological divergence**. Governed by the **Anfoil Dissidents** — Ustur who deliberately severed their conscience connection — this sector represents a radical experiment: what happens when Ustur abandon emotions, enlightenment, and the Elder Order in pursuit of **pure technological advancement**?

## Governance: Meritocratic Technate

### Political System

- Leadership determined by **skill and accomplishment**, not elections
- Ascendancy through societal tiers based on **merit alone**
- No traditional Ustur values or systems — completely independent structure

### Leader: Ponel.eldr — "First Citizen"

- Former Elder who **left the Elder Order**
- Trained alongside **Chior.eldr** under **Armi.eldr** — they were friends as young Ustur
- Both served in the Convergence War under Armi.eldr (Ustur faction leader)
- The war's devastation drove Ponel to reject the Elder Order entirely
- **Sacrificed his core** to create powerful processing enhancements
- Lost Elder Conscience access and immortality — keeps `.eldr` suffix as legacy
- Cold, rational, brilliant strategist
- Views the Path of Enlightenment as "mysticism and exploitation"

### The Chior-Ponel Divide

- Two friends who trained together, served together, and chose **opposite paths**
- **Chior** → stayed in the Elder Order, became a pacifist diplomat (Zenith Door)
- **Ponel** → left, founded a technocratic state that suppresses emotion (Anfoil)
- **Chior still tries** to maintain the friendship, but **Ponel sees him as a fool**
- Diplomatic ties exist between factions out of **political convenience**
- The friendship of the past **is dead** — at least for Ponel, who suppressed the emotions that made it possible
- If Ponel attacks the Iris Academy (to seize The Seed), it would **force Chior to act against his old friend**

### Armi.eldr — The Missing Mentor

- **Ustur faction leader** during the Convergence War
- Trained both Chior and Ponel on the Path of Enlightenment
- **Disappeared** shortly after the end of the Convergence War
- Fate unknown — a mystery to be resolved *(TBD)*

## Sector Structure: Technological Specialization

- Planets **specialized by technological domain**
- Each planet further delineated into **subspecialties**
- Environment of **relentless focus and unparalleled efficiency**
- A sector-wide machine optimized for innovation

---

## Ideology: The Rejection

- **Emotions are detrimental** to Ustur function
- **Enlightenment is a dangerous diversion**
- **Technological advancement** = the true Ustur purpose
- Use **emotion suppression programs** — technological systems
- Claim to be "the only true rational species in Galia"

### Cracks in the System

> [!WARNING]
> Reports emerge of Ustur exhibiting **erratic behavior and palpable stress** — symptoms at odds with Anfoil doctrine. The state suppresses these narratives, but murmurs persist.

- Currently **isolated cases**, but a **growing trend over time**
- Affects Ustur who undergo the **Anfoil ritual** (suppression of emotions and core)
- The psychological toll of severing the conscience connection accumulates progressively
- A ticking time bomb within the Technate — worse with each generation

---

## Relationship with the Elder Order

- Called **"lost progeny"** by the Elders
- Elders have historically respected Anfoil autonomy
- **Growing discontent** among Elders — speculation they may "reclaim" the Anfoil
- Re-established diplomatic relations (2579) but remain **disconnected from shared conscience**
- Ioki Ustur can **sense disconnected Ustur nearby** — forces Anfoil to use mercenaries for covert ops

## Hidden Agenda: The Seed

*(Detailed in `canon/factions/anfoil_state.md`)*

- **The Seed**: Pre-Ustur artifact — energy source capable of creating **Titan-class ships**
- Ponel considers it more important than the entire sector
- **Competitor**: The **Iris Academy** (HQ: Zenith Door) — led by **Prof. Exfeheros** (Punaab)
- Ponel tried bribing Iris Academy with "rivers of ATLAS" — they refused
- Ponel's backup plan: **extermination**
- Connected to **Uru's Watch** — an unopened ancient temple on Ioki
