Building With Star Atlas Creative IP ​
You’re invited to create using the Star Atlas universe art, lore, and branding are yours to build on, with no fees until you reach $1,000,000 in revenue!
Whether you're working on merch, games, media, or something entirely new, we want to support you.
There are just some simple rules to follow before publishing or commercializing your project, you will need to:
Submit the Creators Form to let us know what you're building
Follow the User Services Requirements — key rules and expectations
Use the “Powered by the People” logo instead of the official “Star Atlas” trademarks
Report royalties if your project earns more than $1M USD
Use assets from the Star Atlas Assets library for visuals, logos, and art
Need Help? ​
If you're unsure about a rule, need custom assets, or want feedback on your idea, join us in 💻┃community-developers on Discord, and we will help guide you
We're here to support you, not gatekeep. Let’s build something awesome together!
