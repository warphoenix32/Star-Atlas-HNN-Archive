## Classified Claims

- Starbases are critical hubs in Starbased, supporting crafting, resource management, and faction influence.
- To keep them operational and progressing, you need to upgrade and maintain them regularly.
- Upgrading Starbases Purpose: Unlock new crafting recipes and higher functionality.
- How: Deliver specific crafted materials required for the next tier.
- Assign crew members for a set time to complete upgrades.
- Tip: Higher tiers need more complex goods and more crew.
- Maintaining Starbases Purpose: Keep starbases active and effective for crafting and support.
- How: Regularly supply resources like Food, Toolkits, and other essentials.
- Without upkeep, starbase functions (like crafting) may pause or slow down.
- Investing in starbase upgrades and upkeep ensures your faction’s strength and unlocks more opportunities in Star Atlas
