## Overview

Deep within ONI space lies **Evernat**, a sector that whispers tales of ancient origins. Believed by many scholars to **predate even some regions within the High-Risk Zone**, Evernat is the homeworld of the [Punaab](../species/punaab.md) species and the economic engine of the [ONI Consortium](../factions/oni.md). Its crown jewel — the planet **Akenat** — is one of the oldest inhabited worlds in the galaxy, governed by a unique **dual monarchy** that balances flamboyant capitalism above the clouds with methodical craftsmanship below the earth.

Within ONI, the Punaab contribution is captured in one phrase from the Maharog: *"Gilded by Punaab gold."* They provide the wealth, the trade networks, and the galaxy-spanning merchant infrastructure that transforms ONI from a military alliance into a functioning civilization.

---

## Akenat — The Hourglass World

Akenat's geography defines its civilization. The planet is layered like an hourglass:

| Layer | Environment | Inhabitants |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Upper World** | Enormous bountiful forests on giant clamshell plateaus — the **Skygroves of Yointalie** | **High Punaabs** — Merchant Princes |
| **Sea of Mist** | Ever-present, ever-shifting layer separating upper and lower worlds | Neutral zone — City in the Middle |
| **Lower World** | Labyrinthine spore caves, deep mines, bioluminescent tunnels | **Profound Punaabs** — Noble Craftsmen |

### The Sea of Mist — Akenat's Lifeblood

The Sea of Mist is not weather — it is a **planetary circulatory system**:

1. Underground spores from mushroom beds mix with hot currents from the planet's core
2. Rise as geysers enriched with gases and minerals at the **Geyserfields of Belpraakatu**
3. Form the Sea of Mist
4. Absorbed by upper-world plants → exhaled at the Skygroves → cools → falls as rain → cycle repeats

### The Origin Secret

> *"~2100: An alien traveler transported TED — the creature that would become the God of Mist — to planet Akenat. TED formed the Sea of Mist."*

The Sea of Mist — and by extension, all life on Akenat — exists because an unknown alien deposited the creature that became the Punaab god onto the planet. The Punaab do not know this. Life in Evernat **began** after TED's arrival.

## The God of Mist — A Living Deity

> *"In the Punaabs' darkest hours, this immortal entity rises to guide and shield them."*

The **God of Mist** is the Punaab's supreme religious figure — and unlike most galactic deities, this one is **real, alive, and sleeping**.

| Detail | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Nature** | A colossal squid of immense proportions — immortal |
| **Activity** | 99.9% of the time sleeping inside the Sea of Mist |
| **Awakening** | Rises ONLY in the Punaabs' darkest hours to guide and shield them |
| **Summoning** | Called at the **Mistheart Sanctum** during existential crises |
| **Origin** | Actually a colossal HRZ creature (designated "TED") transported by an unknown alien (~2100) |

Since Evernat predates the HRZ, the God of Mist was already on Akenat **before zone boundaries were drawn**. It is a **living fossil** from an era when all of Galia was dangerous.

### The Puri — Offspring of the God

The **Puri** are physical offspring of the God of Mist — small aquatic creatures with enormous eyes and a dumpling/octopus-like appearance. Every Punaab ship carries Puri as living symbols of the God's blessing. Puri are also used in the **Puri Ritual** — the official method of settling inter-branch disputes, where a released Puri swims toward one of two alternatives to determine the winner.

**Secret:** Since Puri are carried on ships across the galaxy, the God of Mist maintains the **largest passive sensing network in Galia** through its residual connection to its offspring.

## The Dual Monarchy

Two kings rule simultaneously, mediated by a neutral figure:

| Role | Current Holder | Branch | Function |
|------|---------------|--------|----------|
| **King of the Merchant Princes** | **Rikkaatovaalu** | High Punaab | Trade policy, external commerce, galactic relations |
| **King of the Guilds** | **Guildmaster Duumakkerund** | Profound Punaab | Production, mining, craftsmanship, internal governance |
| **Governor of the Mists** | **Saaleesivaan** | Hybrid | Governs the City in the Middle. Mediates disputes |

---

## The High & Profound Divide

| Branch | Appearance | Culture | Habitat |
|--------|-----------|---------|---------|
| **High Punaabs** | Dog-like (~60cm), white/golden fur | Capitalism, flamboyant style, merchant journeys | Skygroves above the mist |
| **Profound Punaabs** | Rat/meerkat-like (~40cm), gray fur | Medieval guild system, stoic craftsmen | Silver Caverns below the mist |

The rift between the two branches remains palpable, but **The City in the Middle** — a unified metropolis at the Sea of Mist's thickest point — represents hope for the **Mist-Born** generation: young Punaab who reject rigid High/Profound labels while honoring both branches.
