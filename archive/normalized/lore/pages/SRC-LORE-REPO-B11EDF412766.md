## Overview

**Neuno** — "Living Home" in the Mierese tongue — is the sentient planet that serves as the [Mierese](../species/mierese.md) homeworld and the political capital of the [ONI Consortium](../factions/oni.md). A colossal space parasite older than three thousand years, Neuno communicates not through words but through **subtle sensations** — shifts in temperature, vibrations in the ground, changes in wind that the wisest [Lore Keepers](../factions/mierese_lore_keepers.md) attempt to interpret. The planet is so renowned that the entire sector bears its name.

Unlike [Iris](../geography/the_cataclysm.md) — a sentient entity with a fractured cosmic mind generating galactic currents — Neuno is more **primal and instinctual**, closer to an animal intelligence. It nurtures and protects the Mierese, but it also confines them: before an unknown extraterrestrial species arrived and guided the Mierese into the cosmic age, the planet functioned as both guardian and warden.

> *"The planet is alive. Not metaphorically alive — alive the way a predator is alive. It watches. It adjusts. It decides."*
> — Iris Academy research report, classified

## The Sentient Planet

### Communication

Neuno communicates like an animal — through **subtle sensations** rather than language. A warmth in the soil may signal approval; a sudden wind may warn of danger; a tremor may express distress. Only the most experienced Lore Keepers can distinguish between Neuno's moods and ordinary weather. The process is imprecise and deeply personal — two Keepers may interpret the same sensation differently, leading to theological disputes that have shaped Mierese history.

### The Living Terrain

Neuno's landscapes morph rapidly, reflecting the planet's fluctuating temperaments. Mountains may shift over decades. Rivers redirect themselves. Forests grow or retreat in response to unknown stimuli. The Mierese draw resilience from this ever-shifting landscape — a people shaped by a world that never holds still.

### Resources

Rich in **biomass and food** — Neuno's sentient ecosystem produces abundant organic resources, though the planet's moods can suddenly alter growing conditions. Mierese agriculture is adaptive by necessity, practiced through oral tradition rather than written manuals.

The Mierese homeworld is divided into six ancestral regions, each lending its name to the Mierese who originate from it. A Mierese's third name (surname) identifies their regional ancestry:

|--------|----------------|-----------------|

| **Morvalel** | Cultural heartland — home of the Resonance Halls and the [Lore Keepers](../factions/mierese_lore_keepers.md)' sacred archives | [Ondara Valel](../factions/mierese_of_neuno.md) (Chief Lore Keeper) |

## The Grand Tale

The **Grand Tale** is the Mierese religion — an oral epic without beginning or end, carried in the memories of the [Lore Keepers](../factions/mierese_lore_keepers.md). It tells of Neuno's birth, the Mierese's emergence, and the unfolding of their cosmic purpose. The Mierese have **no written language** — the Grand Tale exists only in memory and voice, making the Lore Keepers the most politically powerful cultural institution in Mierese society.

Three **Sealed Chapters** of the Grand Tale are known to exist — passages so dangerous that only the Chief Lore Keeper knows their contents. Ondara Valel guards all three.

## The Liberation

An **unknown extraterrestrial species** arrived at Neuno and guided the Mierese into the cosmic age, breaking their planetary seclusion. Their identity remains one of the galaxy's great mysteries — a future lore hook that the Mierese themselves discuss with reverence and unease.

## Political Influence

### ONI Consortium Capital

Neuno is the seat of ONI governance. The ONI **Central Space Station** (CSS) orbits or is stationed near the planet, serving as the consortium's administrative hub — a massive rotating station with architecture designed to accommodate all four ONI species (Mierese, [Sogmian](../species/sogmian.md), [Punaab](../species/punaab.md), [Photoli](../species/photoli.md)). The current ONI leader, **Volmik Vaor Scarka**, governs from this station — a Hikibashi-trained Mierese assassin who became the galaxy's most powerful diplomat.

### The Mierese Leadership Rotation

ONI's executive leadership rotates among its four species. The current Mierese rotation gave rise to Vaor Scarka's presidency — a figure who embodies the paradox of Mierese culture: a species that prizes stories above wealth, yet whose intelligence operatives (the Hikibashi) are among the deadliest in the galaxy.

### The Lore Keepers' Hall

The **Resonance Halls** on Neuno house the Lore Keepers — the custodians of the Grand Tale and the Mierese species' collective memory. Their political influence is immense: no major Mierese decision is taken without consulting the Keepers, and the Sealed Chapters they guard represent knowledge considered too dangerous for public consumption.
