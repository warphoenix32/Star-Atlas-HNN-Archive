## Preserved Official Text

Locking POLIS earns you daily rewards based on your POLIS Voting Power (PVP) and the global emissions schedule. Here's how it works:
Emission Schedule
A total of 127,800,000 POLIS has been allocated for rewards. These are distributed daily over 8 years.
Start: August 1, 2022
End: August 1, 2030
First Daily Emission: 51,121 POLIS
Final Daily Emission: 31,006 POLIS
The amount of POLIS distributed each day decreases gradually over time.
How Your Rewards Are Calculated
Your daily reward depends on how much PVP you have compared to everyone else.
Formula:
User Rewards = (Your PVP / Total PVP) × Daily Emission
Example:
Total PVP in the DAO = 1,000,000
Daily emission = 50,000 POLIS
You have 2,000 PVP
You earn:
(2,000 / 1,000,000) × 50,000 = 100 POLIS
Your PVP is based on how much POLIS you lock and for how long:
The longer you lock, the higher your PVP multiplier.
Where to See Your Estimated Rewards
In the POLIS Locker dashboard, you’ll see an "Estimated Next Reward" on the Rewards Over Time chart.
⚠️ Note: This is a real-time estimate and may change if:
Other users lock more POLIS
You or others refresh their locks before the next reward drop
How It’s Tracked On-Chain
All PVP values and rewards are stored and calculated on-chain through two snapshot accounts:
Escrow History
Tracks your wallet’s individual locking data and PVP
Locker History
Tracks the global PVP across all users
When you lock or refresh your POLIS, the system updates your snapshots—these only affect future reward periods.
🕛 Each reward period starts at midnight GMT
