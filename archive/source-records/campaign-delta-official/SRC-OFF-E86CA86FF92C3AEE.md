Developer RPC Initiative ​
Star Atlas is committed to empowering community developers. Through our Developer RPC Initiative we cover the infrastructure costs of Solana RPC calls so you can focus on building great tools, analytics, and gameplay extensions for the ecosystem.
What the program provides ​
Free, production-grade RPC endpoints for Star Atlas projects
Best-practice guides and tutorials on data access and RPC usage, published on build.staratlas.com
Example code in the open-source cookbook (github.com/staratlasmeta/star-atlas-cookbook). Community pull requests are welcome.
Eligibility: your project must be open-source or offer open public access to qualify for a free RPC key.
How to apply ​
Make sure your repo or hosted app is public.
Fill out this short form: https://forms.gle/oRfGsuc1ArPSj4NBA
Our infra team will review and email you the credentials.
By lowering operating costs and sharing examples, we aim to foster a strong, collaborative developer community around Star Atlas. If you have questions or ideas, reach out in the #community-developers Discord channel.
Let’s build together!
