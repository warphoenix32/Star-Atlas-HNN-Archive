## Preserved Official Text

Explore the unique digital assets that power gameplay in Star Atlas
In Star Atlas, NFTs (non-fungible tokens) represent ownership of in-game items stored on the blockchain. These digital assets can be used within gameplay or traded with other players on the Star Atlas Marketplace. When you purchase an NFT in Star Atlas, it becomes part of your connected wallet and your personal Inventory.
Each NFT is unique and serves a specific purpose in the Star Atlas metaverse whether it's piloting a ship, mining planetary resources, outfitting your crew or customizing your experience
Types of NFTs in Star Atlas
Ships
Ships are core to gameplay. Each comes with its own stats like cargo capacity, speed, and firepower, and serves roles such as combat, transport, or exploration.
Buyable on the Marketplace using ATLAS or USDC
Playable in Faction Fleet and SAGE Labs
Flyable in the UE5 3D Game (available on the Epic Games Store)
Can be upgraded, customized, and staffed with Crew
Can be rented in Faction Fleet
Claim Stakes
Claim Stakes are essential for planetary operations. They allow players to claim plots of land and produce R4 resources (food, fuel, toolkits, ammo) when enlisted in Faction Claims
Currently usable in Faction Claims to passively earn R4 resources (Fuel, Food, Ammo, Toolkits)
Foundational for future gameplay in SAGE and UE5, where they will support mining, building, and deeper planetary mechanics
Buyable on the Star Atlas Marketplace using ATLAS or USDC
Crew
Crew members are essential to operating ships and maximizing fleet performance in Star Atlas.
Required for full ship operations in SAGE Labs
Each Crew has unique stats, aptitudes, and rarities
Playable as avatars in the UE5 3D game (on Epic Games)
Buyable in Crew Packs on the Star Atlas Marketplace and tradable on Tensor
Resources
Resources in Star Atlas include a wide array of materials used across the economy and gameplay systems.
R4 resources – Food, Fuel, Ammo, and Toolkits, are vital for maintaining fleets and starbases
Other resources include raw and refined materials used for crafting and upgrading assets in SAGE Labs and (eventually) UE5
Collectibles
Collectibles are aesthetic or commemorative NFTs with future utility in UE5 or broader Star Atlas systems.
Categories include:
Crew Gear – Decorative items like patches and skins
Charms – Rewards for specific achievements
Emotes – Will be used in the UE5 game
Memories – Lore and worldbuilding artifacts
Posters – Art collectibles and displayables
Pets – Decorative companions, with future perks
Skins – Ship cosmetics
Trophies – Earned through community or in-game events
Access
NFTs that serve as licenses, permits, or event badges. These may offer perks or be commemorative.
Where Can I Use These NFTs?
Faction Fleet: Stake ships and earn passive ATLAS
SAGE Labs: Use ships, crew, and resources to explore, craft, and battle
UE5 Game: Fly your ship and use your crew as your avatar, with more asset integrations coming in the future
Fleet Rental: Create a fleet in SAGE and rent it to other players for passive income
Marketplace: Trade NFTs with others for ATLAS or USDC
