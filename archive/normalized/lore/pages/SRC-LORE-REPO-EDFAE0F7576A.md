## Overview

Once a beacon of prosperity and scientific research within MUD territory, **Mycenas-V** was **deliberately abandoned** by MUD during the Convergence War to preserve their own resources. Eight decades of chaos followed before order returned.

## Key Lore

### The Fall

- **Pre-War**: Thriving community of scientists and research institutions, funded by central MUD authority.
- **The Abandonment**: MUD **deliberately cut off** Mycenas to preserve resources for the core empire during the Convergence War.
- **The Horror**: ~80 years of pirate onslaughts, famines, alien slaver raids, and countless calamities.
- **~2542**: Mycenas-V is **raided by alien pirates** — a devastating attack that plunges the sector into renewed chaos
- **~2542**: In the wake of the raids, **Virel the Serene** starts a **civil movement** to fight against external threats and raids in Mycenas-V
- **~2544**: Virel manages to **unify the academics** of Mycenas-V and creates a **meritocratic government** focused on self-sustenance. Using the HRZ artifact, Virel constructs an **energy shield** that isolates the Mycenas-V homeworld — a **turning point** in the conflict against the pirates
- **~2546**: With the energy shield securing the homeworld, Virel's forces systematically **destroy or expel all alien invaders** from the sector
- **~2553**: Virel upgrades the energy shield into an imposing system-wide **defensive barrier** that **isolates Mycenas from the greater galaxy** — a fortress of self-sufficiency

### Virel the Serene

- **Origin**: A **MUD researcher** during the Convergence War, developing technology for the faction.
- During his research, he **discovered an HRZ artifact** with immense defensive potential.
- When MUD deliberately abandoned Mycenas, Virel **stole the artifact** and stayed behind.
- United the sector's survivors and used his academic expertise + the artifact to build defenses
- Only exception to isolation: a strategically positioned **starbase** for select external interactions
- **Still alive and ruling** as the current governor.

### Anti-MUD Sentiment

- The people remember MUD's **deliberate abandonment** — this is deeply personal.
- **MUD fleets** that venture through the system are met with **outright hostility**.
- Threatened to intervene if COP continued assault on Pergamos.
