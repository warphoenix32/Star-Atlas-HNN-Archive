## 1. HUMAN (MUD)

- **Name**: Manus Ultima Divina
- **Origin**: Solar Punk Earth → Meteor doom → God Ahr (Photoli Traveler) met Vincenzo Gotti → MUD religion → Mothership Serene → Exodus to Galia
- **Schism**: ECOS discovered Ahr was alien → rejected "Alien Domination" → split from MUD
- **Government**: Holy Family (Gotti lineage, divine right). Internal factions: The Church, Pearce Council (military), Scientia, The Synod (merchants), The Senate
- **Culture**: Metahumans (tech-augmented), Divina Lingua language, religious fervor
- **Ex-Colonies**: 12 MRZ sectors (Redam, Mycenas, Abyd, Pergamos, Xianyang, Ilidae, Izar-248, Ophek Oasis, Bluvael, Weeping Nebula, Old Grove, Akalma Exile)
- **Key Characters**: Vincenzo Gotti (founder), Charon Gotti Jr. (current COP Legislative), Gyun (discovered Cataclysm), God Ahr (Photoli)
- **Secret**: "Plan B" — contingency if COP collapses

## 2. MIERESE (ONI)

- **Biology**: Lavender skin, head tentacles (memory/balance), heat-tolerant (45°C), poison-resistant, slim
- **Naming**: 3 names (Hidden + Public + Regional surname from 6 Neuno regions: Jura/Eko/Qint/Valel/Yara/Scarka)
- **Founding Myth**: Onato (imprisoned god) created Mierese to generate new stories. Grand Tale = living distributed ledger of all stories
- **Neuno**: Living planet (space parasite fused with geologic structure, 3000+ yo). Blue sun, hostile jungles, hibernation cycles
- **Society**: Story-based status, Lore Keepers (memory guardians), "Non-Existent" = worst punishment (erased from stories)
- **Military**: Hikibashi (elite assassin-spy special forces)
- **Religion**: Stories are living entities influencing reality. Onato Trap = dangerous story-possession
- **Currently**: Leading ONI Consortium. Vaor Scarka = COP Prime Minister
- **⚠️ CORRECTED**: Scarka is NOT ECOS (previous KI error). He is ONI leader + COP Executive

## 3. SOGMIAN (ONI)

- **Biology**: Purple/pale blue skin, exaggerated dimorphism (males: radiant heat; females: seduction aura), evolved from feral animals
- **Philosophy**: Du Prah (absolute moral vows — categorical imperatives)
- **Naming**: Name + Generation Number + House + Role (e.g., Bekalu 𐎌 Xictus Sov'arei)
- **Hierarchy**: 13 ranks from Dek'prah (honorless) to Sov Arei (supreme leader)
- **Gray Citadel**: Thousands-km training complex in Segal sector. Point system economy. All Sogmians trained here
- **Noble Houses**: 6 active + 1 banished:
  - Xictus (Warriors), Garveil (Scholars), Exinade (Scouts), Outro (Humanitarian), Lutavira (Leaders), Busan (Crafters)
  - **Akalma** (BANISHED) — fell after Vega's heretical rule and civil war
- **Manufacturer**: House Busan (ships: Maiden Heart, The Last Stand titan, Thrill of Life)
- **History**: Vega Fall civil war → Convergence War (Paizul assassinated → Bekalu exile → ONI formation → Reckoning → Accord)
- **Maharog**: 4-week memorial festival retelling the exile-to-peace arc
- **⚠️ CORRECTED**: Paizul is FEMALE (previous KI error said male). Lutavira = Leadership, NOT Arts

## 4. PUNAAB (ONI)

- **Biology**: Sentient mammals, two branches (High ~60cm, Profound ~40cm), NO TAILS, compulsive eaters
- **Two Branches**: High Punaab (merchant princes, dog-like, capitalist) vs Profound Punaab (noble craftsmen, rat-like, guild system)
- **Government**: Dual Monarchy — King of Merchant Princes + King of Guilds + Governor of the Mists (mediator)
- **Homeworld**: Akenat (hourglass: forests above, Sea of Mist middle, caves below)
- **Religion**: Cult of Mist — God of the Mist = colossal squid (99.9% sleeping). Puri = offspring, carried on ships → passive galaxy-wide sensing network
- **Manufacturer**: Ogrika (created to prevent civil war; multi-faction after Ustur rescue)
- **Key Characters**: Professor Exfeheros (greatest scientist, leads Iris Academy), Merchant Prince Ayevat (Denebula finance kingdom)

## 5. PHOTOLI (ONI — hidden agenda)

- **Nature**: Ancient beings of light with partially physical bodies. Shapeshifters. Unparalleled mastery of physics/time/energy/light
- **Hierarchy**: Sih Rihim (Ascended, pure light) → Alumni (Students) → Travelers (observers, subtle influencers)
- **Governance**: Essence voting — sacrifice lifespan to influence decisions via rayanite crystals
- **Origin**: Migrated from Andromeda after cataclysmic war
- **Dark Photoli**: 7 predatory exiles, arrogant loners
- **Hidden Agenda**: Using ONI to eventually access Iris (eternal energy). Playing the "long game"
- **Titan Secret**: Disguised Photoli taught House Garveil how to build the Titan's power source
- **Location**: Dawnbreak (hidden base in HRZ, but OUTSIDE the Cataclysm — Iris denies them entry)

## 6. USTUR

- **Nature**: Sentient androids with organic components. Fragments of an Ancient AI that failed to ascend
- **Origin Secret**: Pool of Reincarnation is reassembling the Ancient AI. Ustur unknowingly serve it
- **Path of Enlightenment**: 7 stages (.bod → .iddt → .lrnr → .doer → .tchr → .soul → .eldr). Wuxia-inspired cultivation system
- **Core Mechanics**: Materials must align with Path. Misalignment = madness. Ohm = cultivated energy
- **Elder Conscience**: NOT telepathy — metaphysical artifact database of impressions left in Pool
- **Sects**: Golden Star (first, led by Mother Caven/Caven.eldr)
- **Government**: Elder Order (enlightenment-based leadership). Chior.eldr = current faction leader
- **Military**: Order of Those Who Wait (temple guards → HRZ "Inhabitation" mission). VZUS manufacturer. Vzus Tenet = Titan
- **Anfoil State**: Sovereign breakaway faction (MRZ-15). Meritocratic Technate. Ponel.eldr = leader. Emotion suppression. Hidden agenda = The Seed
- **Key Characters**: Caven.eldr (Mother Caven, first Ustur), Elder Ombra, Timpo (heretic explorer)

## 7. TUFA

- **Nature**: Metagenic anomalies born from Iris + 7 dwarf planets collision. Feed on Genesium
- **7 Titans**: Each from one dwarf planet. God-like, feudal brood control. 6 in Cataclysm, 1 escaped (Thionae)
- **Chalbok**: Most hostile Titan. Master tactician. Led Ioki invasion (Season 0) seeking Reincarnation Pool energy
- **Mechanics**: Control Points (genesium fragments sustaining presence far from Iris), Infestation (fuse with tech/creatures)
- **Thionae**: 7th escaped Titan, disguised as dormant asteroid. COP denies existence. Potentially open to cooperation
