## Preserved Official Text

POLIS is the governance token of Star Atlas, giving players voting power and influence over the future of the ecosystem.
You can earn POLIS passively by locking either POLIS or ATLAS tokens:
Lock POLIS in the POLIS Locker
This is the most efficient way to earn more POLIS over time.
Earn daily POLIS rewards
Voting power (PVP) increases with the amount and duration of your lock
Rewards are distributed proportionally to your share of total PVP
Emissions are fixed and scheduled until 2030
🔗 Learn how POLIS locking works
Lock ATLAS in the ATLAS Locker
While primarily used for fee discounts on the Star Atlas Marketplace and SAGE’s Local Marketplace, locking ATLAS also grants bonus POLIS rewards.
Earn daily POLIS rewards (lower than with POLIS locking)
Unlock up to 85% fee reduction based on your lock tier
Combine savings with passive earnings
🔗 Learn how the ATLAS locker works
Why Earn POLIS?
Unlock governance participation in the Star Atlas DAO
Vote on proposals that shape the future of Star Atlas
Accumulate more POLIS through passive rewards
