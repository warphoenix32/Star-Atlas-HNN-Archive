## Classified Claims

- Once you’ve formed a fleet, you’ll need to manage it to keep it active and efficient.
- Fleet management lets you adjust settings, assign tasks, and keep track of your ships.
- Ways to manage your fleets: Open the “My Fleets” menu: this tab shows all fleets you have created.
- Check fleet details: click on a fleet to see its ships, crew, cargo, and status.
- Access fleet settings: open the Hangar menu, select a fleet, and click the small cog icon.
- This is where you can adjust options like auto-unload or automation preferences.
- Docking and undocking: fleets can be docked at bases to rest, refuel, or load/unload resources.
- Give orders: fleets can be assigned tasks like mining, transporting, scanning, or combat depending on their ships.
- Managing fleets effectively ensures you always have the right ships and crew working toward your goals, whether that’s gathering resources or defending your faction.

## Preserved Official Text

Once you’ve formed a fleet, you’ll need to manage it to keep it active and efficient. Fleet management lets you adjust settings, assign tasks, and keep track of your ships.
Ways to manage your fleets:
Open the “My Fleets” menu: this tab shows all fleets you have created.
Check fleet details: click on a fleet to see its ships, crew, cargo, and status.
Access fleet settings: open the Hangar menu, select a fleet, and click the small cog icon. This is where you can adjust options like auto-unload or automation preferences.
Docking and undocking: fleets can be docked at bases to rest, refuel, or load/unload resources.
Give orders: fleets can be assigned tasks like mining, transporting, scanning, or combat depending on their ships.
Managing fleets effectively ensures you always have the right ships and crew working toward your goals, whether that’s gathering resources or defending your faction.
