Narrentos, celebrated journalist and the pawn behind the widely read Punaab Whisper, heard the announcement by the Secretary and then raced back to the meeting hall.
He looked around at the crowd of friends and relatives, expecting everyone to be as excited as he was.
“Does anyone want to hear what the Secretary just announced?”
They looked glum, almost hopeless. He asked his assistant, libra.iddt, what was wrong. She said she wasn’t sure why they were all feeling a little down. “It’s been a hard time. Maybe it’s just the natural cycle of life. And it seems like more dark times are ahead. Perhaps even war.”
The Punaab started to interject, but then he stopped. Instead, he turned and raced out of the room, across two levels of the space station, back to his office and began to write.
Minutes later, a document appeared on the screens of the people in the meeting hall, and across that corner of the galaxy.
It read:
A MANIFESTO FOR THE GOLDEN ERA
Beings of the Galaxy,
I’m here to share with you an intergalactic truth that might not always be easy to see, but which is indisputable even by the forces governing the universe. It goes like this.
Here and now, we stand on the doorstep of a Golden Era. Our bonds have already managed to create a civilization that transcends even our ancestors’ wildest dreams, who hoped to live among the stars they could only imagine visiting.
We believe in the power of the imagination.
Power comes from the people, and is exercised directly by the people. We have the most talented workers, devoting themselves to advancing this ethos every day. We all yearn to see each other enjoy our lives in the way we see fit, among an infinite number of fulfilling paths.
We live in a complex system, growing day by day, economically sophisticated, enabling us to dream of a galaxy where the power comes not from the top or the bottom but from the decentralized voices at the very heart of our society.
We embrace difference, and ensure that everyone is able to enjoy our galaxy in whatever ways they find fulfilling.
We are builders. We are not here to create small or easy things. We are here to push forward, stretch farther, and reach higher than many think possible.
We see beauty in art, technology, and simplicity. We use technology to create elegant, groundbreaking art.
We are adventurers and explorers. We believe in the brightest of futures. We do not shy away from the unknown. We go there to know there.
We are constantly improving. All these marvels were built by us and those before our own time. Let us not forget how far we have come so rapidly and how much still lies ahead.
The COP just announced that three titan ships, the ultimate expression of our craftsmanship and cooperation, will take to the skies again after a hundred years. Although they are best known as the deadliest weapons in the galaxy, they are also a wandering home for thousands of people. They prove that we can accomplish anything when we work together, even conquering hostile environments, like the cold void of space.
Take advantage of opportunities as they arise.
Do not lose faith. Do not abandon hope. The noise, fear, and doubt will soon dissipate, washed away in the cosmic currents.
Time passes, and Iris never stops moving. The sunny skies of the Golden Era await us tomorrow.
Narrentos

ABOUT STAR ATLAS
Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state-of-the-art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video games, and decentralized financial technologies.
Using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite, real-time graphics technology allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. In addition, blockchain technology using the Solana protocol establishes a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas create an economy that replicates tangible world assets and ownership.
