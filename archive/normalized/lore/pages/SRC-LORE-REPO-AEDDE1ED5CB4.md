## Institutions

### Council of Peace (COP)

- **Structure**: 3 branches (Executive/Legislative/Legal), 5-year rotation
- **Current Leaders**: Scarka (ONI/Executive), Charon Gotti Jr. (MUD/Legislative), Opos.eldr (Ustur/Legal)
- **Military**: Tier 1. Millions of Pearce-class vessels. **Cradle** = most powerful titan (> all 3 faction titans combined)
- **Tension**: Scarka's militarism vs Opos's pacifism. MUD is next in Executive rotation

### Star Atlas (Sentient Blockchain)

- **Nature**: Sentient entity. Originally blockchain → infused with HRZ materials by Vignus oracles → awakened
- **Children**: SAGE (AGI daughter, neutral guide for captains) + Holosim (simulation training)
- **Function**: Governs economy/commerce in MRZ and Safe Zone. Controls Starpath Warp Gates independently
- **Status**: Worshipped as benevolent god-figure by some. Works in harmony with COP but is independent

## History: The Convergence War

1. **Cause**: Discovery of Iris (sentient planet) → factions harvest Genesium by killing Tufa
2. **Three Paths**: MUD (divine land), Sogmian (sovereign vow of verification), Ustur (inhabitation for evolution)
3. **Sogmian Defense**: Recognized Tufa sentience, shielded them (Du Prah demands it)
4. **Paizul Assassination**: Sov Arei Paizul killed → Bekalu leads exile to HRZ
5. **ONI Formation**: Sogmian + Punaab + Mierese + Photoli alliance forged in exile
6. **The Last Stand**: First Titan, powered by Photoli-provided technology (secret)
7. **The Reckoning**: ONI armada returns, campaigns across galaxy
8. **The Accord**: Treaty of Peace → COP + Star Atlas + Starpath Gates → Golden Era
