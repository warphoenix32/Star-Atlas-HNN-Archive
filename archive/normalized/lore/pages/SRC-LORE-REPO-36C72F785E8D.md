## Overview

- **~2489**: The **Ustur** discover the twin-star system of the Verbis Cluster

In the Verbis Cluster, a cosmic ballet unfolds as a pair of stars — one **crimson**, the other **azure** — dance in a perpetual orbit. Their radiant embrace eliminates darkness, gifting the sector's planets with **unending daylight**.

While these stars are positioned at a safe distance (ensuring the planets aren't scalded), their presence has nurtured an environment rich in extraordinary life.

---

## Environment

### Binary Star System

- **Red + blue binary stars** in perpetual mutual orbit
- The combined radiance eliminates darkness entirely
- Planets experience **eternal daylight** — no night cycle exists
- Stars are distant enough to prevent scorching, but close enough to bathe everything in constant light

### Unique Biosphere

- Ceaseless illumination has birthed species adapted to thrive in **eternal luminescence**
- Many species **harness the relentless light**, converting it into sustenance (photosynthetic life at scale)
- Life here is marked by **truncated lifespans** and a **complete absence of sleep**
- This unique biology captivates biologists across Galia
- **Visiting species also suffer** — constant light exposure naturally affects all biological organisms (sleep disruption, accelerated fatigue, skin damage)

## The Janar (Near-Sentient Species)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Janar are **teetering on the brink of sentience** — remarkable beings with natural biological manufacturing capabilities.

### Biology

- Possess a natural aptitude for **photopolymerization** and **photochemical reactions**
- Their bodies become **biological factories**, producing compounds they later expel
- These expelled compounds serve as the foundation for **structures and tools** they craft
- A testament to their burgeoning intelligence — they are builders without technology

### Discovery

- **~2512**: The **Ustur** formally discover the existence of the Janar
- The Ustur build a **scientific city called Pertuam** to study the Janar and the Verbis Cluster

### Poacher Siege (~2533–2535)

- **~2533**: Due to the **Ustur weakening during the Convergence War**, poachers begin raiding the Verbis Cluster to capture or exploit the Janar
- **Pertuam forces fight back** against the invaders to protect the near-sentient species
- **~2535**: Although Pertuam forces ultimately **win against the poacher raids**, the planet suffers **critical infrastructure attacks**
- The damage forces the **Ustur to withdraw from the sector**
- Before leaving, the Ustur deploy an **automated defense system** to protect the Janar against foreign attackers

### Current Status

- **Protected by automated Ustur defense systems** — but the Ustur themselves have withdrawn
- Other organizations still attempt to hunt and capture them
- Maintain a **cautious distance** from other settlements and species
- Their photopolymerization compounds have **known industrial applications** and would be extraordinarily valuable if harvested
- The key deterrent to exploitation: **Janar do not develop well in captivity** — they cannot be farmed or factory-bred
- This biological limitation, combined with Ustur/COP protection, is the only thing keeping them safe
