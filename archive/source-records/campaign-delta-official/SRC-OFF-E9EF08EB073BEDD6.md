## Preserved Official Text

The Star Atlas Galactic Marketplace is a player-driven platform where you can list ships, crew, collectibles, and other assets for sale to other players. It uses an order-book system, allowing you to match open buy orders and complete secure, on-chain trades.
Step-by-Step: Selling an Item
1. Browse the Marketplace
Go to play.staratlas.com and open the Marketplace tabUse the Search bar to find the item you want to sellOr browse categories using the drop-down menus
2. Select the Item
Click on the item you want to sell to view current buyer listings.Some assets may be listed in both ATLAS and USDC, while others are available in ATLAS only. Choose a buyer listing with your preferred currency.
3. Choose a Buyer
Pick a listing from the available buy orders that matches your desired price and quantity.
4. Review Terms & Policies
Check the boxes to agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
5. Connect Your Wallet
If you haven’t already, click Connect Wallet and approve the connection using your Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom, Backpack, etc.).
6. Set the Quantity
Adjust the Quantity to Sell based on how many units you want to list.
7. Confirm Price & Currency
Double-check the total amount you’ll receive and make sure it’s in the correct token (ATLAS or USDC).
8. Click “Sell Item”
Once everything looks good, hit the Sell Item button.
9. Approve the Transaction
Your wallet will prompt you to approve the transaction. Confirm to proceed.
10. Order Complete
Once processed, you’ll see a confirmation notification.You can view the transaction under “My Orders” in the Marketplace menu.
