# Governance Timeline

This page distinguishes governance design, platform release, proposals, deliberation, votes, approvals, and execution. A passed proposal is not treated as executed without separate evidence.

## 2021

### 2021-08-18 — Economic and governance blueprint published

**Description:** ATMTA published a blueprint for its proposed economic and governance model before the web governance platform existed. **Significance:** This is an early official statement of governance intent. **Affected entities:** POLIS, ATLAS, Star Atlas DAO. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-44DE029022F88A60](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-44DE029022F88A60.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Design publication, not implementation. **Superseded:** Partly; later framework and PIP documents formalized the process.

## 2022

### 2022-07-21 — Initial DAO governance and POLIS locking launched

**Description:** ATMTA stated that the initial DAO governance and locking module had launched, enabling POLIS locking, rewards, and voting power. Aephia and HNN published contemporaneous operational coverage. **Significance:** This is the principal release anchor for web-accessible Star Atlas governance. **Affected entities:** Star Atlas DAO, POLIS Locker, POLIS, ATLAS. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-E5AEFD6B36E3CE06](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-E5AEFD6B36E3CE06.md), [SRC-AEPHIA-9625CEC37184A8ED](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-9625CEC37184A8ED.md), [SRC-HNN-9912D3E691A37CE7](../../archive/source-records/campaign-charlie-hnn/SRC-HNN-9912D3E691A37CE7.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Platform and locking release; no proposal approval is implied. **Superseded:** No.

### 2022-09-29 — Sustainable governance framework released

**Description:** ATMTA announced a sustainable governance framework intended to guide gradual community control. **Significance:** The framework separated governance rules from the earlier locking-module release. **Affected entities:** Star Atlas DAO, POLIS, governance framework. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-68D126528852339E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-68D126528852339E.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Framework publication; later PIPs define specific rules. **Superseded:** Partly.

### 2022-09-29 — ATLAS Locker released

**Description:** ATMTA separately announced the ATLAS Locker. **Significance:** This added an economic-locking mechanism distinct from POLIS governance locking. **Affected entities:** ATLAS Locker, ATLAS, Galactic Marketplace. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-D07F401D77E9CA13](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-D07F401D77E9CA13.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Economic utility should not be conflated with POLIS voting power. **Superseded:** No.

## 2023

### 2023-06-11 — Community proposal for DAO-owned Claim Stakes published

**Description:** HNN published a feature describing a community-originated proposal for DAO-owned Claim Stakes. **Significance:** The record shows bottom-up governance experimentation before the formal PIP-1 announcement. **Affected entities:** Star Atlas DAO, Claim Stakes, community proposers. **Sources:** [SRC-HNN-C568C274EEB5471E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-charlie-hnn/SRC-HNN-C568C274EEB5471E.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Community proposal; not an official PIP approval or execution record. **Superseded:** Unknown.

### 2023-06-14 — Alternative to PIP-X proposed

**Description:** HNN reported that Polaris Fuel proposed an alternative to PIP-X. **Significance:** This preserves a contested proposal lineage rather than treating a single draft as consensus. **Affected entities:** Polaris Fuel, PIP-X, Star Atlas DAO. **Sources:** [SRC-HNN-9C5AFDED678A96D8](../../archive/source-records/campaign-charlie-hnn/SRC-HNN-9C5AFDED678A96D8.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Proposal stage only. **Superseded:** Unknown.

### 2023-07-07 — PIP-1 and Foundation framework announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced PIP-1 as the inaugural on-chain POLIS Improvement Proposal, introducing governance mechanisms, a constitution, deliberation process, and the real-world Star Atlas Foundation. **Significance:** PIP-1 formalized the proposal process after the 2022 platform launch. **Affected entities:** PIP-1, Star Atlas DAO, Star Atlas Constitution, Star Atlas Foundation, POLIS. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-50186C400EF7CC79](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-50186C400EF7CC79.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Proposal announcement; this entry does not assert approval or execution. **Superseded:** No.
