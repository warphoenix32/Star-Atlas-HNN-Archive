## Overview

The Oxir are a **native species of the High Risk Zone (HRZ)**, one of the few known sentient peoples to originate within the dangerous territory surrounding the Cataclysm.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Oxir are the **first known species to discover the nature and aspects of cosmic currents** — the fundamental energy flows that permeate the galaxy. This discovery predates all known factions' understanding of cosmic mechanics.

---

## Religion & Belief

### Worship of Iris

- The Oxir **worship Iris** — the sentient planet at the heart of the Cataclysm
- **~2460**: A conscious **Tufa** discovers the tribes of Oxir; the **Cult of Iris** begins among the Oxir people
- The Tufa's arrival deepened and formalized existing reverence for Iris into an organized cult

### Cosmic Currents

- The Oxir were **first to discover the nature and aspects of cosmic currents**
- This knowledge gives them unique insight into the energy mechanics of the HRZ and Galia at large

### First Contact

- **~2503**: Oxir tribes meet the galactic factions for the first time — they **steer away from them**, choosing isolation over engagement
