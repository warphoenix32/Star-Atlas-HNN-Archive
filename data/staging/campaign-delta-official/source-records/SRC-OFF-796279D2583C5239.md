## Preserved Official Text

Short Answer: Not Yet
Currently, there is no functionality in SAGE that allows crew members to gain experience points (XP). This feature is planned for future updates, where crew leveling will likely become an integral part of gameplay, allowing for more personalized and strategic use of your crew members.
Looking Forward to Future Updates
While XP is not available yet, the development team is working towards incorporating this feature in future game releases. Stay tuned for announcements and updates on when crew XP and leveling will become part of the SAGE experience!
