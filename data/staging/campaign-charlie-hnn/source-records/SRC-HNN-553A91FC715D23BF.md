Super Phoenix DAO Announces Mid-June CORE Data Cube NFT Mint
By KrigsPublished On: 3 June 2024Tags: superphoenix
ECOS SP Limited – The Super Phoenix DAO is gaining momentum as it heads towards its first public mint of the CORE Data Cube NFT. Owning a CORE Data Cube NFT will provide holders with a path to shared ownership of the largest ship in Galia, the Superphoenix Titan ship. Additionally, holders will receive a host of other airdrops, rewards, benefits, and exclusive access to the Superphoenix Titan ship itself. The CORE data Cube NFTs allow holders to participate in the creation of a powerful community within the Star Atlas ecosystem centered around the largest NFT asset sold in Web3.
There are six levels of Core Data Cubes, with the level of the NFT correlating to the rewards and airdrops that the holder will receive. These rewards can include Star Atlas ships, posters, and DAO tokens. The color of the NFT indicates the level of data Cube energy it contains. White and green colored NFTs are lower levels, while blue and red colored NFTs are higher levels. The highest level of Data Cube energy is contained within NFTs that are gold and and anomaly level.
