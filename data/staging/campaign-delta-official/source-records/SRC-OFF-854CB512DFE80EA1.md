## Preserved Official Text

Automation in Holosim allows your fleets to run routes automatically, repeating tasks like mining, transporting, or patrolling without needing constant manual input.
How to enable automation:
Open “My Fleets”: click the My Fleets tab to see your fleets.
Select a fleet: choose the fleet you want to automate.
Click the automation icon: look for the symbol with a curved arrow and gear
Enable route: click “Enable Route.”
Choose an action: select one of the available automation tasks:
Mining: automatically mine resources.
Transport: move goods between bases.
Patrol: keep your fleet active in specific areas.
Set the details: depending on the action, you may need to choose:
Which base or resource to mine.
The origin and destination for transport.
The patrol sector.
Assign the automation: once configured, confirm to activate.
How to stop automation:
Open the same fleet, click the automation icon again, and disable the route. This stops the automated task immediately.
Automation helps you save time and keep multiple fleets working in the background, but make sure you have enough crew, food, fuel, and resources to sustain the tasks.
