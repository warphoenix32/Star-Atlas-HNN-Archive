# Powers and Organizations of Galia

The Galia Expanse is governed by more than three major factions. Its order depends on a dense network of councils, governments, houses, corporations, shipbuilders, academies, media systems, religious movements, ecological powers, criminal markets, and autonomous territories. Some reinforce the postwar order; others survive by working around it.

This page follows the preferred taxonomy of the captured Star Atlas Lore Repository. It is a map of major institutional forms rather than a complete encyclopedia.

## The Council of Peace

The Council of Peace is the central post-Convergence War governing body in the captured lore. It brings MUD, ONI, and Ustur into a shared institutional order while leaving major inequalities and opposition intact. The source snapshot describes a rotating political structure, ministries, military capacity, and contested representation. [Council of Peace source](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-D530A59F4FDE.md)

This lore institution is unrelated to the real-world Star Atlas Council elected through POLIS governance.

## Governments, houses, and territorial powers

The taxonomy includes dynastic houses, local governments, independent states, pirate societies, and AI-controlled territories. House Busan and other human houses sit beside governments such as Kamec, Mycenas, and Pergamos. Ka-dara is modeled as an AI-governed territory organized around Ingbus and the attempted restoration of the extinct Ka-raviam civilization. [Ka-dara](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-D954B959C4B6.md)

These entities should not all be flattened into "factions." Their narrower forms - house, government, territory, corporation, order, or network - carry historical meaning.

## Commerce and soft power

The Meridian Trading Company represents commercial power used as political influence. The captured source describes it as a trade and logistics company whose contracts, credit, and control of infrastructure advance the interests of the Synod. [Meridian Trading Company](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-C345AEBBD4A7.md)

The Black Market Auction House occupies the opposite side of commerce: a mobile underworld institution that trades prohibited goods and survives through the dependence and vulnerability of powerful clients. [Black Market Auction House](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-49B94941D03A.md)

## Manufacturers

The canonical lore taxonomy identifies Opal Industries, VZUS, and Calico Industries as manufacturers:

- Opal combines MUD and Ustur design traditions. [Opal Industries](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-5D75710B645F.md)
- VZUS is the leading Ustur manufacturer in the snapshot, associated with defensive hull construction. [VZUS](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-C57BAD3B0897.md)
- Calico emerged from MUD and ONI collaboration and emphasizes cultural integration, civilian systems, and non-militaristic design. [Calico Industries](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-7754F49AEC24.md)

The wider Fimbul lineage is represented through organizations and splinters rather than one undifferentiated manufacturer. Fimbul BYOS emphasizes modular, accessible hulls, while Fimbul ECOS connects shipbuilding to the ecological movement and the Superphoenix. [Fimbul BYOS](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-AED0E08C9EDD.md) [Fimbul ECOS](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-6601AA485376.md)

## Knowledge, media, and influence

Iris Academy is described as a neutral research institution whose Project Starpath helped reconnect the postwar galaxy. Its institutional role combines education, scientific authority, and political access. [Iris Academy](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-5EE88D8F5840.md)

The Real Truth Network is a decentralized media platform whose competing channels operate outside the major factions' controlled information systems. It embodies the lore's conflict over who can define public truth. [Real Truth Network](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-073830CC078B.md)

## Ecological and ideological powers

ECOS, the Earth Conscious Ontological Society, is an independent human-origin ecological faction opposed to MUD's extractive and political order. In the captured snapshot it combines spiritual ecology, agricultural reach, armed power, and a contested relationship with the Council of Peace. [ECOS](../../archive/normalized/lore/pages/SRC-LORE-REPO-C4B33E06F94F.md)

Religious orders, Ustur paths, separatists, anarchists, and local movements further complicate the political map. Their existence shows that faction membership does not exhaust belief or allegiance in Galia.
