## Preserved Official Text

The Star Atlas Showroom is available on the Epic Games Store, and getting access is now easier than ever. Game keys are distributed through our official Discord server, no badge or Faction Fleet enrollment is required.
How to Get a Game Key
Join the Star Atlas Discord: https://discord.gg/staratlas
In the Start Here (INFORMATION) category, go to the
🔑┃game-access-key
channel
Use the command:
/key
The bot will instantly reply with your personal Epic Games key for the Showroom
How to Redeem Your Key on Epic Games
Open or install the Epic Games Launcher
Log into your Epic Games account
Click your profile name in the upper-right corner
Select “Redeem Code” from the dropdown
Enter the game key you received from Discord
Open your Library — Star Atlas will be there!
