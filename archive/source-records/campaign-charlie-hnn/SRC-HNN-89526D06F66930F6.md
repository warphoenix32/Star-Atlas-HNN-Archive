In select editions of the graphic novel, single characters were discovered at the bottom corners of each page within the manuscript. This first episode contained 13 distinct characters within its pages. However, there was simply not enough data to reconcile a complete record. As intriguing as the secret message may have been, linguists and cryptographers alike would have to wait until another record was found.
