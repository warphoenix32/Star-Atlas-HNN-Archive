## Preserved Official Text

A maximum rental period of 24 Days is enforced to protect fleet owners. In cases where a renter loses access to their wallet or disappears, the system automatically returns the fleet to the owner once the rental period ends. This safeguard ensures that you are not left without control over your asset.
