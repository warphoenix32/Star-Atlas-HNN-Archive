## Classified Claims

- Mining and transport are two of the most important activities in Holosim.
- They allow you to gather raw resources and move them between bases to keep your fleets and crafting running.
- Mining resources Go to a starbase controlled by your faction: every starbase has its own available raw materials.
- Check resources: click on the starbase and open the Details tab to see what can be mined there.
- Undock your fleet: fleets must be undocked to start mining.
- Start mining: select your fleet, click Start Mining, then confirm with Initiate Mining.
- Monitor your cargo: resources are stored in your fleet’s cargo hold.
- Stop mining when full: click your fleet, select Stop Mining, then dock at a base to unload your cargo.
- Any ship can mine, but mining ships are more efficient and consume fewer supplies.
- Transporting resources Dock your fleet at a starbase with resources: load items from the base inventory into your fleet’s cargo.
- Undock and set your destination: send your fleet to another faction-controlled starbase.
- Dock at the destination: once arrived, dock your fleet.
- Unload cargo: move the resources into the base inventory.
- Any ship can transport cargo, though transport ships are designed for efficiency and larger loads.
