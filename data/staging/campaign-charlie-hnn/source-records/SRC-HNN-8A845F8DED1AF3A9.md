Star Atlas: A New Social Frontier
Do we need a new Social Network?
Oni Merchant4 min read·Jan 15, 2023

The developing Star Atlas metaverse is a unique and immersive gaming experience that combines the elements of gaming, space exploration, and cryptocurrency. While the game itself is already becoming a captivating experience, imagine for a moment the addition of a simple, yet visually appealing social network on the Solana blockchain, built for browser and Unreal Engine 5 integration. This would open up the possibility of changing the Star Atlas community forever.

One of the main benefits of a social network on Web3 is the ability for players to easily connect and form communities with one another based on real value. The Star Atlas space is already developing into something different, where players find themselves transacting AAA digital assets with people they’ve never met before. A system fully taking advantage of crypto’s trustless environment would allow players to easily connect with one another, exchange information, and achieve shared goals. This would increase the sense of community and collaboration within the game, making the experience more engaging and rewarding for all players.
In the Star Atlas metaverse, individuals will have the task of gathering resources, crafting items, and trading with one another to build and expand their ships and economic standing. Building a new type of dynamic system, specific to Star Atlas, would allow players to easily connect with one another, create smaller groups, join guilds, exchange resources, and trade items while the game itself is still being developed. The freedom of competition and collaboration would be much more liberating… making the experience rewarding for all life-forms.

Social Networking Built For Web3 and Blockchain
One might ask, why not simply create Discord integration straight into the game itself and forgo this idea completely? Of course, this could eventually happen and would be cool. But after all, it’s nearly the same thing as having a second PC monitor. We need a system built from the ground up that visually represents a new type of world…a system built specifically for a metaverse that can interact with everything in the Star Atlas economy and the crypto community itself.
Why put up with the same old MMO friends list, chat, and trade window when we can think bigger, and have something new altogether…inside and outside the system? A social network built specifically for the metaverse would allow for seamless exchange of information and value. Building a ground-up project would also enhance the overall appeal for the Star Atlas metaverse. Unreal Engine 5 is known for its stunning graphics and advanced rendering capabilities, and the integration of a new-age system would allow for players to see and connect with friends and acquaintances across the internet in an incredibly unique way. Something of this nature could quite possibly revolutionize the industry as a whole.
In conclusion, a social network using blockchain, with cross-platform capability between browser and the Star Atlas game, would greatly enhance the overall experience for everyone, both now and in the future. The ability for players to easily connect and form communities with one another, trade and exchange items and resources, engage in space combat, and experience advanced visuals would greatly enhance the sense of community, competition, and collaboration within the metaverse.
I believe that the Star Atlas community is ready to lead the next step in gaming evolution and in the metaverse as a whole.
