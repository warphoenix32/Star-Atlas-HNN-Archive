Start playing Star Atlas in under 15 minutes. This guide will quickly walk you through how to add a crypto wallet, buy a ship, and start Star Atlas Faction Fleet.

Star Atlas Overview
To start, Star Atlas is a grand-strategy, space exploration, massively multiplayer online role playing game built in Unreal Engine 5 and backed by the Solana Blockchain. The first Star Atlas mini-game, Faction Fleet, is available in web bowser. SAGE will also be playable in a web-browser mini-game by the end of 2022. Currently, the UE5 virtual environment of ships you own is available on the Epic Games store. Eventually, the full game will be released as a downloadable client from the Epic Games store.
Press enter or click to view image in full sizeStar Atlas SCREAM work in progress design
Major breakthroughs in recent technology created an opportunity for a futuristic science fiction experience that is entirely new and groundbreaking. Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. Blockchain technology using the Solana protocol established a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas creates an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership. That’s the basics about the new video game, Star Atlas, and what you need to know before playing.
1. Downloading A Wallet
Since Star Atlas is a blockchain game, it requires a cryptocurrency wallet to store your digital tokens and in-game currency.
Step 1 — In any web browser download and install a Solana wallet that is compatible with Star Atlas. We recommend Phantom — How to add a Phantom Wallet
Wallets That Are Fully Supported by Phantom
Optional — Hardware WalletWe strongly recommend taking the extra step in security and using a hardware wallet in conjunction with your Phantom wallet. This acts like two factor authentication and can help prevent your funds from accidentally being drained.
Step 2 — Add Funds to You Wallet
Star Atlas is built on the Solana ecosystem, hence you will need to add the Solana ($SOL) cryptocurrency to your wallet. This can be accomplished one of two ways.
Shorter Method: You can buy Solana or USDC directly in your Phantom Wallet.
Open your Phantom Wallet and hit Buy
From there, select either Solana or USDC
Select how much you want to deposit
Select your provider
Coinbase Pay links to your Coinbase account.
If you don’t already have a Coinbase account, we recommend MoonPay
DO NOT use FTX Pay. FTX is now bankrupt.
5. You should see your funds in your account now!
Note — Star Atlas Ships are bought in ATLAS or USDC. If you already know what ship you want to buy, we recommend getting USDC directly.
Longer Method: First, you can purchase Solana from a Centralized Exchange (CEX) like Coinbase or Binance. You can then send Solana tokens from your CEX account directly to your Phantom Wallet. You will need your Phantom Wallet public address or wallet address and it looks something like this:

2. Accessing the Game
As of now, Star Atlas exists in two different formats. Faction Fleet, and eventually SAGE, are web browser based mini-games. The Star Atlas Showroom is an Unreal Engine 5 (UE5) client from in the Epic Games store.
Faction Fleet and SAGE
Faction Fleet, and eventually SAGE, can be accessed at play.staratlas.com. This will take you to the main page where you will be asked to connect you Phantom Wallet. If you aren’t automatically asked, there is a connect wallet button in the left menu at the bottom.

4. Choosing A Star Atlas Faction
Before you can enlist your ships in Faction Fleet, you will first have to choose a Star Atlas faction.
There are three Star Atlas factions and they are:
OniThe ONI faction is a harmonious consortium of diverse species. The people of the Om sector, Segal, Akenat, and Neuno systems collectively set aside their differences in the hopes of a more bountiful life in space.
MudManus Ultimus Divinum, The Last Divine Hand, is a spacefaring faction of humans originating from their home planet of Earth. After uniting under a common cause inspired by a benevolent Photoli scout, humans harnessed Earth’s resources to set off for the stars.
UsturUstur cybernetic sentient beings have an origin shrouded in complex mystery. One legend tells of a Dyson sphere-powered supercomputer harnessing the energy of a magnetar class neutron star. The energy throughput allowed the installed AI to advance beyond its physical location and withdrew from the parent machine without a trace.
Press enter or click to view image in full sizeStar Atlas Factions
Important: Your faction selection is tied to your wallet! You cannot change it later.
Think of your wallet as an account. The only way to change your faction is to create a new wallet (account) and go through the selection process again.
If you do decide to change factions later, you can always transfer assets from your old wallet to your new wallet. However, any perks associated with your account will not be transferred.
Selecting a Faction
As of writing, there are no gameplay advantages to any faction. The faction selection process will show you where each faction gains certain bonuses, but the extent of those bonuses are currently unknown. Some known bonuses have been stated by the developers but are currently not implemented in the game.
Known Bonus:
Factions should gain loyalty points quicker with their Faction Aligned Companies (FACs)
At present, selecting one faction over another can come down to personal preference or guild preference. If you’ve already joined a Star Atlas guild, or are considering joining one, they may have faction restrictions in place.
When it comes to flying ships, factions do not restrict your ability to pilot a ship.
