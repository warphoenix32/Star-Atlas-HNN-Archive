First-ever Meta-Poster Experience Featuring Blond:ish and Jason Silva
Star Atlas NFT Debut Gives a Taste of the Metaverse
Back to top
Star Atlas has entered the next level of manifesting the future with the Blond:ish and Jason Silva meta-poster debut. This is the first of fourteen NFT meta-posters in the ReBirth: Genesis of a Metaverse series of next-gen experiences. Featuring augmented reality, ambient music and AAA graphics, the inaugural NFT offers an insight into the galactic metaverse, blurring the lines between the real and the virtual.
Star Atlas: ReBirth /// Discovery of Iris /// Meta-Poster 1/14
Vivie-Ann Bakos, better known as Blond:ish, a sound artist specializing in house and techno music. Her work has been inspiring and fueling creative tour de force Burning Man along with dozens of music venues worldwide. As a grand gesture to the Star Atlas metaverse, Blond:ish contributed the dreamy, slow soundscape to this week’s NFT. The ethereal vibe of her musical contribution sets the scene for the genesis moment of Star Atlas, the rebirth of the universe as we know it into an interactive and collaborative metaverse.
Blond:ish
Alongside Jason Silva, the storyteller, futurist, and keynote speaker, the artistic duo worked together to create Star Atlas’s custom soundscape for the meta-poster series’ debut NFT, “Discovery of Iris.” Silva is well known for hosting brilliant and thought-provoking National Geographic’s BrainGames and his YouTube series, Shots of Awe, with over 100 million views. Through the transcendental artistic medium of the meta-poster, Atlassians can hear Silva’s voice reverberating through the orbit of planet Iris, an echoing of the genesis moment into the future where all things are possible.
Jason Silva
The “Discovery of Iris” reveals the rogue planet Iris, dense with valuable materials and emanating mysterious energy that poster owners can now experience and explore through Star Atlas’s AR and multi-modal soundscape.
The next meta-poster NFT will drop this Saturday, May 1st, and will be followed by one drop per week until July 24th. Prepare yourself for a journey deeper into the future and a metaverse reborn.
Star Atlas: ReBirth /// Discovery of Iris /// Meta-Poster 1/14 (Augmented Reality Experience)
Star Atlas is a virtual gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state of the art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video game, and decentralized financial technologies.
Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic-quality visuals. Blockchain technology using the Solana protocol established a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. NFTs obtained and traded within Star Atlas creates an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership. To learn more, please visit staratlas.com
