## Overview

**Verzan** is a sanctified nexus where **God Ahr** was last heard from before his departure (~2400). The Photoli Traveler [Ahr](../species/photoli.md) — the being MUD believes was God — guided humanity from Earth to Galia, then left from Verzan with a promise: he would return when humanity found its *true home*. That promise made Verzan the holiest ground in [MUD](../factions/mud.md) theology.

The sector is defined by **unforgiving landscapes**, **harsh living conditions**, devout pilgrims, and a festering rivalry between two religious powers — one sanctioned by MUD, the other secretly controlled by an alien intelligence.

---

## The Spiritual Battleground

### The Church (Holy Servants)

The **Church** rules Verzan with an iron hand, controlling pilgrimages, tithes, and doctrinal interpretation. Led by **High Prelate Cassandra Vael**, it views Verzan as a sacred trust — the site where God spoke his last earthly words.

### The Order of Light

A rival religious power operates from a moon purchased from MUD:

| Detail | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | [Order of Light](../factions/order_of_light.md) |
| **Nature** | Ancient congregation of Photoli worshippers |
| **Creator** | **Photoli Reh** — a [Photoli](../species/photoli.md) who created the Order to gain influence over humans |
| **Location** | Principal temple on a moon devoid of resources |
| **Secret** | Reh shares minor Photoli powers with senior members to **brainwash** recruits |
| **The Church's View** | Heretical — but their growing influence among the populace is undeniable |

### 🔴 Archon Secret

The Order of Light predates Verzan, but Reh **expanded to Verzan deliberately** — Ahr's departure left a spiritual vacuum that made this population especially susceptible to Photoli-worship. The irony is profound: MUD settlers came to Verzan because God (Ahr, a Photoli) spoke here. Now another Photoli (Reh) is using that same reverence to build a rival power base.
