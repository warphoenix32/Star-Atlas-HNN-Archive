Galia Merchants Sponsor COPA 2024 Design Contest and Giveaway
By Hologram News NetworkPublished On: 13 February 2024Tags: copa, galia merchants, Star Atlas Community
Galia Merchants is hosting a design contest for COPA 2024. Winners receive the opportunity to convert designs into t-shirts and receive a portion of revenue for their work!
The contest will be judged by a panel of popular Star Atlas community members and design artists, including the Director of Star Atlas Product Design!

4K
300 dpi
