## Preserved Official Text

The Battle Pass in Holosim is an optional upgrade that provides extra rewards and bonuses as you play. It is not required to enjoy the game, you can still complete quests, form fleets, and explore freely without it.
Key things to know:
Optional purchase: costs $10 per Chapter.
Exclusive cosmetics & perks: such as an avatar border and extra crew bonuses.
Daily streak boosts: get more rewards each day you check in.
zATLAS multiplier: significantly increases the amount of zATLAS you earn from daily streaks.
Seasonal: each Chapter introduces a new Battle Pass with fresh rewards.
The Battle Pass is designed as a way for players who want more rewards to enhance their experience, but the game remains fully accessible without it.
