## Star Atlas Community Week: A Galactic Celebration

Star Atlas

Star Atlas

The esteemed community of Star Atlas is cordially invited to partake in an extraordinary celebration — the Star Atlas Community Week. This event marks a significant occasion, uniting ONIs, Usturs, and MUDs for a grand five-day gala, commencing soon in the vibrant expanses of Galia. Join us, and you will have the chance to win various exciting prizes!


## An Unprecedented Celebration Across the Ecosystem

Star Atlas is renowned for its dynamic and dedicated community, a group that stands as a pinnacle within the web3 gaming ecosystem. The citizens of the Galia expanse have been honored consecutively for two years with the esteemed Best Community Award in web3 Gaming.

Their unwavering commitment has been instrumental in the evolution of Star Atlas, which is evident through continuous feedback across various community channels and the creation of exceptional content, tools, and projects under the Star Atlas IP Build Program.

In recognition of this fervent enthusiasm, Star Atlas is delighted to host a five-day festivity to take in the Star Atlas community’s official Discord channel, scheduled from February 26th to March 1st. These events are tailored to engage and entertain our players and their Decentralized Autonomous Corporations (DACs).


## Detailed Schedule of Events:

February 26

- The Community Week Kickoff
A live event, hosted by our community team — Santi, José, and Dominic — will inaugurate the week. This event will take place on Discord and include an array of giveaways, news, and exciting announcements to get everyone started!

Attendance is highly recommended, as we will also provide details on every other event we will host during this festival.


## February 27

- Special Event
We have a special event prepared for the 27th of February.

While we don’t want to spoil the surprise, we are aiming to create some good memories with the community, so make sure to find some hours of your day to join us on Discord on February 27 . A day-long and very unique party awaits you!


## February 28

- Atlas Brew #100
The Atlas Brew, Star Atlas Weekly Community show, is celebrating an impressive mark of one hundred editions! This incredible milestone was only possible due to the passion and support of Star Atlas community, so we are cooking a special episode full of surprises, giveaways, and entertainment to give back some of the love our community has bestowed upon us.

As always, don’t forget to bring your favorite brew and enjoy a delightful moment with us on Discord !


## February 29

- COPA (Guild awards)
Another highlight of the community week is the second edition of the Council of Peace Assembly, an event aimed at letting the spotlights shine over the talented Decentralized Autonomous Corporations (DACs) of Star Atlas metaverse!

Following the last edition, we will have a friendly competition, where our DACS will present all the work they have been putting in to help develop the Star Atlas ecosystem.

Nevertheless, improvements have been made to ensure this edition’s competition is even more fun!

More information about that will be released in the coming days, so keep your eyes open, as some exciting prizes are on the line!


## March 1

- Joni Awards (Community awards)
On the last day of this epic festival, we close our party by celebrating the individuals who work hard every day to push the boundaries of the Galia expanse through our legendary Joni Awards!

Those individuals who never cease to amaze us with their relentless dedication to Star Atlas will be recognized and rewarded for their passion.

To support your favorite community member, you first must fill out the form, which will go live tomorrow, 01/26/2024, and stay up for voting until 02/09/2024!

The awards being given this year are:

- Community Member of the Year
- Content Creator of the Year
- Most Entertaining Video of the Year
- Best Educational Video of the Year
- Best Community Tool of the Year
- Community Helper of the Year
- Most Knowledgeable of the Year
- Star Atlas IP Project of the Year
- Best Star Atlas In-game Photo of the Year
The selection of the winners will be voted by a panel of judges and community members, with 20% of the weight to the panel and 80% for the community decisions.

So make sure to show up and show your support for your favorite community member!

Join us in the First Community Week Festival on Star Atlas Discord !!


## ABOUT STAR ATLAS

Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state-of-the-art blockchain technologies, real-time graphics and multiplayer video games.

Using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite real-time graphics technology allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. In addition, blockchain technology using Solana establishes a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Player assets obtained and traded within Star Atlas create an economy that replicates tangible world assets and ownership.

To learn more, join a faction at Play.StarAtlas.com , get a spaceship in the Galactic Marketplace , and start playing the SAGE Labs browser game to explore, gather, craft and prosper on the galactic frontier.

Then, download the UE5 PC Game from the Epic Games Store.

Participate in governance of the Star Atlas DAO . Send your spaceships on a deep space mission by enrolling them in a Faction Fleet and harvest resources with Faction Claims .
