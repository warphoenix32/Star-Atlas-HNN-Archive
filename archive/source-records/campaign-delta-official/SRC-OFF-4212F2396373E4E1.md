## Preserved Official Text

The Star Atlas team does not publish official staking rewards tables for Faction Fleet (SCORE).However, several community guilds and contributors have created detailed spreadsheets and tools to help players estimate staking returns based on ship models, resources, and durations.
Where to Look
Community-Created Resources
Many guilds have shared staking data across platforms like Discord, Twitter, and YouTube. These resources are created by active community members who regularly track performance and reward rates.
While these tools are not official, they are widely used and helpful for understanding potential returns
Join the Star Atlas Discord
The #help channel and guild threads in our official Discord are great places to:
Ask for staking calculators or updated spreadsheets
Get guidance from experienced players
Discover new tools shared by the community
