## Preserved Official Text

In the current version of the Star Atlas UE5 game, all ships available in the Star Atlas ecosystem can be flown, and most are fully equipped for combat in various game modes
While not all ships are optimized for racing, every flyable ship can be summoned, piloted, and put to the test across multiple gameplay experiences including multiplayer, mining, and dogfighting.
Ship System Updates
Each ship now includes:
Functional HUDs with updated systems
Combat-ready weaponry for PvP and PvE scenarios
New handling, flight tuning, and animations
Summoning and docking support at terminals
Featured Flyable Ships
Here are seven standout ships that have received full UE5 implementation:
Pearce X4 – A nimble, entry-level fighter built by Pearce Industries
Opal Jet – A sleek, faction-favorite racing ship from Opal
Fimbul Lowbie – A medium-zone transport with distinctive flair
Fimbul Airbike – The most affordable racer in Star Atlas
Pearce X6 – The standard patrol ship of the Council of Peace
Fimbul ECOS Unibomba – A compact but deadly bomber for the ECOS
Floyd Liner – A unique, non-purchasable showpiece available for all players
