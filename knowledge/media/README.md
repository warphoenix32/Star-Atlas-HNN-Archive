# Media and Creators

This domain documents the publications, channels, shows, creators, and source lineages that preserve Star Atlas history. It is an archival navigation layer, not an endorsement list. Researchers should consult the relevant profile before relying on a source so that publication identity, institutional affiliation, capture coverage, author or speaker attribution, and known preservation gaps remain visible.

Official status establishes institutional publication, not the truth of every claim or completion of every announced action. Discord and X have validated but bounded preserved corpora; the Medium profile is discovery-oriented and does not claim article-level completeness. When a claim depends on delivery, execution, payment, governance outcome, or event occurrence, use evidence capable of establishing that later state.

## Archival citation standard

A durable citation should preserve the source ID, title or exact post text, publisher, author or speaker attribution status, canonical URL, publication date, capture provenance, and relevant timestamp. Researchers should also record whether the item is original reporting, an interview, an editorial, an official announcement, a summary, a reshare, or repackaged material. Corrections, mirrors, deletions, and superseding records belong in the lineage rather than being silently substituted.
