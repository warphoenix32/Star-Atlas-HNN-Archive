Hello Moon will provide Star Atlas gaming products with real-time scalable access to Solana blockchain data, enabling seamless player-owned gaming experience

Star Atlas, the highly popular Solana-based game with millions of daily transactions, unveiled a strategic partnership with Hello Moon, an industry-leading blockchain indexer. The alliance aims to enhance the robustness and scalability of Star Atlas real-time data handling capabilities, effectively replacing its proprietary in-house solution.

Hello Moon’s solution is robust, providing Star Atlas with a live data stream facilitated through its cutting-edge technology stack — Geyser, a plugin on the Solana validator, and Kafka, a highly scalable distribution pipeline.

This collaboration brings about near-limitless scalability and improved game performance, leading to a better gaming experience for Star Atlas players worldwide.

“Hello Moon is about connecting the blockchain world in a way that makes data more accessible and usable. Our partnership with Star Atlas empowers us to showcase our technical prowess by providing real-time, Solana blockchain data for their web3 gaming ecosystem.”

“Working with Hello Moon equips Star Atlas with the speed and scalability to deliver smoother and more immersive gaming experience, setting a new standard for data handling in gaming.”

- Frank Berreth, Principal Engineer at Hello Moon

The partnership marks a new chapter for data handling in gaming and presents enormous potential for the future of blockchain-enabled tech. It showcases how industry collaboration can leverage shared technology to improve product performance and enhance user experience, ultimately driving the entire blockchain and web3 sector forward.

ABOUT STAR ATLAS

Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state-of-the-art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video games, and decentralized financial technologies.

Using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite, real-time graphics technology allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. In addition, blockchain technology using the Solana protocol establishes a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas create an economy that replicates tangible world assets and ownership. To learn more, visit StarAtlas.com, join a faction at Play.StarAtlas.com, send your spaceships on a deep space mission by enrolling them in a Faction Fleet, harvesting resources with Faction Claims and download the Showroom on your PC. Hunt for treasures by playing Escape Velocity. Participate in governance of the Star Atlas DAO.

ABOUT HELLO MOON

Hello Moon is a leading blockchain indexer, known for providing efficient, real-time data streams to blockchain and web3 companies. Hello Moon prides itself on its ability to handle large volumes of data, providing the scalability needed for fast-growing tech companies in the web3 space. Find out more on the website.
