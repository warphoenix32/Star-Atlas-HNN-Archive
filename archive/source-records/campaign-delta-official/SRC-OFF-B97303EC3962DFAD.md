## Preserved Official Text

The metaverse is a connected digital universe where users can interact, play, trade, build and even earn—in real time and often across multiple experiences.
In Star Atlas, the metaverse comes to life through a combination of:
Immersive 3D gameplay in Unreal Engine 5
A real in-game economy powered by crypto and NFTs
Social and strategic collaboration between players and guilds (DACs)
Decentralized governance through the Star Atlas DAO
Where Does the Word Come From?
The word "metaverse" originally came from the 1992 sci-fi novel Snow Crash, blending “meta” (beyond) and “universe.” It describes a virtual world where people live digital lives through avatars—and that vision is now becoming a reality.
How Is Star Atlas a Metaverse?
Unlike traditional games, Star Atlas isn’t just a single game—it’s a growing ecosystem:
You can fly ships, explore and battle in the 3D world (UE5 Game)
You can craft, mine,and earn in a browser-based strategy game (SAGE)
You can trade NFTs and resources in a live economy
You can own your assets, vote on proposals and participate in the game’s future via the Star Atlas DAO
Together, these features make Star Atlas a living metaverse where players aren’t just users—they’re citizens, builders and stakeholders
