## Classified Claims

- Sometimes you’ll need to move crew from one starbase to another.
- This is done with fleets that have passenger slots available.
- Steps to transport crew Open the Hangar menu: click on the base, then select Hangar.
- Select your fleet: click the fleet you’ll use for transport.
- Go to the Passenger tab: here you’ll see available crew at the base.
- Load crew: click Fill capacity or manually choose how many crew members to embark.
- Undock and travel: send your fleet to the destination starbase.
- Dock at the new base: once arrived, open the Hangar again.
- Disembark crew: move the crew from your fleet into the new base.
- ⚠️ Make sure to always leave the required crew in the fleet.
- Without them, the fleet won’t be able to continue operating.
