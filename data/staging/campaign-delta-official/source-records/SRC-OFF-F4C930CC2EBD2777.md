## Preserved Official Text

Great news! Ledger support is now available in the Star Atlas Unreal Engine 5 Game!In this article, we’ll show you how to connect your Ledger hardware wallet by following a few simple steps.
Step-by-step Instructions
Step 1: Connect and unlock your Ledger wallet
Connect your Ledger to your computer and enter your PIN.
Step 2: Open the Solana app on your Ledger
On your Ledger device, open the Solana app.
Step 3: Import your wallet
If this is your first time launching the game:On the wallet connection/creation screen, click Import Wallet
If you already have a profile:Click your profile icon in the top-right corner of the screen, then select Link an existing wallet
Step 4: Select the Ledger option
Click Use a Ledger Hardware Wallet.
Step 5: Choose your derivation path and account
Select a Derivation Path and your Account, then click Select Account.
Step 6: Confirm your public key
Confirm the public key displayed on your Ledger device.
Step 7: Create a wallet name and password
Choose a wallet name and a password for your local access.
