## Preserved Official Text

Star Atlas ships are NFT-powered vehicles that form the backbone of gameplay across the entire Star Atlas ecosystem.
Each ship is a unique digital asset stored in your wallet and used to explore space, engage in battles, transport goods, craft items and more. Ships are composed of various components and modules and require crew members to operate at full potential
Utility & Gameplay Integration
Ships are more than just collectibles—they’re essential for progression:
Faction Fleet: Stake ships to earn passive ATLAS rewards
SAGE Labs: Use your ships to mine, craft, transport and soon fight
UE5 Game (Epic Games): Fly your ship in immersive 3D environments
Fleet Rental: Assemble and rent your Sage fleet for passive income
Components, Crew & Customization
Each ship includes:
Modular Components – Can be upgraded or swapped to suit your strategy (e.g. weapons, shields, scanners)
Crew Slots – Most ships require crew to function fully. Matching the right crew to the right ship improves efficiency and unlocks buffs
Aptitudes – Ships have native aptitudes that align with specific gameplay roles, like combat, freight, or repair
🚀 Proper crew assignment improves performance. Crew members trained together will receive synergy buffs, while mismatched assignments may lead to de-buffs
How to Get a Ship
Buy directly from the Star Atlas Marketplace using ATLAS or USDC
Trade with other players on the secondary market
Craft in SAGE Labs
