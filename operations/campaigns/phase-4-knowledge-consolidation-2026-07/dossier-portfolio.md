The portfolio deliberately favors coherent institutional and historical stories
over one-public-page-per-registry. Internal Source IDs, workflow labels, and
taxonomy remain available in evidence panels rather than dominating the future
reader experience.

The next gate is deliberate consolidation of the ten dossier histories. Lore
consolidation must create or expand the necessary reader-oriented entity pages
without treating the captured taxonomy snapshot as proof of current upstream
completeness.
