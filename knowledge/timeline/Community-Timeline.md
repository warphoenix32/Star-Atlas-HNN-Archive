# Community Timeline

This chronology tracks publications, guilds, competitions, conferences, and community initiatives. Community-source milestones remain attributed to their publishers.

## 2021

### Estimated 2021-07-18 — Aephia Industries opened

**Description:** Aephia's first-anniversary and later anniversary publications date the organization's opening to July 18, 2021. **Significance:** Aephia became one of the earliest persistent Star Atlas DACs and later a major archival publication. **Affected entities:** Aephia Industries, Prometheus, Funcracker, Star Atlas DAC community. **Sources:** [SRC-AEPHIA-472542398C7633EF](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-472542398C7633EF.md), [SRC-AEPHIA-A9A7DC7C1D1E800F](../../archive/source-records/campaign-alpha-aephia/SRC-AEPHIA-A9A7DC7C1D1E800F.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Retrospective community self-history; no 2021 source record survives in the archive. **Superseded:** No.
