## Preserved Official Text

Fleets are the backbone of Holosim, they allow you to mine, transport, fight, and explore the universe. Forming your first fleet is part of the tutorial, but you can create new fleets anytime.
Steps to form a fleet:
Open the Hangar: click on your Central Space Station (CSS) on the map, then select “Hangar” from the Star Base menu.
Click “Form a New Fleet”: you’ll see a list of available ships.
Assign ships to the fleet: choose the ships you want to add. Each fleet has a maximum capacity limit.
Name your fleet (optional): make it easier to identify different groups of ships.
Finalize: once everything is set, confirm to create your new fleet.
Your fleet will now appear in the “My Fleets” menu, ready for missions like mining, transport, or combat.
