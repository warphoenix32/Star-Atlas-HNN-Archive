Star Atlas Launches A.I.-Generated Art Contest

Artificial Intelligence Art Contest Invites Worldwide Community to Get Inspired By Star Atlas and Create A.I. Derivative Art

Galactic Explorers!

While you’re waiting for the 426LIVE: NYK event, here is an exciting Star Atlas competition to get you ready for this unreal event! Many of us in the Star Atlas community have been obsessively playing around with A.I.-generated art. So we thought, let’s make a contest!

Harness your inner artist from the future for a chance to win some incredible prizes and faction glory!

Summary of Contest

Contest Rules: Please read the full Contest Rules (see below). Entry into this Contest constitutes your acceptance of these Contest Rules.

How to enter:

  • Tweet an A.I.-generated still picture of the planet or other environment in the Star Atlas galaxy you’re most excited to see or visit.
  • Make sure to follow and tag @staratlas in your tweet, add the #WeAreStarAtlas hashtag, and specify which A.I. tool you used (e.g., Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL·E).
  • Please keep it fair and don’t enhance the A.I.-created images with any other editing software.
  • There is no limit on the number of submissions.
  • No purchase is necessary to enter or win.

Timing:

  • All submissions must be received by September 27th, 2022, at 12PM (EDT).
  • The winner (s) will be announced on a Special Twitter Spaces on September 28th, 2022. Winners will be required to provide a valid email address, mailing address, and Solana wallet address, which will be used to fulfill the prize and ensure eligibility to receive a prize. ATMTA, Inc. reserves the right to disqualify a winner if not eligible based on these eligibility requirements.

Judging:

All entries will be judged on Star Atlas look-and-feel, creativity, uniqueness, and ability to inspire.

Who can enter:

Anyone with a Solana wallet address, except for employees or contractors of ATMTA, Inc., its affiliates, subsidiaries, and agencies, and members of their immediate family or persons living in the same household.

Awards:

  • 1st place: Star Atlas Explorer Kit — 3 Fimbul Lowbie + 2 Fimbul Airbike + Merch kit (t-shirt, hat, and mug) + resources (ammunition, food, fuel, toolkit). Approximate Retail Value: $270.00
  • 2nd place: 2 Fimbul Lowbie + 1 Fimbul Airbike. Approximate Retail Value: $135.00
  • 3rd place: 1 Fimbul Lowbie. Approximate Retail Value: $60.00

Community A.I. Art Contest: Full Contest Rules

  • NO PURCHASE IS NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN THE COMMUNITY A.I. ART CONTEST (“CONTEST”). Entry into this Contest constitutes your acceptance of these Contest Rules.
  • ELIGIBILITY: Open to persons with a Solana wallet address, except for employees or contractors of ATMTA, Inc. (“Sponsor”), its affiliates, subsidiaries, and agencies, and members of their immediate family or persons living in the same household. Void where prohibited.
  • CONTEST OBJECTIVE: The objective of the Contest is to show what your favorite Star Atlas environment or planet will look like.
  • HOW TO ENTER: Tweet an A.I.-generated still picture inspired by Star Atlas (“Entry”). Follow and tag @staratlas, add @WeAreStarAtlas, add #WeAreStarAtlas hashtag, and specify which A.I. tool you used to create your art (e.g., Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL·E). Each Contest participant (“Entrant”) can submit unlimited Entries.

CONTEST RULES:

  1. All Entries must be received by September 27th, 2022 at 12pm (EDT).
  2. Each Entry must be the original work of the Entrant and must not infringe upon the copyrights, trademarks, rights of privacy, publicity, or other intellectual property or other rights of any person and must not violate any law.
  3. Each Entry must adhere to the Star Atlas Terms of Service, including but not limited to the provisions regarding usage of Star Atlas Marks.
  4. Each Entry must be posted on Twitter with the tags @staratlas and @WeAreStarAtlas.
  5. Entrants must use a reputable A.I. program (such as Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL·E) for each Entry. Each Entry must list the A.I. program used.
  6. Entrants may not enhance their A.I.-created images with any other editing software.
  7. Sponsor reserves the right to disqualify any Entry for any reason, in its sole and absolute discretion.
  8. Odds of winning depend on the number of Entries received.

JUDGING CRITERIA: All Entries will be judged based on the judges’ determination of:

a) Star Atlas look-and-feel

b) Creativity

c) Uniqueness

d) Ability to inspire

e) Sponsor shall select the judges at its sole and absolute discretion.

f) There will be up to three Entrants selected as winners (“Winners”) by the judges.

WINNER NOTIFICATION:

Winners will be determined at the conclusion of the Contest and will be notified by Discord direct message and/or by email. Winners will be required to provide a valid email address, mailing address, and Solana wallet address, which will be used to fulfill the prize and ensure eligibility to receive the prize. At the discretion of Sponsor, a Winner may be disqualified if not eligible based on the eligibility requirements set forth above. In the event it is determined that a Winner has not complied with these Contest Rules, has failed to execute and return any required documents within the specified time period, has made false statements or a prize notification is returned as undeliverable, then the winner will be disqualified and at Sponsor’s sole discretion, the Entry with the next highest score may then be declared an alternate Winner.

  • 1st place: Star Atlas Explorer Kit — 3 Fimbul Lowbie + 2 Fimbul Airbike + Merch kit (t-shirt, hat, and mug) + resources (ammunition, food, fuel, toolkit). ARV: $270.00.
  • 2nd place: 2 Fimbul Lowbie + 1 Fimbul Airbike. ARV: $135.00.

ABOUT STAR ATLAS

Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state-of-the-art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video games, and decentralized financial technologies.

Using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite, real-time graphics technology allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. In addition, blockchain technology using the Solana protocol establishes a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas create an economy that replicates tangible world assets and ownership. To learn more, visit StarAtlas.com, join a faction at Play.StarAtlas.com and send your spaceships on a deep space mission by enrolling them in a Faction Fleet.
