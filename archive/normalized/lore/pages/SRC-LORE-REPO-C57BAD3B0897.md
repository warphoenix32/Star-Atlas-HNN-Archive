## Overview

**VZUS** is the leading Ustur manufacturer, owned and operated by the Ustur faction. Renowned across Galia Expanse for its **defensive prowess**.

## Key Facts

- **Specialty**: Unmatched **hull construction** — perfected to a degree no competitor matches.
- **Guidance**: Established under direct guidance of the Ustur Elders. One Elder once served as VZUS's **Chief Technology Officer**.
- **Products**: Ships and technology in general.
- **Philosophy**: Vessels are more than tools — they are **cherished companions**, viewed as living members of the household, as significant as any pet.
- **Design Ethos**: Steadfast guardians on voyages of discovery. Resilient and resolute.

## History

- **Original HQ**: Eternity Sector (near Ioki).
- **Relocation**: Moved to **Foy Fields** — the natural electric fields dramatically boosted production efficiency. (Eternity/Ioki deemed too sacred for heavy industry.)
- **Trend**: Other enterprises followed VZUS's move, turning Foy Fields into the Ustur economic nexus.

## Military Infrastructure

- **Vzus Defensive Belt**: Series of star fortresses in interplanetary space around Ioki. Central command on Shariput (moon).
- **Season 0**: Belt sabotaged by mercenary infiltrators, went down for hours allowing Tufa landing.
