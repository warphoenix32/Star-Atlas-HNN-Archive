Star Atlas’ Ash Details Upcoming Releases On The LIT Late Show
Posted by Krigs | Jul 15, 2022 | Community, Roadmap Updates
Star Atlas Media Host and Executive Producer Ash made a guest appearance on The LIT Late show this past Tuesday evening. The LIT Late show is a twitter space hosted by MoonRoving SOL NFTs and covers upcoming and exciting Web 3.0 projects. Ash’s appearance on the show was viewed by 291 audience members where she detailed the exciting prospects in store for the Star Atlas community.
Ash opened the dialogue answering a question from co-host Mish regarding the current development state of the Star Atlas game. Without giving exact details, Ash responded that “the first immersive UE5 module will be released this summer“. Regarding the UE5 module, one of the expected releases coming within the “next few months” is the Pre-Alpha Showroom. Ash continued that the full MMORPG game is still a few years out.
“Upcoming Releases in the Next Few Weeks”
Ash continued the conversation by coyly teasing the next features for the remainder of the year. “We have our first releases coming in a few weeks, the next one will be a few weeks after that, and then we’ll have our first real game style roll out at the end of the year” Ash comments align with the current timeline of expected Star Atlas releases. Regarding actual gameplay, it seems that the highly anticipated SCREAM module may release closer to November or December. Ash ended the topic with “Our devs are really turning it up and they have their heads buried working away“.
At one point later on during the conversation, Ash remarks that our “POLIS token will be getting more utility very soon.” The POLIS DAO is one of the most anticipated and likely releases coming within the next week. All eyes will be on July 21st for this announcement.
Ash promotes 426 Live
During the talk show, Ash talked about the 426 community meme and the live event that has spawned from this communal joke. “We will be dropping several products next Thursday during another virtual live event“. Without going into further details, Ash said these products are some of the most highly desired products that the community has been voicing their opinion for.
“We Have One of the Strongest Communities”
Ashe went on to recap the recent COPA event as the highlighting kickoff to Star Atlas summer. COPA was a digital festival with over 700 participants. Ash went on to praise the Star Atlas community. “We did the event to support our guilds and communities and we have one of the stongest communities I feel around this entire space” Star Atlas community members were also present in audience. Australian metaverse artist Arktype received a special shoutout from Ash on his recent work with The Moon Man on their recent animated digital comic.
WHAT IS THE BOND THAT KEEP THE STAR ATLAS COMMUNITY TOGETHER?
In response to the community discussion, host Mish remarked on how strong the Star Atlas community is, especially during the current bear market.
Ash succinctly replied, “We have one of the strongest communities. What contributes to that is the Star Atlas community team that makes sure our community’s needs are met and heard. Our founders really strive to have true transparency and dialogue with the community. You can hop into our discord and have a direct conversation with the CEO. We take feedback from the community and we are proactive in incorporating it into the project. We like to engage with them and make sure they feel included. We have over 50 organic guilds. They also generate their own community content. For any project the community is the backbone. Without community we have no project.”
