ATLAS Price Falls to Historic Low
Posted by Krigs | May 12, 2022 | Business
Today, the price of the ATLAS token fell to news in what is sure to be a historic moment in the Star Atlas metaverse. Late Wednesday evening, the price of ATLAS fell to below .01 USD. The crash of ATLAS, and the related POLIS token, was driven by the broader sell-off of major layer one crypto currencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum. Compounding the issue was the unpegging of the UST stable coin from the US dollar. Associated token Luna lost nearly 90% in value shortly thereafter.
The price of Solana also suffered major setbacks as investors seek to reduce risk in their portfolios across the board. The overall downtrend in crypto markets comes as the United States Federal Reserve focuses on combating soaring inflation through the tightening of fiscal policy.
Ships selling on the Galactic Marketplace have also seen a significant decrease in value as increased sell pressure drives valuation lower. For the past 6 months, ships have dropped as low as 50% from their original value. However, Wednesday’s crypto sell off has driven ship prices to new historic lows. Common ships like the Fimbul Airbike are now valued at $3.08 or roughly 80% down from its volume weighted average price.
While market sentiment remains grim for the short term, the historic low price of ATLAS represents a unique opportunity for long term investors that remain bullish on the Star Atlas project. Several rare and expensive ships are now significantly cheaper than before. These ships could potentially represent a huge upswing as crypto prices recover. Alternatively, players looking to acquire a larger ship for play purposes can now enter at a lower price point. Star Atlas intends to release its updated SCREAM module later this year which will bring a robust gameplay environment to the community.
