## Core Beliefs

### Planetary Communion

4. **Purification** â€” Full military assault. Terraforming weapons deployed. Infrastructure destroyed and returned to wilderness.
5. **Rebirth** â€” ECOS seeds are planted. The "purified" territory is claimed as a new grove.
