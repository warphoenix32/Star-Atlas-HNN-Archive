⚠️ Security Reminder: Never share your seed/recovery phrase or private key with anyone. The Star Atlas team will never ask for it under any circumstance.
If you created a wallet inside the Showroom and want to use it in Backpack (or any other Solana wallet), you can do so by importing your 24-word recovery phrase. This guide uses Backpack Wallet as the example.
Step-by-Step Guide
1. Download Backpack Wallet
Backpack is available as a browser extension for Chrome, Brave, Firefox, and Edge.Download Backpack Wallet
2. Open Backpack and Choose Import
Launch the Backpack extension.
Click “I already have a wallet.”
Select the Solana Chain
Select "Recovery Phrase"
3. Enter Your Recovery Phrase
Enter the 24-word phrase that was provided to you when you created your UE5 Star Altas wallet.
After entering your phrase, Backpack will search for any associated accounts
Click "Continue" and create a password
