## Classified Claims

- Locking your POLIS tokens grants you POLIS Voting Power (PVP), which allows you to participate in governance and shape the future of Star Atlas.
- You can lock POLIS on the official governance platform: https://govern.staratlas.com/lockers Step-by-Step: Lock POLIS for Governance Power Go to the Governance Website Visit govern.staratlas.com/lockers Click “Connect Wallet” and approve the connection using your Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom, Backpack, etc.).
- Click “Lock POLIS” On the Lockers page, locate the POLIS Locker section.
- Click the “Lock POLIS” button to begin the process.
- Choose Lock Period & Amount Scroll down to the lock settings.
- Choose your lock duration using the slider (longer locks = more PVP).
- Review the summary and confirm you're happy with your inputs.
- Approve the Transaction Click “Lock Token” and approve the transaction in your wallet.
- Once confirmed, your POLIS is locked and you’ve earned PVP, giving you access to vote and contribute in the DAO.
- 📌 Notes Locked POLIS cannot be withdrawn before the end of the chosen lock period.
- The longer you lock, the more PVP you receive per POLIS.
- You’ll be able to see your active locks and rewards on the same page

Choose Lock Period & Amount
Scroll down to the lock settings.
Select the amount of POLIS you want to lock.
Choose your lock duration using the slider (longer locks = more PVP).
Review the summary and confirm you're happy with your inputs.
Approve the Transaction
Click “Lock Token” and approve the transaction in your wallet.
Once confirmed, your POLIS is locked and you’ve earned PVP, giving you access to vote and contribute in the DAO.
📌 Notes
