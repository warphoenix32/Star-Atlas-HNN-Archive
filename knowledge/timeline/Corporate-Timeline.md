# Corporate Timeline

This chronology records ATMTA funding, partnerships, staffing changes, acquisitions, and institutional milestones. Official announcements establish what the company stated; they do not independently establish later commercial performance.

## 2021

### 2021-06-24 — Seed round announced with Serum leading

**Description:** ATMTA announced that Star Atlas had closed a seed round led by Serum. **Significance:** This is the earliest dated financing announcement preserved in the official archive. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, Star Atlas, Serum, Michael Wagner. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-723C7D67B80744DF](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-723C7D67B80744DF.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** The retrievable official record does not preserve complete terms, amounts, or all participants. **Superseded:** No.

### 2021-08-23 — Animoca Brands partnership announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced Animoca Brands as a Star Atlas stakeholder and partner. **Significance:** The partnership linked Star Atlas to a major blockchain-entertainment company during its initial commercial expansion. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, Star Atlas, Animoca Brands. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-601DB73CC99402B0](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-601DB73CC99402B0.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Partnership claim; later asset purchases are recorded separately. **Superseded:** No.

### 2021-10-04 — Yield Guild Games asset commitment announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced that Yield Guild Games had committed to a $1 million purchase of Star Atlas game assets. **Significance:** This was an early institutional guild-economy commitment. **Affected entities:** Yield Guild Games, ATMTA, Star Atlas assets. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-A887A417A8066E37](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-A887A417A8066E37.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Announced commitment; this source alone does not prove settlement or later holdings. **Superseded:** No.

### 2021-11-30 — Sperasoft co-development partnership announced

**Description:** ATMTA and Sperasoft announced an ongoing co-development partnership for the Star Atlas game. **Significance:** The arrangement expanded external production capacity for the UE5 product line. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, Sperasoft, Keywords Studios, UE5 game. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-187A8E889198C5D3](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-187A8E889198C5D3.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Partnership announcement; scope and duration require later evidence. **Superseded:** Unknown.

## 2022

### 2022-01-22 — Animoca Brands asset sale reported above $3 million

**Description:** ATMTA announced completion of an NFT sale to Animoca Brands valued above $3 million. **Significance:** This is separate transaction evidence following the 2021 partnership announcement. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, Animoca Brands, Star Atlas assets. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-8AC8EED8F9A8B82E](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-8AC8EED8F9A8B82E.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Official transaction claim; valuation methodology is not independently audited here. **Superseded:** No.

### 2022-06-08 — iBUYPOWER partnership announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced a partnership with iBUYPOWER covering gamer outreach, COPA sponsorship, custom hardware, and prospective equipment commercialization. **Significance:** The partnership connected the project to conventional gaming hardware and COPA. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, iBUYPOWER, COPA. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-17C770884734231A](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-17C770884734231A.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Announced plans are not treated as completed commercialization. **Superseded:** Unknown.

### 2022-06-10 — MoonPay on-ramp partnership announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced MoonPay as a fiat-to-crypto payments partner for the Star Atlas ecosystem. **Significance:** The arrangement targeted lower-friction onboarding into asset purchases. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, MoonPay, ATLAS, Star Atlas ecosystem. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-F546F327C3C9B727](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-F546F327C3C9B727.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Partnership announcement; implementation and continued availability require separate evidence. **Superseded:** Unknown.

## 2023

### 2023-04-26 — Royalty-free community IP threshold announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced that the first $1 million in revenue from qualifying community uses of Star Atlas intellectual property would be royalty-free. **Significance:** This established a commercial policy for third-party creators and builders. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, Star Atlas IP, community entrepreneurs. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-B19E0D091A8449AB](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-B19E0D091A8449AB.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Policy announcement; current terms must be checked against later builder documentation. **Superseded:** Unknown.

### 2023-07-06 — Hello Moon data partnership announced

**Description:** ATMTA announced a live-data-streaming partnership with Hello Moon using Solana validator and distribution infrastructure. **Significance:** The partnership supported analytics and live game-data access. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, Hello Moon, Solana, Star Atlas data systems. **Sources:** [SRC-OFF-2BC7D317600C6107](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-2BC7D317600C6107.md). **Confidence:** HIGH. **Notes:** Official partnership claim; current operational status is not established. **Superseded:** Unknown.

### 2023-07-26 — Major layoffs and runway reprioritization reported

**Description:** HNN reported major ATMTA layoffs, founder pay cuts, and a reprioritization toward SAGE and a mobile application, while Marketplace, DAO, Faction Fleet, and Faction Claims maintenance moved to lower priority. **Significance:** The report marks a substantial corporate restructuring and product-priority change. **Affected entities:** ATMTA, employees, SAGE, Marketplace, DAO, Faction Fleet. **Sources:** [SRC-HNN-A0BE0ADE1E7A46D5](../../archive/source-records/campaign-charlie-hnn/SRC-HNN-A0BE0ADE1E7A46D5.md). **Confidence:** MEDIUM. **Notes:** Contemporary community reporting attributed to HNN; exact headcount and runway require primary-source recovery. **Superseded:** No.
