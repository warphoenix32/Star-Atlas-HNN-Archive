## Overview

Glowhaven is far more than a luminous frontier. On the surface: a sector where **mineral flora reacts to living electromagnetic fields**, painting ethereal light shows. Beneath that beauty lies a **sacred Mierese site** — the planet their god **Onato** directed them to through shared dreams, where a mysterious artifact was found. The **Iris Academy** has established its research outpost **Anchorage** here to study what may be the key to one of Galia's greatest mysteries.

---

## The Mineral Flora

### Biology (Mineral-Based)

- The "flora" is **mineral formations**, not organic material
- Possesses **innate energy currents**
- Bursts into **radiant illumination** upon interaction with **electromagnetic fields of living entities**
- Creates a landscape that **responds to presence of sentient beings**

### The Light Shows

- At night, horizons come alive in a **mesmerizing dance of shimmering hues**
- Nocturnal creatures going about their night hunts create **trails of living light**
- An extraordinary ecosystem painted in ethereal luminescence

## The Mierese Dream Arc

### Causal Chain (Canon)

> [!NOTE]
> War agitated Iris → cosmic currents intensified → enabled Onato's signal (or what Mierese interpret as Onato's signal)

### Phase 1: The Shared Dreams (~2396)

- **~2396**: Many Mierese begin **dreaming of a beautiful landscape where flora is made of minerals that shine in the dark**
- A world **filled with life and luminance**, filled with dangers and discovery
- The dreams are **shared across the entire species** — a collective vision
- The Convergence War **agitated Iris**, which amplified **cosmic currents** across Galia
- These intensified currents enabled the communication — whether from Onato or another source

### Phase 2: The Debate

- Mierese debate what the **shared dream** could mean
- Some theorize that **Onato is directing his children** to a piece of the puzzle of his imprisonment
- The religious significance electrifies Mierese communities galaxy-wide

> [!WARNING]
> **Ambiguity is intentional**: There is NO certainty it was actually Onato. The Mierese are a species **massively influenced by stories and ideas**. The shared dream could be divine intervention — or collective mytho-psychic resonance. The canon deliberately does not confirm either interpretation.

### Phase 3: The Discovery

- Mierese discover **MRZ-31, Glowhaven**
- Upon exploring the sector, they realize: **this IS the place from their dreams**
- Mierese throughout the **Galia Expanse start flocking** to the sector

### Phase 4: The Intensification (~2513)

- **~2513**: As the Convergence War rages, the **dreams grow more intense**
- The **Lore Keepers reunite** in the sector to understand the visions collectively
- Despite their combined wisdom: **no answer found**

### Phase 5: The Box (~2522)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> A young **female** Mierese explorer finds a **box made of unknown material** in the heart of Glowhaven.

- Upon discovery, the **Mierese dreams finally cease**
- The Lore Keepers declare: this is **part of the quest to free their god Onato**
- **Celebrations erupt** in Mierese settlements throughout the Galaxy
- The box becomes the most important Mierese artifact in living memory
- **The box remains UNOPENED** — no one has discovered how to open it
- The next step of the mystery: finding clues to unlock the box

## The Iris Academy: Anchorage

- The **Iris Academy** creates **Anchorage** — a research outpost in Glowhaven
- Purpose: **decipher the curious artifact site** the Mierese excavated
- Located on one of the planets of the star system
- **Tension exists** between the Iris Academy and the Lore Keepers (science vs. faith)
- However, the Lore Keepers **want help opening the box** and are **willing to compromise**
- A reluctant partnership: sacred custodians + secular researchers working toward the same goal
- What the Academy finds could reshape understanding of Galia itself

## Frontier Activity

- **Numerous ONI settlements** dot the landscape, but vast areas remain **untamed**
- **Explorers**, **hunters**, and **miners** drawn to the luminous wonder
- Mining expeditions driven by curiosity and greed
- The sector is a mix of sacred site, research hub, and wild frontier

## Narrative Hooks

- **The Unopened Box**: The galaxy's greatest locked puzzle — what's inside? How do you open it?
- **The Explorer's Burden**: A young woman who never wanted fame, now immortalized in the Grand Tale
- **Faith vs. Fact**: Did Onato really send the dreams, or is it collective imagination? The canon intentionally never answers
- **War → Iris → Dreams**: The causal chain shows cosmic events have psychic consequences
- **Reluctant Alliance**: Lore Keepers and scientists working together despite philosophical friction
- **Living Landscape**: A world that glows when you walk through it — cinematically stunning
