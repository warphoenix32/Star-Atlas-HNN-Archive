## Preserved Official Text

Fleet Rentals lets you lend or borrow ships in the Star Atlas ecosystem, creating a new way to earn or access gameplay without full ownership.
Built on Solana smart contracts, Fleet Rental is a secure and transparent system where:
Owners can list their ships and earn passive income in ATLAS
Borrowers can rent ships to explore SAGE Labs and engage in gameplay without needing to buy assets upfront
This marketplace lowers the barrier to entry for new players and offers utility for idle fleets, making it a win-win for both sides.
