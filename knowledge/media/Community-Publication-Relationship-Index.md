# Community Publication Relationship Index

This index records institutional and attribution relationships among major Star Atlas community media sources. It does not imply shared ownership unless explicitly stated.

| Entity | Canonical identity | Relationship guidance |
|---|---|---|
| `MEDIA-AEPHIA` | Aephia Industries publications | Guild-affiliated publication linked to `GUILD-AEPHIA`; preserve cited official and community sources |
| `MEDIA-INTERGALACTIC-HERALD` | Intergalactic Herald | May act as original publisher, curator, directory, or secondary summarizer; source lineage is mandatory |
| `MEDIA-HNN` | Hologram News Network | Independent publication founded and operated by `ACTOR-KRIGS`; never attribute to Intergalactic Herald |
| `MEDIA-THE-HOLOGRAM` | The Hologram Medium publication | Historically related to HNN but retained as a distinct publication identity |
| `ACTOR-KRIGS` | Krigs personal publication identity | Personal Medium work remains distinct from HNN and The Hologram |
| `MEDIA-VBTV` | VBTV, formerly Star Atlas TV | Community broadcaster operated by ODVB/VB; may preserve broadcasts it did not originate |
| `MEDIA-ATLAS-BREW` | Atlas Brew | Official Star Atlas team-hosted recurring event; rebroadcasting does not transfer original authorship |

## Attribution rules

- Publisher, broadcaster, host, original creator, and curator are separate roles.
- Reposting or rebroadcasting does not establish original creation.
- A community outlet summarizing an official event is a secondary source unless it adds original reporting.
- A linked primary source should be preserved and preferred for direct factual claims.
- Missing lineage is recorded as unknown, not inferred.

## HNN identity separation

The following are related but distinct archival identities:

1. Hologram News Network / HNN website.
2. Archived `thehologram.io` material.
3. The Hologram Medium publication.
4. Krigs personal Medium publications.

These identities may share ownership or authorship history but should not be collapsed into a single source record.
