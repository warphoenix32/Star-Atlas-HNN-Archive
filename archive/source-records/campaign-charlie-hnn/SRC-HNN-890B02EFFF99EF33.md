Galia Merchants and Star Atlas Golden Ticket Limited Time Redemptions

From now until December 13th, Galia Merchants is offering a guaranteed redemption for Star Atlas themed merchandise up to $30 in value from our store in exchange for Golden Tickets
Press enter or click to view image in full size
Here’s how it works:
1. Join the Galia Merchants Discord here and message Krigs. This is to ensure ease of communication and validity of claim.
Important: No one from the Galia Merchants team will message you first. Galia Merchants team members will NEVER ask for your passwords or seedphrases. Galia Merchants team members will never ask you to connect your wallet to a website for verification or to complete the Golden Ticket redemption process. Beware of scammers initiating direct messages.
2. Send 300 Golden Tickets to the Galia Merchants wallet. For security reasons and to prevent spam, we will provide you with the wallet address only when you initiate a claim with Krigs. DO NOT send tickets until your process has been initiated.
3. Select which single product, up to $30 USD value that you want from Galia Merchants. Ticket redemption package is only valid for a single item
You can view our newest Star Atlas merchandise line here and our full line here.
4. Krigs will complete the checkout process with you individually through direct message.
Rules
1. There is no limit to the number of redemptions a person can claim. Every 300 tickets equates to one single product of $30 USD value or less. On a case by case basis, the team reserves the ability to combine or adjust redemption packages for items of greater value.
2. Due to the unique process of this redemption system, no refunds will be given once a product has been ordered and shipped. Should a product arrive defective, Galia Merchants will provide a replacement at no cost.
Disclaimers
1. Galia Merchants reserves the right to refuse redemption from any claimant for any reason. If tickets have been sent to Galia Merchants and product has not been shipped, Galia Merchants will make every effort to return tickets to the claimant.
2. Galia Merchants reserves the right to end this redemption process early or without notice. Promotion redemption limitations are at the discretion of the Galia Merchants team.
3. Efforts to deceive, disrupt, or deliberately threaten the integrity of the process will be reported to the Star Atlas team.
4. All claimant’s info will be kept private and used for contest purposes only. Claimant info will not be shared, sold, or publicized to anyone outside of the Galia Merchants team and shipping companies. In order to complete the claim, some personal information is required. Divulgence of personal information is completely voluntary, however failure to provide minimum required information will result in the inability to claim prizes.
4. This promotion is not endorsed by the Star Atlas team. While Star Atlas has given Galia Merchants authority to conduct such a redemption, all claimant’s interaction with Galia Merchants is not covered by the Star Atlas team.
5. Due to limited resources by both Galia Merchants and Star Atlas, all transactions and redemptions are conducted through manual verification and coordination. Please exercise patience as this is a limited “test run” to gauge the validity of expanding the Star Atlas ecosystem to physical products.
