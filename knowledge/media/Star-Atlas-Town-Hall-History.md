# Star Atlas Town Hall History

The Star Atlas Town Hall is a recurring official community-discussion format documented by official newsletters from at least May 2021. The repository preserves 14 transcript sources, but they represent a partial and heterogeneous sample rather than a complete numbered series.

## Institutional identity

*Atlas Star Issue #3*, published 2021-05-18, described Friday Town Halls as already traditional and characterized them as guest and community discussions. This establishes the format by May 2021, not its founding date. [SRC-OFF-DD9175EF794C1F8D](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-DD9175EF794C1F8D.md)

Later official records schedule a July 16, 2021 Town Hall, place Town Halls in a three-week 2022 publishing cadence, and retrospectively report 12 Town Hall editions during 2022. The retrospective count is an official summary, not an independently enumerated episode ledger. [July 2021 notice](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-1943EB02EECEDABB.md) [2022 retrospective](../../archive/source-records/campaign-delta-official/SRC-OFF-4A9FCC4E28487231.md)

Aephia’s dated report identifies Community Week Town Hall #42 and names Santi, Michael, and Chris in that event. The transcript source preserves content but does not systematically identify speakers. The page therefore uses the Aephia record for dated event context and the transcript only for unattributed timestamped evidence until diarization is reviewed. ## Evidence rules

- A scheduled Town Hall is not proof the event occurred.
- A recording title is not a publication date.
- A name spoken in a transcript is not a speaker label.
- A numbered label is not proof of a complete sequence.
- Official event origin and later replay publisher must be recorded separately.
- Material roadmap, release, governance, treasury, or personnel claims require claim-level corroboration.

## Missing artifacts

Recover the complete episode inventory, original URLs/video IDs, Discord event notices, recording and upload dates, host/guest rosters, transcripts with speaker diarization, edited-versus-live versions, description metadata, and any corrections. The “Joni awards” item additionally requires an independent results ledger before any winners are promoted.
