## Preserved Article

The award-winning StarAtlas.com website experience brings the blockchain gaming metaverse on Solana a victory in the Awwwards Users’ Choice category.

*StarAtlas.com Website /// Award-winning Digital Experience /// Metaverse Information Hub*

The new site , which launched on August 25, 2021 along with a cinematic teaser trailer , has achieved worldwide acclaim by being proclaimed an Awwwards Site Of The Year.

The StarAtlas.com website began its victorious journey by first achieving the title of the Website of the Day , then proceeded by being selected as the Website of the Month , and was subsequently declared a nominee for the annual award among many other great works.

The stars aligned for Star Atlas and Hello Monday , a US-Danish digital studio, in creating an award-winning immersive website experience that conveys the depth and quality players can look forward to in the triple-A title.

> On behalf of the team creating our blockchain gaming metaverse, I’d like to thank our community for sharing the vision of an immersive open world the size of a galaxy and building the future with us. I also want to thank the creative team at Hello Monday for helping us manifest and condense this intergalactic vision in the form of a website on StarAtlas.com. It’s a great honor to receive this award and be recognized as a driving force in inspiring people around the world. -Pablo Quiroga, CRO and Co-founder of Star Atlas

The StarAtlas.com website features the immersive experience, a showroom , and a repository of fresh information about the making of a metaverse as well the team behind it.

> The seamless immersive experience Hello Monday has put together to allow us to dive into the world of Star Atlas is incredible. Using simple mechanics, the website adds a consistent depth to the game, enlarging its universe and giving it a voice. While also displaying layouts and interactive visuals that are real eye candy. — Bastien Allard , Awwwards Jury member

## ABOUT STAR ATLAS

Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state of the art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video game, and decentralized financial technologies.

Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. Blockchain technology using the Solana protocol established a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas creates an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership.
