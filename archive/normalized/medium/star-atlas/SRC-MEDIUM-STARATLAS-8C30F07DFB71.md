## The Largest Lootfest in Star Atlas History

The Golden Carnival is a celebration of everyone in Galia, bringing prosperity to every corner of the galaxy. For 16 weeks, win big prizes by submitting Golden Tickets into weekly loot drops with unique Star Atlas loot totaling $4,000,000!

Here’s a simple breakdown of how to get involved and bring home your slice of the $4,000,000 pie:

1. Craft Golden Tickets in Star Atlas: Golden Era OR get them directly from players on the Galactic Marketplace .

2. Submit your tickets into a weekly loot drop on Hedgehog (Opens early next week)

3. Join our Discord every Friday at 10AM PT / 1PM ET to see if you win!

This gives players options in how they experience the carnival. If you’re more of a ‘supply the shovels during the gold rush’ type, you can craft Golden Tickets in SAGE Labs and supply them to other players.

The in-game recipe is straightforward. All you’re going to need is:

• Titanium: 230 units required

• Toolkits: 137 units required

• Framework: 103 units required

• Electronics: 82 units required

• Power Source: 69 units required

• Electromagnet: 51 units required

• Field Stabilizer: 34 units required

• Radiation Absorber: 26 units required

• Particle Accelerator: 21 units required

And done! All you have to do is set your crew to work on crafting the tickets. Or, if you’d rather just simply join the loot drop, you can get your tickets from players who’ve put in the hard work to craft them. For more details on getting involved, keep reading!


## The 16-Week Loot Roadmap

Each week, you’ll face a new challenge — which loot drop to participate in and how many tickets to commit during a particular loot drop . Players have full visibility on how many tickets have already been submitted for each week’s loot drop, meaning you’ll have the chance to adjust your strategy up to five minutes before each one .

The Wandering Caravan , true to its unpredictable nature, has organized 16 weeks of drops:

- 12 Regular Weeks , each featuring a manufacturer sponsor offering $200,000 in prizes. If you have a preferred manufacturer aesthetic or utility style, choose a set of ships that match your tastes.
- 3 Mega Weeks , where the stakes rise significantly, offering $400,000 in potential loot, doubling a regular week’s loot pool.
- Mystery Week , the unpredictable grand finale, where you’ll have to trust the cosmic currents for the ultimate reward.

## Acquiring Golden Tickets

At the heart of the Golden Carnival are Golden Tickets . Each week, you’ll have a chance to carefully consider when and where to deploy your Golden Tickets to maximize your chances of winning. In essence, the Golden Carnival itself is a minigame of strategy , enabling you to test your wits, planning, and timing. There are 2 ways to get your own Golden Tickets:

- Craft them in-game : Players with even modest fleets can work hard to craft tickets in based.staratlas.com , a browser-based testbed for our evolving economy. Use your resources wisely and churn out tickets to increase your chances in the loot drops, or sell them to other players through our Galactic Marketplace.
- Simply get them on the marketplace from other players : If crafting isn’t your thing, you can easily head to our galactic marketplace to get Golden Tickets from other players who put in the hard work to craft them.

## Joining a Weekly Loot Drop

So you’ve seen the Loot Roadmap, know what weeks you want to participate in and have your Golden Tickets. Now what? Early next week, head to our partner Hedgehog’s website to submit your ticket(s).

Let the Golden Carnival begin, and may the cosmic currents favor you! Submit your tickets to prosperity early next week on Hedgehog.

## Frequently Asked Questions

- How can I craft the tickets? You can craft them in SAGE Labs at https://based.staratlas.com/ by completing in-game recipes.
- Where do I submit my Golden Tickets? You can submit your tickets at Hedgehog .
- Can I submit more than 1 ticket per week? Yes, you can submit as many golden tickets as you want.
- Once I craft a ticket, have I entered the loot drop? No, you must enter the tickets into the weekly loot drop itself on Hedgehog .
- Do I have to enter my tickets in the week I craft them? No, you can decide to enter your tickets at any time during the duration of the promotion.
- Are tickets reusable? No, tickets are considered consumed once used in a weekly loot drop and new ones need to be obtained to participate in the following weeks.
- How will I claim the loot? Loot will be claimable on Hedgehog Markets with your associated game wallet.
- Can I win loot more than once? Yes, but not with a ticket that has already won loot. Once a golden ticket wins loot, it will not be able to win subsequent loot.
- What happens with a winning ticket? A winning ticket is instantly burned. You can win more loot with other tickets you’ve submitted.
- In what order will loot be drawn? The loot will be drawn from least valuable to most valuable.
- What happens to golden tickets at the end of the Golden Carnival? At the end of the Golden Carnival, the tickets will no longer be usable.
- Will tickets have additional costs? No, tickets will not cost any extra fees.
