## Preserved Official Text

The DAC Platform is Star Atlas’s official on-chain system for managing player-created organizations known as Decentralized Autonomous Corporations (DACs). It serves as the foundation for collaborative play, enabling individuals to formally unite under shared goals and values.
While gameplay integration is still in development, the platform already supports the creation, branding, and discovery of DACs, helping players organize today, and prepare for deeper social and strategic systems coming in the future.
What Does the DAC Platform Do?
The DAC Platform at play.staratlas.com/dac allows players to:
Register a DAC with a unique name and visual identity
Write a public description and outline their mission or values
Create custom tags for filtering and discoverability
Generate a join link to recruit new members
Explore other DACs based on faction, focus, or philosophy
Each DAC is stored on-chain, meaning its data is secure, permanent, and transparently managed within the Star Atlas ecosystem.
Key Features
Name, Avatar & CoverRepresent your DAC with a visual identity and a name that reflects your vision in Galia.
Public DescriptionShare your story, gameplay focus, and expectations with potential recruits.
Custom TagsAdd tags like “Mining,” “Freight,” “Combat,” or “Ustur” to help players find organizations that match their interests and values.
Join URLInstantly recruit members with a personalized DAC invite link.
Public DiscoveryBrowse the full DAC registry, review their tags, and apply to join the one that fits you best.
⚠️ Note: The current version of the DAC Platform is not yet tied to in-game utility. However, it lays the groundwork for major gameplay systems to come.
What’s Coming Next?
Future DAC Platform updates will unlock powerful features like:
Treasury and financial tools
Member roles and advanced permissions
Shared asset and resource management
On-chain proposal voting systems
In-game integrations with SAGE and UE5
These updates will position DACs as fully functioning guilds with the tools to strategize, build, govern, and thrive together in the Star Atlas metaverse.
